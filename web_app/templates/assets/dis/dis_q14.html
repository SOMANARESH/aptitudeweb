Question :14

In covering a certain distance, the speeds of A and B are in the ratio of 3:4. A taken 30 minutes more than B to reach the destination. The time taken by A to reach the destination is:

A.  1 hour

B.  1 hour 30 minutes

C.  2 hours

D.  2 hours 30 minutes


Ratio of speeds = 3:4

Ratio of time = 4:3

Difference of time = (4-3)ratio

1 ratio = 30 minutes

Time taken by A = 4 ratio

= 4×30 minutes

= 120 minutes

= 2 hours.
