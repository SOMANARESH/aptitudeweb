Question :68

A train 150 long running at the speed of 36 km/h takes 30 seconds to cross a platform completely. Find the length of the platoform.

A.  300 meter

B.  250 meter

C.  150 meter

D.  100 meter


Speed of train = 5⁄18×36

= 10 m/s

Distance covered in 30 seconds = 30×10

=300 meters

Length of platform = 300-150

=150 meters.
