Question :85

A theft is reported to policeman. The thief starts running and the policeman chases him, when the policeman starts chasing, the thief was at a distance of 150 meter. The thief and the policeman run at the speed of 9 km/h and 10 km/h respectively. How far the policeman had to run to catch the thief?

A.  1000 m

B.  1250 m

C.  1500 m

D.  None of these

Answer: C

Ratio of speeds = 9:10

Police man gains = (10-9)km

= 1 km for every 10 km covered by him

Required distance = 10×150m

=1500 m.
