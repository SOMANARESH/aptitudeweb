A train 220 meter long running at 90 km/h crosses a 180 meter train coming from the opposite direction in 9 seconds. The speed of the second train is:

A.  50 km/h

B.  70 km/h

C.  100 km/h

D.  160 km/h

Answer: b

Distance covered = (220+180) meters

= 400 meters

Relative speed of two trains= 400÷9 m/s

= (400÷9)×(18×5) km/h

= 160 km/h

Speed of the second train = (160-90) km/h

= 70 km/h.
