Pratibha covers a distance of 24 kms at the speed of 8 km/hr and a distance of 18 kms at the speed of 9 km/hr. further she covers a distance of 12 km at the speed of 3 km/hr. what is her average speed in covering the whole distance?

A.  8 kmph

B.  5.5 kmph

C.  3 kmph

D.  6 kmph

Answer:d

Total distance covered = 24+18+12

=54 km

= totaltime taken 24÷8+18÷9+12÷3

=3+2+4= 9 hours

Average speed = 54÷9

=6 kmph.
