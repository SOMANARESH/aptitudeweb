Question :54

A train moving at a rate of 36 km/hr crosses a standing man in 10 seconds. It will cross a platform 55 meters long, in:

A.  5 1⁄2 seconds

B.  6 seconds

C.  7 seconds

D.  15 1⁄2 seconds


Speed of train = 36 km/hr

= 36×5÷18

=10 m/s

Time taken to cross platform = 10 seconds + (55÷10)seconds

= 15 1⁄2 seconds

Trick: time should be more than time taken to cross standing man.
