Question :43

If a man reduces his speed to 2/3, he takes 1 hour more in walking a certain distance. The time (in hours) to cover the distance with his normal speed is:

A.  2

B.  1

C.  3

D.  1.5


Walking at 2/3 of speed, he will take 3⁄2 of the normal time.

3⁄2 of usual time = usual time + 1 hour

1⁄2 usual time = 1 hour

Usual time = 2×1 hour

= 2 hours.
