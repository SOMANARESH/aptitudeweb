Question :34

A motor starts with the speed of 70 kmph with its speed increased every two hours by 10 kmph. In how many hours will it cover 345 kms?

A.  2 1⁄4 hours

B.  4 1⁄2 hours

C.  4 hours 5 minutes

D.  None of these


speed of motor in the 1,2,3,4,5,6th hours

= 70,70,80,80,90,90 kmph respectively

Distance covered in first four hours

= 70+70+80+80=300 km

Remaining distance = 345 − 300 = 45 km

Time taken to cover 45 km at 90 kmph = 45÷90

= 1⁄2 hour

Total time taken = 4+1⁄2= 4 1⁄2 hour.
