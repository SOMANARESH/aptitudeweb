Question :51

A train crosses 80 meter long platform in 30 seconds. If the speed of the train is 4 m/s. what is the length of the train?

A.  40 meters

B.  55 meters

C.  80 meters

D.  60 meters


Distance covered in 30 seconds = 4×30

= 120 m

Length of train + platform = 120 m

Length of train + 80 m = 120m

Length of train = 120-80

= 40 m.
