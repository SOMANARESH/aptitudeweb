Question :81

A train of length 150 m takes 10s to cross another train 100 m long coming from the opposite direction. If the speed of first train is 30 kmph, what is the speed of second train?

A.  72 kmph

B.  60 kmph

C.  54 kmph

D.  48 kmph

Answer: B

Total distance covered = (150+100)meters

=250 meters

Relative speed of two trains = 250⁄10× 18⁄5

=90 kmph

Speed of the second train = 90-30

= 60 kmph.
