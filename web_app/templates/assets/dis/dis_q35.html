Question :35

A man takes 6 hours 35 minutes in walking to a certain place and riding back. He would have taken 2 hours less by riding both ways. What time would he take to walk both ways?

A.  4 hours 35 minutes

B.  8 hours 35 minutes

C.  10 hours

D.  8 hours 25 minutes

Answer: b

One way walking + one way riding = 6 hours 35 minutes

Two ways walking time

= 6 hours 35 minutes + 2 hours

= 8 hours 35 minutes.
