Train a took 35 minutes to cover a distance of 50 km. If the speed of train B is 25% faster than train A, it will cover the same distance in?

A.  25 mins

B.  28 mins

C.  30 mins

D.  36 mins


Let sped of train A = 100 km/h.

Then speed of train B = 125 km/h

Ratio of speeds of trains A and B = 100:125= 4:5

Ratio of speeds of trains A and B = 10:125=4:5

Ratio of time taken by them to cover equal distance = 5:4

Time taken by train A = 35 minutes

Time taken by train B = 4⁄5×35 minutes

=28 minutes.
