Question :9

A car covers its journey at the speed of 80 km/hr in 10 hours. If the same distance is to be covered in 4 hours, by how much the speed of the car will have to increase?

A.  80 km/hr

B.  100 km/hr

C.  120km/hr

D.  160 km/hr

Answer: C

Total distance covered = 80×10

= 800km

New speed = 800÷4

=200 km/hr.

Increase in speed = (200-80) kmph

= 120 km⁄hr.
