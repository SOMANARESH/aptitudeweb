Question :55

A 120 meter long train is running at a speed of 90 km per hour. It will cross a railway platform 230 m long in:

A.  4 4/5 seconds

B.  9 1/5 seconds

C.  7 seconds

D.  14 seconds


Total distance = 120+230

=350 meters

Speed of train = 90 km/hr

= 90×5⁄18

=25m/s

Required time = 350÷25

=14 seconds.
