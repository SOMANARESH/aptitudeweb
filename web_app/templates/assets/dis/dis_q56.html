Question :56

A train moves past a telegraph post and a bridge 264 m log in 8 seconds and 20 seconds respectively. What is the speed of the train?

A.  69.5 km/h

B.  70 km/h

C.  79 km/h

D.  79.2 km/h


Time take to cover 264 m = (20-8) seconds

= 12 seconds

Speed of train = 264÷12

=22 m/s

= 22×5⁄18 km/hr

= 79.2 km/hr.
