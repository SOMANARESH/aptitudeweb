Question :71

Two trains of lengths, 100 m and 150 m are travelling in opposite directions at speeds of 75kmph and 50 kmph respectively. What is the time taken by them to cross each other?

A.  7.4 s

B.  7.2 s

C.  7 s

D.  6.8 s

Answer: b

Relative speed = (74+50) kmph

=125+5⁄18 m/s

Total distance = (100+150)m

= 250 m

Required time = 250×1⁄125×18⁄5

=7.2s.
