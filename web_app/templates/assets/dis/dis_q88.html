Question :88

A and B can walk around a circular path in 4 hours and 6 hours respectively. If they start from the same point in the same direction, when will they be together at the starting point again?

A.  2 hours

B.  6 hours

C.  8 hours

D.  12 hours

Answer: d

LMC of time taken by A and B = LCM of 4 and 6

= 12 hours

They will be together again at the starting point after 12 hours.
