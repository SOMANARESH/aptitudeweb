Question :21

A car can cover a certain distance in 4 1⁄2 hours. If the speed is increased by 5 km/hr, it would take 1⁄2 hour less to cover the same distance. Find the slower speed of the car.

A.  50 km/h

B.  40 km/h

C.  45 km/h

D.  60 km/h

Answer: b

Ratio of time taken = 4 1⁄2: (4 1⁄2 -1⁄2)

= 9⁄2:4

= 9:8

Ratio of speeds = 8:9

Difference of speeds = (9-8) ratio

= 1 ratio

= 5 km/hr

Slower sped = 8 ratio

= 8×5 km/hr

= 40 km/hr.
