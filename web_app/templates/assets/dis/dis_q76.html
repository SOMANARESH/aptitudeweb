Question :76

Two trains are running in the same direction at 74 km/h and 38 km/h. if the faster train crosses a man sitting in the slower train in15 seconds, find the length of the faster train.

A.  100 meter

B.  125 meter

C.  150 meter

D.  165 meter


Relative speeds of the two trains = (74-38) km/h

=36 k/h

Distance covered in 15 seconds

=36×5⁄18×15=150 m

Length of the faster train = 150 meter.
