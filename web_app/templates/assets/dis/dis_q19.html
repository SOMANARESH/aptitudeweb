Question :19

A man completed a certain journey by a car. If he covered 30% of the distance at the speed of 20 kmph. 60% of the distance at the 40 kmph, and the remaining distance at 10 kmph, his average speed for the whole journey was:

A.  25 kmph

B.  28 kmph

C.  30 kmph

D.  33 kmph


Let total distance = 100 km

Then total time = 30⁄20+ 60⁄40+ 10⁄10

=1.5+1.5+1= 4 hours

Average speed = 100÷4

= 25 km/hr.
