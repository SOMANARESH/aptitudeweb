Question :72

Two trains 140 m and 160 m long run at the speeds of 60 km/h and 40 km/h respectively in opposite directions on parallel tracks. The time (in seconds) which they take to cross each other is:

A.  10

B.  10.8

C.  9

D.  6

Answer:b

total distance = 140+160

=300 m

relative speed = (60+40) km/hr

= 100 km/hr

= 100×5⁄18 m/s

required time = 300÷(100÷5⁄18)

=(300×18)÷(100×5)

=10.8 seconds.
