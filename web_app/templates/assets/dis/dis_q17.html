Question :17

A person travelled by train for 1 hour at a speed of 50 kmph. He then travelled by a taxi for 30 minutes at a speed of 32 kmph to complete his journey. What is the average speed at which he travelled during the journey?

A.  44 kmph

B.  42 kmph

C.  41 kmph

D.  33 kmph

Answer: a

Total distance covered = 1×50 + 1⁄2×32

= 50+16

= 66 km

Total time taken = 1 hour 30 minutes

=3⁄2 hour

Average speed during the journey = 66÷ 3⁄2

= 66 × 3⁄2

= 44 km.
