Question :45

Walking 4⁄5 of his usual speed, a man reaches is late by 20 minutes in reaching his destination. His usual time to reach his destination is:

A.  2 1⁄2 hours

B.  2 hours

C.  1 1⁄2 hours

D.  1 1⁄3 hours


Time taken at 4⁄5 of usual speed = - of usual time

5⁄4 of the usual time = usual time + 1⁄3 hours

1⁄4 of the usual time = 1⁄3 hours.

usual time = 1⁄3 × 4⁄1

= 1 1⁄3.
