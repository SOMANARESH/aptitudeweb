Question :22

A man can reach a certain place in 30 hours. If he reduces his speed by 1⁄15 th, he goes 10 km less in that time. Find his speed (in km/hour)

A.  6

B.  5 1⁄2

C.  4

D.  5


Ratio of speeds = 15:14

Difference in distance covered = (15-14)ratio

= 1 ratio

=10 km

original distance = 15 ratio

= 15×10 km

= 150 km

Original speed = 150÷30

= 5 km/hr.
