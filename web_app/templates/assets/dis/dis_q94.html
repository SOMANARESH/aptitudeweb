Question :94

A and B can walk around a circular path in 5 minutes and 8 minutes respectively. If they start from the same point in the same direction, after how much time will they meet again for the first time?

A.  13 minutes

B.  12 minutes

C.  40 minutes

D.  13 minutes 20 seconds


Let length of the circular path = LCM of 5 and 8

= 40 meters

Then speeds of A and B are 8 and 5 meters/minute

They will meet for the first time when A(i.e. the faster one) has gained one round i.e. 40 meters over B.

Time taken by A = 40÷(8-5)

= 13 1⁄3 minutes

= 13 minutes 20 seconds.
