Question :64

A 300 meter long train crosses a platform in 42 seconds while it crosses a pole in 18 seconds. What is the length of the platform?

A.  400 meters

B.  200 meters

C.  300 meters

D.  None of these


Time taken to cover distance equal to length of platform = 42-18

=24 seconds

Time taken to cover distance equal to length (300 m) of train

= 18 seconds

Length of the platform = (300÷18)×24

=400 m.
