Question :16

A car covers the first 35 km of its journey in 45 minutes and covers the remaining 69 km in 75 minutes. What is the average speed of the car?

A.  42 km/hr

B.  50 km/hr

C.  52 km/hr

D.  60 km/hr

Answer: c

Total distance covered = 35+69

= 104 km

Total time = 45+75

= 120 minutes = 2 hours

Average speed = 104÷2

= 52 km/hr.
