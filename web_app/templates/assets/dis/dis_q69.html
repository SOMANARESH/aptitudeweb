Question :69

A train crosses a pole in 10 seconds and a platform 150 meter long in 16 seconds. The speed of the train in km/h is:

A.  60

B.  90

C.  120

D.  180


Train crosses distance equal to its length in 10 seconds

And distance equal to sum of length of train and length of platform in 16 seconds.

Train covers 150 meters i.e. length of platform in 6 seconds

Speed of the train = 150÷6 m/s

= (150÷6)×18÷5 km/h

= 90 km/hr.
