Question :24

A 240 metres long train crosses a platform twice its length in 40 seconds. What is the speed of the train?

A.  6 m/s

B.  28 m/s

C.  18 m/s

D.  16 m/s


Length of platform = 2×240 = 480m

Total distance covered = 240+480

= 720 m.

Speed of the train = 720÷40 = 18 m/s.
