Question :20

A car covers four successive 6 km stretches at speeds of 25 kmph, 50 kmph, 75 kmph and 150 mph respectively. Its average speed over his distance.

A.  25 kmph

B.  50 kmph

C.  75 kmph

D.  150 kmph

Answer:b

Let he covers four successive stretches of 150 km each

Then total time taken = 150⁄25+ 150⁄50+ 150⁄75+ 150⁄150

=6+3+2+1

=12 hours

Average speed for whole journey = (4×150)÷12

=50 kmph.
