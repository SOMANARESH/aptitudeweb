Question :23

A car travelling with 5/7 of its usual speed covers 42 km in 1 hour 40 mins 48 seconds. What is the usual speed of the car?

A.  17 6⁄7 km/hr

B.  35 km/hr

C.  25 km/hr

D.  30 km/hr


40 minutes = 40×1⁄60hour

= 2⁄3hours

48 seconds = 48×1⁄60×1⁄60

= 1⁄75hour

Total time = 1+2⁄3+1⁄75

= 126⁄75

=42⁄25hours.

Sped of car = 42÷42⁄25

= 42×25⁄42

= 25 km/hr.

Usual speed of the car = 7⁄5×25 km/hr

= 35 km/hr.
