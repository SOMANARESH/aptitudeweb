Question :46

Walking at 5⁄4th of the usual speed, a person reaches his office 12 minutes too early. What is the usual time?

A.  48 minutes

B.  60 minutes

C.  70 minutes

D.  80 minutes

Answer:b

Walking at 5⁄4th his usual speed, he will take 4⁄5th of usual time

Usual time = 4⁄5th of usual time + 12 minutes

1⁄5thusual time = 12 minutes.

Usual time = 5×12 minutes = 60 minutes.
