Nitin rides a bicycle at the speed of 15 km/mhr. But stops for 10 minutes to take rest every 20 km. how much time will he take to cover a distance of 150 kms?

A.  12 hours 30 mins

B.  11 hours 10 mins

C.  10 hours 20 mins

D.  12 hours 10 mins

Answer:b

Time taken to cover 150 km @ 15 km/hr = 10 hours

He will take rest 150÷20

=7 times

Total rest time = 10×7

=70 minutes

= 1 hours 10 minutes

Total time taken = 10 hours + 1 hor 10 minutes

= 11 hours, 10 minutes.
