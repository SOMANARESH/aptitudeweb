Question :92

From a point on a circular track 5 km long A, B and C started running in the same direction at the same time with speeds of 2 1⁄2 km per hour, 3 km per hour and 2 km per hour respectively. Then on the starting point all three will meet again after:

A.  30 hours

B.  6 hours

C.  10 hours

D.  15 hours


Time taken by A, B and C to complete one round

=5⁄(21⁄2), 5⁄3, 5⁄2 =2,5⁄3, 5⁄2 hours

Then will be at starting point again after LCM of 2, 5⁄3, 5⁄2

=10 hours.
