Question :62

A train, 150 m long, passes a pole in 15 seconds and another train of the same length travelling in the opposite direction in 12 seconds. The speed of the second train is:

A.  45 km/h

B.  48 km/h

C.  52 km/h

D.  54 km/h

Answer:d

Speed of the first train = 150÷15

=10 m/s

Relative speed of two trains = (150+150)÷12

= 25 m/s

Speed of the second train = 25-10

=15 m/s

=15×18÷5

=54 km/hr.
