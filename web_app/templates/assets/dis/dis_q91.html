Question :91

A, B and C walk round a circle of 900 meters in circumference at the rate of 60m, 50 and 45 m per minute respectively. If all of them start together in the same direction after how long will they all meet for the first time:

A.  60 min

B.  90 min

C.  120 min

D.  180 min

Answer: d

A gains 10 m over B in 1 minute

A gain 15 m over C in 1 minute

A will gain one round over B in 90 min

A will gain one round over C in 60 min

All of them be together again after 180 minutes (i.e. LCM of 90 & 60).
