Question :30

Two persons start walking from a place in opposite directions at 5 km/h and 4 km/h respectively. After how many hours will they be 45 km apart?

A.  5 hours

B.  7 hours

C.  9 hours

D.  10 hours


Relative speed of two persons = 5+4

= 9 km/h

Required time = 45÷9

= 5 hours.
