Question :50

A train running at the speed of 25 m/s takes 10 seconds to pass a man running at the speed of 10 m/s in the opposite direction. Find the length of the train.

A.  150 meter

B.  200 meter

C.  250 meter

D.  350 meter


Relative speed = 25+10 = 35 m/s.

Distance covered in 10 seconds = 35×10

= 350 meters

Length of the train = 350 meters.
