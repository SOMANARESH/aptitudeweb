Question :80

Two trains of length of 250 and 200 meter are running in the same direction. If the trains cross each other in 45 seconds and the speed of the first train is 27 km/h, find the speed of the second train.

A.  36 km/h

B.  54 kmph

C.  63 km/h

D.  72 km/h

Answer: c

Total distance covered = (250+200) meters

= 450 meters

Relative speed of two trains = (450÷45)×(18÷5)km/h

= 36 km/h

Speed of the second train = (27+36)km/h

= 63 km/h.
