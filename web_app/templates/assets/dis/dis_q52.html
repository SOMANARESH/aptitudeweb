Question :52

A train 100 meters long takes 5 seconds to pass a standing man. Find the time taken by the train in crossing a railway platform of 260 meter in length.

A.  18 seconds

B.  13 seconds

C.  26 seconds

D.  36 seconds

Answer: a

Speed of the train = 100÷5

=20 m/s

Time taken by the train to cross the platform = (260+100)÷20

= 18 seconds.
