Question :13

The ratio of speeds of A and B is 4:5. If difference between time taken by two in covering a certain distance is 20 minutes, find the time taken by A.

A.  4 minutes

B.  5 minutes

C.  70 minutes

D.  100 minutes

Answer:d

Ratio of speeds = 4:5

Ratio of time taken = 5:4

Difference of time = (5-4)ratio

= 1 ratio

= 20 minutes

Time taken by A = 5 ratio

= 100 minutes.
