Question :12

Points A and B are 90 km apart from each other on a highway. A car starts from A and another from B at the same time. If they go in the same direction, they meet after 9 hours and if they go in opposite directions, they meet after 9/7 hours. The speeds (in km/hr) of A and B are:

A.  25,45

B.  20,50

C.  30,40

D.  35,65


Relative speed (when in same direction )

= 90÷9

=10 km/hr

Relative speed (when going in opposite direction)

= 90 ÷ 9⁄7

=70 kmph

Their speeds are (70-10)÷2 and (70+10)÷2

=30 and 40 km/hr.
