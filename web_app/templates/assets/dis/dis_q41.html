Question :41

If a train, with a speed of 60 kmph, crosses a pole in 30 seconds, the lengh of the train (in meters) is:

A.  1000

B.  900

C.  750

D.  500


Speed of the train = 60 km/hr

= 60×5⁄18 m/s

Length of train = distance covered in 30 seconds

= 60×5⁄18×30 m

= 500 meters.
