Question :70

A train 150 meters long running at an average speed of 52 km/h crosses a man walking in the same direction at an average speed of 7 km/h in:

A.  12 seconds

B.  13 seconds

C.  15 seconds

D.  None of these


Relative speed = (52-7) km/h

= 45 km/h

= 4×5⁄18 m/s = 25÷2 m/s

Time taken to cover 150 meter= 150÷25⁄2

=(150×2)÷25

=12 seconds.
