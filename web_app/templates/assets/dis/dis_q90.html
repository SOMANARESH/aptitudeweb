Question :90

A and B walk around a circular path of 5 km in circumference, starting together from the same point in the same direction. If their speeds are 4 km/h and 3 km/h respectively, after how many hours will they be again at the starting point?

A.  3 hours

B.  4 hours

C.  5 hours

D.  6 hours

Answer: c

They will be together at the starting point when A (the faster one) has gained 5 km (i.e. one round ) over B.

in one hour A gains (4km - 3 km) i.e 1 km over B.

A gains 5 km in 5 hours.
