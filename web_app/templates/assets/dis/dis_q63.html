Question :63

A person started his journey in the morning at 11 a.m, he covered 3/8 of the journey and on the same day at 4.30 pm, he covered 5/6 of the journey. He started his journey at:

A.  6 a.m

B.  5.30 a.m

C.  7 a.m

D.  6.30 a.m


Journey covered between 11 a.m. and 4:30 p.m.

=(5⁄6) − (3⁄8) =11⁄24

Time taken = 4.30 p.m. − 11 a.m. = 51⁄2 hours

= 11⁄2 hours

11⁄24 of journey covered in 11⁄2 hours

3⁄8 of journey covered 11⁄2 × 24⁄11 × 3⁄8 = 9⁄2 hours

=41⁄2 hours

Journey was started at 11 a.m. − 4 1⁄2 hours

= 6.30 a.m.
