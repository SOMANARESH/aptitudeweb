Question :44

Walking at three fourth of his usual speed, a man covers a certain distance in 2 hours more than the time he takes to cover the distance with his normal speed. The time taken by him to cover the same distance with his usual speed is:

A.  4⁄5hours

B.  5.5 hours

C.  6 hours

D.  5 hours


Walking at 3⁄4 th of his usual sped, he will take 4⁄3 of usual time

4⁄3 of the usual time

= usual time + 2 hours

1⁄3of the usual time = 2 hours

Usual time = 3×2 hours

= 6 hours.
