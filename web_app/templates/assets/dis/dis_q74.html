Question :74

Two trains one 160 m and the other 140 m long are running in opposite directions on parallel rails, the first at 7 km an hour and the other at 67 km an hour. How long (in seconds) will they make to cross each other?

A.  7

B.  7 1⁄2

C.  6

D.  10

Answer:b

Total distance = (160+140)m

= 300 m

Relative speed = (77+67)km/hr

= 144 km/hr

=144×5÷18 m/s

= 40 m/s

Required time = 300÷40

= 7 1⁄2 seconds.
