A person can walk a certain distance and drive back in six hours. He can also walk both ways in 10 hours. How much time will he take to drive both ways?

A.  Two hours

B.  Two and half hours

C.  Five and a half hours

D.  Four hours.

Answer: a

One way walking+ one wy driving = 6 hours

2×(one way walking + one way driving)= 12 hours

Two ways walking time = 10 hours

Two ways driving time = 12 hours − 10 hours

= 2 hours.
