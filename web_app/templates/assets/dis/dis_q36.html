Question :36

I walk a certain distance and ride back taking a total time of 37 minutes. I could walk both ways in 55 minutes. How long would it take me to ride both ways?

A.  9.5 minutes

B.  19 minutes

C.  18 minutes

D.  20 minutes


One way walking + one way riding = 37 minutes

2× (one way walking + one way riding )

= 74 minutes

Two way walking time = 55 minutes

Two ways riding time = 74 minutes − 55 minutes

= 19 minutes.
