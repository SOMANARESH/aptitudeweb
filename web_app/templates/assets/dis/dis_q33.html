Question :33

A and B, standing at places having distance 12 km, start walking towards each other and meet in 1 hour 15 minutes. If the speed of a is 4 km/hr. then the speed of B is:

A.  3 3⁄5 km/hr

B.  4 3⁄5 km/hr

C.  5 3⁄5 km/hr

D.  6 3⁄5 km/hr


Time taken = 1 hour 15 minutes = 1 1⁄4 hour

= 5⁄4hour

Their relative speed = 12÷ 5⁄4

= 12×4⁄5

= 48÷5 km

Speed of B = (48⁄5)-4

= 28÷5

= 5 3⁄5 km/hr.
