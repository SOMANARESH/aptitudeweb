Question :82

Two trains of the length of 200 meter and 250 meter are running in the opposite directions at the speed of 35 kmph and 55 km/h respectively. Find the time in which the faster train will pass a man sitting in the slower train.

A.  2 seconds

B.  8 seconds

C.  10 seconds

D.  18 seconds

Answer: c

Relative speed = (35+55) km/h

= 90 km/h =(5÷18)×90 m/s

= 25 m/s

The faster train will completely cross a man sitting in the slower train when a distance equal to length of the faster train is covered

Distance to be covered = 250 meter

Required time = 250÷25

= 10 seconds.
