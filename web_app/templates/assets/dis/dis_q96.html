Question :96

A man walking at a speed of 10 km/h. after every km, he takes rest for 4 minutes. How much time will he take to cover a distance of 10 km?

A.  1 hour

B.  90 minutes

C.  96 minutes

D.  100 minutes

Answer: c

He covers 10 km in one hour (i.e. in 60 minutes) and rests for 4 minutes after every km.

He will take rest 9 times.

Total time taken = 60+9×4

=96 minutes.
