Question :49

A train running at the speed of 15 m/s takes 20 seconds to pass a man running at the speed of 5 m/s in the same direction. Find the length of the train.

A.  50 meter

B.  100 meter

C.  200 meter

D.  250 meter


Relative speed = 15-5 = 10 m/s

Distance covered in 20 seconds = 10×20

= 200 meters

Length of the train = 200 meters.
