Question :77

Two trains running in the opposite directions at the speeds of 44 kmph and 55 kmph take 12 seconds to cross each other. If the length of one train is 150 meter, what is the length of the other train?

A.  120 meter

B.  180 meter

C.  220 meter

D.  250 meter


Their relative speed = (44+55)km/hr

=99 km/h

=(5÷18)×99 m/s

=55÷2 m/s

Distance covered in 12 seconds = 12×55÷2

=330 meter

Length of the second train = 330-150

=180 meter.
