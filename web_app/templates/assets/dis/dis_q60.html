A train crosses a pole and a bridge 300 meters long in 10 seconds and 25 seconds respectively. How long will the train take to cross a platform 200 meters long?

A.  10 seconds

B.  18 seconds

C.  20 seconds

D.  25 seconds

Answer:c

Time taken to cover 300 meters = 25-10

=15 seconds

Speed of the train = 300÷15

=20 m/s

Length of train = 10×20m

=200 meters

Time taken by to cross a platform of 200 meters

= (200+200)÷20

=20 seconds.
