A car starts from A to B and another car starts form B to A. they meet at the exact middle of the places A and B. after meeting, they complete their journeys in 2 hours and 1 hour 20 minutes respectively. The ratio of the time taken by them to complete the whole journey is:

A.  3:2

B.  16:24

C.  13:16

D.  16:13

Answer:a

Time taken by two cars to cover equal distances

= 2 hours and 1 hours 20 minutes

= 120 minutes and 80 minutes respectively

Required ratio = 120:80

= 3:2.
