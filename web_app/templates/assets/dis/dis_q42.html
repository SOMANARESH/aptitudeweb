Question :42

A train running at 7/11 of its own speed reached a place in 22 hours. How much time could be saved if the train would have run at its normal speed:

A.  14 hours

B.  7 hours

C.  8 hours

D.  16 hours

Answer: c

Running at 7⁄11 of speed, the train will take 11⁄7 of the normal time.

11⁄7 of normal time = 22 hours

Normal time = 22×7⁄11

= 14 hours

Time saved at normal seed = 22-14

= 8 hours.
