Question :65

A train passes a man standing on a platform in 8 seconds and also crosses the platform which is 264 meters long in 20 seconds. The length (in meters ) of the train is:

A.  188

B.  176

C.  175

D.  96


Let length of the train be x meters.

Time taken to cover x meters = 8 seconds

Time taken to cover (x+264) meters = 20 seconds

Time taken to cover 264 meters = 20-8=12 seconds

Speed of train = 264÷12

=22 m/s

Length of train = distance covered in 8 seconds

= 8×22

=176 meters.
