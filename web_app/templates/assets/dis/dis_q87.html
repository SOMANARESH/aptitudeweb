A, B, C walk 1 km in 5 minutes, 8 minutes and 10 minutes respectively. C starts walking from a point, at a certain time. B starts from the same point 1 minute later and A starts from the same point 2 minutes later than C. then A meets B and C at times.

A.  4⁄3 min, 3 mins

B.  5⁄3 min, 2 mins

C.  1 mins, 2 mins

D.  2 min, 3 min

Answer:b

Speeds of A, B, C(In m/min) = 1000÷5, 1000÷8,1000÷10

=200,125,100

Distance covered by B in 1 minutes = 125 m

Distance covered by C in 2 minutes = 200 m

A gains 75 m/min over B and 100 m/min over A.

Time taken by A to gain 125 m over B = 125÷75=5⁄3

Time taken by A to gain 200 m over C = 200÷100

=2.
