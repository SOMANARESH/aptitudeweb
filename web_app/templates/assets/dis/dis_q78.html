Question :78

Two trains running in the same direction at the speeds of 68 km/h and 48 km/h take 36 seconds to cross each other. If the length of one train is 85 meter, what is the length of the other train?

A.  115 meter

B.  125 meter

C.  150 meter

D.  160 meter

Answer:a

Their relative speed = (68-48)km/h

= 20 km/h

Distance covered in 36 seconds = 5⁄18×20×36

=200 meter

Length of the second train = (200-85) meter

= 115 meter.
