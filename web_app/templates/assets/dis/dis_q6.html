Question :6

A train running at the average speed of 30 km/h covers a distance in 6 hours. Find the speed of another train which covers the same distance in 5 hours.

A.  24 km/h

B.  25 km/h

C.  36 km/h

D.  42 km/h


Ratio of time taken by two trains = 6: 5

Ratio of speed = 5:6

Speed of second train = 30 × 6⁄5

= 36 k/m.
