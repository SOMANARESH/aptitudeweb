Question :38

A man takes 6 hours 15 minutes in walking a distance and riding back to the starting place. He would walk both ways in 7 hours 45 minutes. The time taken by him to ride both says is:

A.  4 hours

B.  4 hours 30 minutes

C.  4 hours 45 minutes

D.  5 hours

Answer: c

One way walking + one way riding = 6 hours 15 minutes

2× (one way walking + one way riding) = 12 hours 30 minutes

Two ways walking time = 7 hours 45 minutes

Two ways riding time = 12 hours 30 minutes − 7 hours 45 minutes

= 4 hours 45 minutes.
