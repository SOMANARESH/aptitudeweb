Question :97

A man rides at the rate of 18 km/hr, but stops for 6 mins. To change horses at the end of every 7th km. the time that he will take to cover a distance of 90 km is:

A.  6 hrs

B.  6 hrs. 12 mins

C.  6 hrs. 18 mn.

D.  6 hrs. 24 min


He covers 90 km in 5 hours @ 18 km/hr and stops for every 6 minutes at the end of every 7th km

He will stop = 90÷7

= 12 times

Total time taken = 5 hours + 6×12 minutes

= 6 hours 12 minutes.
