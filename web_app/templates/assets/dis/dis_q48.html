Question :48

A and B started at the same time from same place for a certain distance. B walking at 5/6 of As speed reached destination 1 hour 15 minutes after A. time taken by B is:

A.  6 hour 45 min

B.  7 hours

C.  7 hours 15 minutes

D.  7 hours 30 minutes

Answer: d

Ratio of speeds of A and B = 6:5

Ratio of time taken by A and B = 5:6

Difference in time = (6-5)ratio

= 1 ratio

=1 hour 15 minutes

= 75 minutes

Time taken by B = 6 ratio

= 6×75 minutes

= 450 minutes

= 7 hours 30 minutes.
