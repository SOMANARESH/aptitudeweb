Question :61

A train passes two bridges of lengths 800 m and 400 m in 100 seconds and 60 seconds respectively. The length of the train is:

A.  80 m

B.  90 m

C.  200 m

D.  150 m

Answer: c

(800m-400m)=400m is covered in (100-60)=40 seconds
Speed of the train = 400÷40=10 m/s

Distance covered in 100 seconds = 100×10=

1000 meters

Length of the train = 1000-800=

200 meters.
