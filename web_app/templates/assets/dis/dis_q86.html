A thief is noticed by a policeman from a distance of 200 m. the thief starts running and the policeman chases him. The thief and the policeman run at the rate of 10 km and 11 km per hour respectively. The distance (in meters) between them after 6 minutes?

A.  100

B.  150

C.  190

D.  200

Answer: a

Police man gains = (11-10)km/hr

= 1 km/hr

= 1000 m/hr In 6 minues, he gains = (6÷60)×1000

=100 m

Required distance = 200-100= 100m.
