Question :32

P and Q are 27 km away. Two trains with speeds of 24 km/hr and 18 km/hr respectively start simultaneously from P and Q and travel in the same direction. They meet at a point R beyond Q. distance QR is:

A.  48 km

B.  36 km

C.  126 km

D.  81 km


The faster train gains = 24 km/hr -18 km/hr

= 6 km/hr

Distance travelled by faster train (starting from P ) to gain 27km

=27×24÷6

= 108 km

Distance QR = (108-27)km

=81 km.
