Question :4

A train travelling with uniform speed crosses two bridges of lengths 300 m and 240 m in 21 seconds and 18 seconds respectively. The speed of the train is:

A.  72 km/hr

B.  68 km/hr

C.  65 km/hr

D.  60 km/hr


(300-240)m = 60 m covered in (21-18)seconds

= 3 seconds

Speed = 60÷3 m/s

= 20 m/s

= (20×18)÷5 km/h

= 72 km/h
