A train 150 meter long crosses a 150 meter long platform, in 15 seconds. What is the speed of the train?

A.  20 kmph

B.  36 kmph

C.  72 kmph

D.  144 kmph

Answer: c

Total distance covered = (150+150)miters

= 300 miters

Speed of train = 300÷15 m/s

= (300÷15)×(18÷5) km/h

= 72 km/h.
