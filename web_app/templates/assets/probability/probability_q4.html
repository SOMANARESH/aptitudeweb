Question :4

When two coins tossed,what is the probability of head on both coins is

A.   1 ⁄ 8

B.   1 ⁄ 4

C.   1 ⁄ 2

D.   1

Answer: b



Here we are tossing two coins.

We will get following possibilies.

Number of outcomes n(A)= {HH,HT,TH,TT} = 4

Here HT represents Head on Face of first coing and Tail on face of second coin

Now In this case we need to have Heads on both coins.

i.e,HH = n(HH) = 1

SO the probability of gettinig two Heads on both coins p(HH) = (Number of possibilities of getting Both Heads) ⁄ (Total number of outcomes)

P(HH) = n(HH) ⁄ n(A)

P(HH) = 1 ⁄ 4
