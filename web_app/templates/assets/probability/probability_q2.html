The probability of getting 2 on a die when it is rolled is

A.   1 ⁄ 6

B.   1 ⁄ 3

C.   1 ⁄ 2

D.   1

Answer: a



In this case, if you roll a die you will get six possibilities from 1 to 6 on the face of the die

i.e, {1,2,3,4,5 and 6} = 6

Total number of outcomrs n(A) = 6

In this you will get only one possibility of getting 2 on a face of the die

So, n(2) = No.of Possibilitites of getting 2 = {2} = 1

So the probability of getting 2 on face of die when it is rolled P(2)= n(2) ⁄ n(A)

P(2) = 1 ⁄ 6
