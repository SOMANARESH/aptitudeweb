Question :3

The probability of not getting saturday in a week is

A.   2 ⁄ 7

B.   6 ⁄ 7

C.   1

D.   1 ⁄ 7




For a week we will have 7 days namely Mon, Tue, Wed, Thu, Fri, Sat and Sun.

Total outcomes n(A) = 7

In this case, we need to find the number of possibilities of not getting saturday.

In the sense we have six possibilities not getting saturday = {Mon, Tue, Wed, Thu, Fri and Sun}

n(Sat|) = 6

So the probability of not getting Saturday P(Sat|) = n(Sat|) ⁄ n(A)

P(Sat|) = 6 ⁄ 7
