Question :18

What should be added to 15762 to make it divisible by 18?

A.  4

B.  6

C.  9

D.  12

Answer: b

A number is divisible by 18, if its units digit is divisible by 2 and 9 both

a number is divisible by 2, if its units digit is an even number

The number to be added must be an even number so that sum of two numbers is even number

A number is divisible by 9, if sum of its digits is divisible by 9

sum of digits of 15762 is 3

3+6 = 9 i.e. sum of digits is divisible by 9

6 is the required number.
