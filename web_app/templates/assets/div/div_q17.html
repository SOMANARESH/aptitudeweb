Question :17

What must be added to 583171 to make it exactly divisible by 11:

A.  5

B.  6

C.  7

D.  8

Answer:a

Sum of digits at odd places (from left side) = 5+3+7 = 15

Sum of digits at even places (from left side) = 8+1+1=10

whatever we add to the given number, will increase the sum of digits which includes the units digit i.e. to 10.

if we add 5 to given number difference between the sum of digits becomes 15-15 = 0.

5 is to be added to the given number.
