Question :14

What value must be given to X to make 5X8428 exactly divisible by 11?

A.  0

B.  2

C.  3

D.  8


Sum of digits at odd places (from left side ) = 5+8+2 =15

Sum of digits at even places (from the left side ) = X+4+8+=12+X

Difference between the two sum is 0 when 12+X=15

X=3.
