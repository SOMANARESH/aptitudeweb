Question :9

An even number, sum of whose digits is 36, is always divisible by:
A.  9

B.  18

C.  32

D.  76


An even number is always divisible by 2

Further any number sum of whose digits is 36 is always divisible by 9.

The number is always divisible by both 2 and 9

The number is divisible by 2 × 9 = 18 .
