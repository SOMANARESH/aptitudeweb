By which least number 325 must be multiplied to get a multiple of 35?

A.  3

B.  5

C.  7

D.  11


35 = 5× 7

any multiple of 35 must be divisible by 5 and 7

325 = 5 × 5 × 13 , 325 is divisible 5, but not by 7

325 must be multiplied by 7 to make it a multiple of 35.
