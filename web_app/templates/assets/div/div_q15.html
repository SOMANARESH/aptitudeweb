Question :15

What value must be given to X to make 8597X65 exactly divisible by 11?

A.  0

B.  2

C.  4

D.  7


Sum of the digits at odd places (from left side) = 8+9+X+5=22+X

Sum of digits at even places (from left side ) = 5+7+6=18

Difference between the two sums = (22+X) - 18 = 4+X

We know that any number is divisible by 11, if the difference is 0 or a multiple of 11

The difference between 22+X and 18 will be 11 when X=7
