Question :12

What value must be given to Z so that the number 9781Z4 is exactly divisible by 6:
A.  0

B.  1

C.  2

D.  3

Answer:b

A number is divisible by 6 if it is divisible by 2 and 3

The given number is divisible by 2 as it has an even number in the units place

Sum of the digits of the given number is 11 or 2

On adding 1 to it, sum of the digits will become 3 and hence divisible by 3.

Z is replaced by 1.
