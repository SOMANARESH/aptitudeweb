Question :16

A three digit number 4a3 is added to another three digit number 984 to give the four digit number 13b7 which is divisible by 11. Then the value of (a+b) is :

A.  11

B.  12

C.  9

D.  10


Sum of digits at odd places (from left side )= 1+b

Sum of digits at even places (from left side) = 3+7=10

Difference between the two sums = (1+b)-10=b-9

We know that any number is divisible by 11, if the difference is 0 or a multiple of 11

a+8 = b=9

b−9=0 when b = 9

a = 9-8 =1

a+b = 9+1 = 10.
