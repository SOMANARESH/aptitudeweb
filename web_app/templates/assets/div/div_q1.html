1. Consider the following statements
A number a 1 a2 a 3 a 4a5 is divisible by 9 if:
1. a1+a2+a3+a4+a5 is divisible by 9.
2. a1-a2+a3-a4+a5 is divisible by 9.
Which of the above statements is/are correct?

A.  1 only

B.  2 only

C.  Both 1 and two

D.  Neither 1 nor 2

Answer:a

A number is divisible by 9 if and only if sum of its digits is divisible by 9.
