Question 5 :

A mother with 6 children takes three at a time to a park. She goes with every group of three that she can form, How many times can she go to the park with distinct group of three children.

A.10

B.30

C.20

D.40


Solution:

The solution of this problem is the number of ways of selecting a group of three children among six

We know that the number of combinations of “n” things by taking “r” at a time is n C r

So the Answer is 6 C 3 = 6! ⁄ 3!×(6-3)!

6 C 3 = 6×5×4×3! ⁄ 3!×3!

6 C 3 = 6×5×4 ⁄ 3×2×1 = 20
