Question 3 :

In how many ways a team of 11 players can be selected from a group of 15 members?

A.1365

B.165

C.30030

D.54486432000

Answer: A

Solution:

The number of ways of selecting a team of 11 players from a group of 15 players can be calculated as

15 C 11 = 15! ⁄ (11!×(15-11)!)
15 C 11 = 15×14×13×12×11! ⁄ 11!×4!
15 C 11 = 15×14×13×12 ⁄ 4×3×2×1
15 C 11 = 32760 ⁄ 24 =1365
