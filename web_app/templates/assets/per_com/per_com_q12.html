Question 12 :

In how many ways can a group of 5 men and 2 women be made out of a total of 7 men and 3 women?

A.24

B.25

C.63

D.80

Answer: C

Solution:

Let us consider a men’s group.

The number of ways of selecting 5 men out of 7 men will be calculated as

7 C 5 = 7! ⁄ 5! × (7 - 5)!

7 C 5 = 7×6×5! ⁄ 5! × 2!

7 C 5 = 7×6 ⁄ 2×1 = 21

Similarly for the group of women.

The number of ways of selecting the 2 women out of 3 women is

3 C 2 = 3! ⁄ 2! × (3 - 2)!

3 C 2 = 3×2! ⁄ 2! × 1!

3 C 2 = 3 ⁄ 1 = 3

So the total number of ways of selecting a group of 5 men and 2 women out of 7 men and 3 women is calculated as

7 C 5 × 3 C 2 = 21 × 3 = 63
