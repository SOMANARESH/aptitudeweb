Question 14 :

How many straight lines can be formed from 12 non linear points on the X-Y plane?

A.66

B.132

C.22

D.33

Answer: A

Solution:

Here Straight line can be formed with the help of any two non linear points.

So the number of ways of forming straight lines is equal to selection of two points from 12 non linear points.

Number of ways = 12 C 2

12 C 2 = 12! ⁄ 2! × (12 - 2)!

12 C 2 = 12×11×10! ⁄ 2! × 10!

12 C 2 = 12×11 ⁄ 2×1 = 66
