Question :29

A tank can be filled by pipes A and B separately in 4 and 5 minutes respectively. A third pipe C can empty the tank in 3 minutes. If all the three pipes are opened together, how long will the three pipes take to fill the 7⁄10 th full tank?

A.  3 mins

B.  4 mins

C.  5 mins

D.  6 mins


Formula Used:

If a man can do a piece of work in x days,

Then his work for one day = 1⁄x

One minute’s work of three pipes = 1⁄4 + 1⁄5 - 1⁄3

= 15+12-20⁄60

Required time to fill the tank = 60⁄7

minutes. Tank to be filled = 7⁄10

= 7⁄10 × 60⁄7

= 6 minutes.
