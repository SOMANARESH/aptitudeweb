Question :9

A alone can do a piece of work in 21 days. B who is 40% more efficient than A, will finish the work in:

A.  10

B.  12

C.  15

D.  18


Formula Used:

If a man can do a piece of work in x hours,

Then his work for one hour = 1⁄x

You can simply say that

Bs one day work = 140% of As one day work

then Bs one day work = 140⁄100 of As one day work

= 140⁄100 × 1⁄21

Bs one day work =1⁄15

B can do the work in 15 days.
