Question :46

Two men undertook to do a job for 1400. One of them alone can do it in 7 days, and the other in 8 days. with the assistance of a boy. They together completed the work in 3 days. how much money will the boy get?

A.  200

B.  250

C.  275

D.  325


First man work for 3 days = 3⁄7

Second man work for 3 days = 3⁄8

Boys work = 1 - 3⁄7 - 3⁄8

= (56-24-21)÷56

= 11⁄56

Boys share = 11⁄56×1400

= 275.
