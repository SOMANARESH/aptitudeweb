Question :36

A can cultivate 2⁄5th of a land in 6 days and B can cultivate 1⁄3rd of the same land in 10 days. working together A and B cultivate 4⁄5th of the land in:

A.  4days

B.  5days

C.  8days

D.  10days

Answer:c

One day work of A = 2⁄5 × 1⁄6 = 1⁄15

One day work of B = 1⁄3 × 1⁄10= 1⁄30

One day work of A and B = 1⁄15 + 1⁄30

= 3⁄30

=1⁄10

Days taken to do 4⁄5 th work = 4⁄5 ×10

= 8 days.
