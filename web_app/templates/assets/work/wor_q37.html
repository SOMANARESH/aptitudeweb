Question :37

A, B and C did a work together and earned 729 for the work. If the ratio of work done by A, B and C is 2:3:4, find C’s share.

A.  81

B.  162

C.  243

D.  324


Wages is distributed in ratio of work done

i.e. in the ratio of 2:3:4

Sum of ratios = 2+3+4 = 9

C’s share = 4⁄9 ×729

= 324.

lets check this out

As share = 2⁄9 × 729

= 162

Bs share = 3⁄9× 729

= 243.

A’s share +B’s share + C’s share = 243+162+324. = 729.
