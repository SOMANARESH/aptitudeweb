Question :50

A job can be completed by 12 men in 12 days. how many extra days will be needed to complete the job if 6 men leave after working for 6 days.

A.  3

B.  6

C.  12

D.  24

Answer:b

Formula Used:

If a man can do a piece of work in x days,

Then his work for one day = 1⁄x

After 6 days work left is 12 men work for 6 days

remaining work have to be done by 6 men only

lets assume that they took x days to complete it

we can say that

12 men 6 days work = 6 men x days work

12 men one day work = 1⁄6

then 1 man one day work = 1⁄6×12

6 men 1 day work = 1⁄x

then one man work for one day = 1⁄6x

1⁄12×6 = 1⁄6x

x = 12

Extra days = 12-6 =6 days. Extra days = 12-6 = 6 days.
