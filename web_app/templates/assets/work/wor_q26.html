Question :26

Two pipes can fill cistern in 3 hours and 4 hours respectively and a waste pipe can empty it in 2 hours. If all the three pipes are kept open. Then the cistern will be filled in:

A.  5hours

B.  8hours

C.  10hours

D.  12hours


Formula Used

If a man can do a piece of work in x days,

Then his work for one day = 1⁄x

one minute work of three pipes = 1⁄3 + 1⁄4 - 1⁄2

= 4+3-6⁄12

= 1⁄12

times takes to fill the tank = 12 minutes.

When there is a negitive work we use a (-) sign.
