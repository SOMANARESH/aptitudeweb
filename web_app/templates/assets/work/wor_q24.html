Question :24

A and B can do a work in 12 days, B and C in 15 days, C and A in 20 days, if A, B and C work together, they will complete the work in

A.  5days

B.  10days

C.  12days

D.  18days


(A + B) + (B + C) + (A + C) = 1⁄12 + 1⁄15 + 1⁄20

= 1⁄5

Then 2 (A + B + C) = 1⁄5

=> (A + B + C) = 1⁄5 × 1⁄2

= 1⁄10days.

working together they can complete the work in 10 days.
