Question :45

A and B undertake to do a piece of work for 1,200. A alone can do it in 8 days, while B can do it in 6 days. with the help of C, they complete it in 3 days. find C”s share.

A.  150

B.  100

C.  450

D.  300

Answer:a

If a man can do a piece of work in x days,

Then his work for one day = 1⁄x

As one day work = 1⁄8

Bs one day work = 1⁄6

assume that Cs one day work = 1⁄x

ABCs one day work = As +Bs+ Cs one day work

1⁄3= 1⁄8 + 1⁄6 + 1⁄x

1⁄x = 1⁄24

Ratio of wages = 1⁄8: 1⁄6 : 1⁄24

= 3:4:1

Cs share = 1⁄8 × 1200

= 150.
