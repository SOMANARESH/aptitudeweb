Question :14

A alone can do 1⁄2 of a work in 5 days and B alone can do 1/3 of the same work in 4 days. In how many days will they work together to finish the work?

A.   22⁄9days

B.  3days

C.  4days

D.   55⁄11days

Answer:d

Formula Used

If a man can do a piece of work in x days,

Then his work for one day = 1⁄x

A can do 1⁄2 of the work in 5 days.

=> A can do the work in 5×2 = 10 days

B can do 1⁄3 of the work in 4 days

=> B can do the work in 4×3 = 12 days

As one day work = 1⁄10

Bs one day work = 1⁄12

A and B’s work for one day = 1⁄10 + 1⁄12

= 11⁄60

A and B do the work in 60⁄11 days

=55⁄11 days.
