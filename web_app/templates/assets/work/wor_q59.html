Question :59

10 men working 6 hours a day can complete a work in 18 days. how many hours a day must 15 men work to complete the same work in 12 days?

A.  6

B.  10

C.  12

D.  15


10 men can complete a work in 18 days

then 1 man can complete a work in 180 days

then 1 man working 6 hours a day can complete the work in = 180 × 6⁄24

in case two

15 men can complete a work in 12 days

1 man can complete a work in 12 × 15 days

then 1 man working x hours a day can complete the work in 12 × 15 × x⁄24

both are same because its the same work

180 × 6⁄24 = 12 × 15 × x⁄24

x= 6.
