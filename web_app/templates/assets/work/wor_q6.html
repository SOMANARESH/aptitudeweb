Question :6

A and B together can type 35 pages in 5 minutes whereas A alone can type 20 pages in 4 minutes. How many pages can B type in 1 minute?

A.  2

B.  4

C.  5

D.  6



Together they can type 35⁄5 = 7 pages in 1 minute.

A alone can type 20÷4 = 5 pages in 1 minute.

Then B alone can type 7-5 = 2 pages in 1 minute.
