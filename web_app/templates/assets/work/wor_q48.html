Question :48

While working 7 hours a day, A alone can complete a piece of work in 6 days and B alone in 8 days. in what time would they complete it together, working 8 hours a day?

A.  3days

B.  4days

C.  2.5days

D.  3.6days

Answer:a

Formula Used:

If a man can do a piece of work in x hours,

Then his work for one hour = 1⁄x

A alone can finish the work in 7 × 6 = 42 hours.

one hour work of A = 1⁄42

B alone can finish the work in 7 × 8 = 56 hours

one hour work of B = 1⁄56

one hour work of A and B = 1⁄42 + 1⁄56

=4+3⁄168

= 7⁄168

one hour work of A and B = 1⁄24

A and B both do the work in = 24 hours = 8 × 3

working 8 hours a day they will complete in = 3 days.
