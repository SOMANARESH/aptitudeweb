Question :39

A alone can do a piece of work in 12 days and B alone in 10 days. They undertook to do the work for 330. How much will A get?

A.  120

B.  150

C.  160

D.  180

Answer:b

Formula Used:

If the ratio of time taken by A and B in doing a work = x:y

Then ratio of work done by A and B = 1⁄x : 1⁄y = y:x

Ratio of wages = 1⁄12 : 1⁄10

= 10:12

=5:6

A’s wages = 5⁄11 ×330

= 150.
