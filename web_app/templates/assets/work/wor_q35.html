Question :35

A boy and girl together fill a cistern with water. The boy pours 4 liters of water every 3 minutes and the girl pours 3 liters every 4 minutes. How much time will it take to fill 100 liter of water in the cistern?

A.  36minutes

B.  42minutes

C.  48minutes

D.  42minutes


A boy pours 4 liters of water per every 3 minutes

boys work per one minute = 4⁄3

girl pours 3 liters per every 4 minute

girls work per one minute = 3⁄4

boy and girl one minute work together = 4⁄3 + 3⁄4

= 25⁄12

one minute work of boy and girl = 25⁄12 liters

12 minute work of boy and girl = 25 liters

100 liters = 48 minutes.
