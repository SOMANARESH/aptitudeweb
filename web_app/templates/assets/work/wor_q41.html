X completes a job in 2 days and Y completes it in 3 days and Z takes 4 days to complete it. If they work together and get 3900 for the job, then how much amount does Y get?

A.  1,800

B.  1,200

C.  900

D.  800

Answer:b

Formula Used:

If the ratio of time taken by A and B in doing a work = x:y

Then ratio of work done by A and B = 1⁄x : 1⁄y = y:x

Ratio of wages = ratio of work done by x:y:z = 1⁄2 : 1⁄3 : 1⁄4

= 6:4:3

ys share = 4⁄13 × 3900

=1200.
