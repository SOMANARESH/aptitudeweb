Question :23

Three pipes A, B and C can fill a cistern in 10, 12 and 15 hours respectively, while working alone. If all the ghee pipes are opened together, then find the time taken to fill the cistern:

A.  3

B.  4

C.  5

D.  6


Formula Used

If a man can do a piece of work in x days,

Then his work for one day = 1⁄x

Pipe A can fill a tank in 10 hours

Then one hour work of Pipe A = 1⁄10

similarly, Pipe B one hour work = 1⁄12

Pipe C one hour work = 1⁄15

one hour work of Pipe A,B and C together = 1⁄10 + 1⁄12 + 1⁄15

= 6+5+4⁄60

= 15⁄60

= 1⁄4

A,B and C take 4 hours to fill the tank.
