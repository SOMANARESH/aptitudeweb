Question :58

If 18 men working 7 hours a day can finish a work in 24 days. in how many days 21 men working 8 hours per day will finish the same work?

A.  18days

B.  21days

C.  24days

D.  27days


18 men can complete a work in 24 days

1 man can complete work in 24 × 18 days

1 man working 7 hours a day can complete a work in 24 × 18 × 7⁄24 days

in case two

in x days 21 men working 8 hours per day will finish the work

then 1 man working 8 hours a day can complete the work in x × 21 × 8⁄24

both are equal because its the same work

24 × 18 × 7⁄24 = x × 21 × 8⁄24

x= 18.
