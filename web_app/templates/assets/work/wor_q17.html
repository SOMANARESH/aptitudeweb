Question :17

Pipes A and B together can fill a tank in 18 hours. Pipe A alone can fill the tank in 24 hours. In how many hours, can pipe B alone fill the tank?

A.  6hours

B.  36hours

C.  48hours

D.  72hours


Formula Used

If a man can do a piece of work in x days,

Then his work for one day = 1⁄x

Pipe A and B together can fill a tank in 18 hours

Then A and Bs one hour work = 1⁄18

Pipe A can fill it in 24 hours

Then one hour work of Pipe A = 1⁄24

Lets assume that Pipe B can fill it in x hours

then one hour work of Pipe B = 1⁄x

Therefore,

Pipe A and B one hour work =

Pipe A one hour work +

Pipe B one hour work

1⁄18= 1⁄24+ 1⁄x

1⁄x= 1⁄18- 1⁄24

1⁄x= 4-3⁄72

x=72.
