Question :8

A can do a piece of work in 24 days. If B is 60% more efficient that A, then how many days does B require to do the same work?

A.  12

B.  15

C.  16

D.  18

Answer:b

Formula Used:

If a man can do a piece of work in x hours,

Then his work for one hour = 1⁄x

You can simply say that

Bs one day work = 160% of As one day work

then Bs one day work = 160⁄100 of As one day work

= 160⁄100 × 1⁄24

Bs one day = 1⁄15

B do the work in = 15 days.
