Question :44

A alone can do a piece of work in 6 days and B alone in 8 days. A and B undertook to do it for 3200. With the help of C they completed the work in 3 days. how much is to be paid to C?

A.  375

B.  400

C.  600

D.  800

Answer:b

Formula Used:

If a man can do a piece of work in x days,

Then his work for one day = 1⁄x

A can do a piece of work in 6 days

Then As work for one day = 1⁄6

Bs work for one day = 1⁄8

assume that C alone can do it in x days

then his work for one day = 1⁄x

AB and C completed whole work in 3 days then

ABCs work for one day = As + Bs +Cs work for one day

1⁄3 = 1⁄6 + 1⁄8 + 1⁄x

1⁄x= 1⁄3 - 1⁄6 - 1⁄8

1⁄x = 8-4-3⁄24

1⁄x = 1⁄24

Ratio of wages = 1⁄6: 1⁄8 : 1⁄24

= 4:3:1

Cs share = 1⁄8× 3200

= 400.
