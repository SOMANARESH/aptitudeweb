Question :27

Two taps can fill a tub in 5 minutes and 7 minutes respectively. A pipe can empty it in 3 minutes. If all the three taps are kept open simultaneously, when will the tub be full?

A.  60mins

B.  180mins

C.  90mins

D.  105mins

Answer:d

Formula Used

If a man can do a piece of work in x days,

Then his work for one day = 1⁄x

one minute work of three pipes = 1⁄5 + 1⁄7 - 1⁄3

= 21+15-35⁄105

= 1⁄105

Required time = 105 minutes.
