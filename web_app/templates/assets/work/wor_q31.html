Question :31

A tank can be filled by two pipes A and B separately in 3 hours, and 3 hours and 45 minutes respectively. A third pipe C can empty the full tank in 1 hour. When the tank was exact half filled, all the three pipes are opened. The tank will become empty in:

A.  1 hour 15 minutes

B.  2 hours 30 minutes

C.  3 hour15 minutes

D.  4 hour 10 minutes


Formula Used:

If a man can do a piece of work in x days,

Then his work for one day = 1⁄x

pipe A can fill it in = 3 hours. then one hour work of A = 1⁄3

Pipe B can fill it in = 3 hours and 45 minutes

= 33⁄4

= 12+3⁄4

= 15⁄4

then one hour work of pipe B = 4⁄15

pipe C can empty it in one hour

one hour work of pipe C = 1

one hour work of pipe A,B and C =

1⁄3 + 4⁄15 - 1

= 5 + 4 - 15⁄15

= -6⁄15

= -2⁄5

Time taken to empty the full tank = 5⁄2

(empty because we got negative value)

Time taken to empty half filled tank = 1⁄2 × 5⁄2

= 5⁄4

= 11⁄4

= 1 hour and 15 minutes.
