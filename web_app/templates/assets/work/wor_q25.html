A and B can complete a piece of work in 30 days, B and C in 20 days, and C and A in 15 days. If all of them work gogether, the time taken in completing the work will be:

A.  10days

B.  12days

C.  12 1/3 days

D.  13 1/3 days

Answer:d

(A + B) + (B + C) + (A + C) = 1⁄30 + 1⁄20 + 1⁄15

= 2+3+4⁄60

= 9⁄60

= 3⁄20

Then 2(A + B + C)= 3⁄20

=> work of A,B and C for one day = 3⁄20 × 1⁄2

= 3⁄40

Required time = 40⁄3

= 13 1⁄3 days.
