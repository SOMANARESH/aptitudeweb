Question :28

A leak can empty a full tank in 12 minutes. If the leak is absent, two pipes A and B can fill the tank separately in 15 minutes and 20 minutes respectively. If the leak is active and both the pipes are open, then the tank will be filled in:

A.  30mins

B.  60mins

C.  23mins

D.  35mins


Formula Used

If a man can do a piece of work in x days,

Then his work for one day = 1⁄x

one minute work of three pipes = 1⁄15 + 1⁄20 - 1⁄12

= 4+3-5⁄60

=4+3-5⁄60

=1⁄30

Required time = 30 minutes.

when a pipe doing a negitive work we use (-) sign.
