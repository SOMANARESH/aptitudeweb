A, B and C together can do a piece of work in 12 days. A and B together can do the same work in 20 days. In how many days C working alone can finish the work?

A.  8days

B.  16days

C.  25days

D.  30days

Answer:d

Formula Used

If a man can do a piece of work in x days,

Then his work for one day = 1⁄x

A, B and C together complete a work in 12 days.

Then A, B and C one day work = 1⁄12

A and B together can do same work in 20 days

Then one day work of A and B= 1⁄20

lets assume that C can do the same work in x days

Then one day work of C = 1⁄x

one days work of A+B+C=

one day work of (A+B)+

one day work of C

1⁄12= 1⁄20+ 1⁄x

1⁄x= 1⁄12- 1⁄20

1⁄x= 5-3⁄60

1⁄x= 2⁄60

x = 30.
