Question :53

certain number of men can complete a job in 30 days. if there were 5 men more, it could be completed in 10 days less. How many men were in the beginning?

A.  10

B.  15

C.  20

D.  25

Answer:a

let x men can complete a job in 30 days

then one man complete a work in 30x days

given if there were 5 men more, it could be completed in 10 days less. means

x+5 men can complete the same work in 20 days

then one man can complete the work in 20(x+5) days

both are equal because its the same work

30x = 20(x+5)

30x = 20x+100

10x = 100

x = 10.
