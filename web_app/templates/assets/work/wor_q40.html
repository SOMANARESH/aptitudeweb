Question :40

Suman can do a work in 3 days. Sumati can do the same work in 2 days. Both of them finish the work together and get 150. What is the share of Suman?

A.  30

B.  60

C.  70

D.  75

Answer:b

Formula Used:

If the ratio of time taken by A and B in doing a work = x:y

Then ratio of work done by A and B = 1⁄x : 1⁄y = y:x

Ratio of wages = 1⁄3 :1⁄2

= 2:3

Suman’s wages = 2⁄5 × 150

= 60.
