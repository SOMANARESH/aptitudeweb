A tank has three pipes. The first pipe can fill 1⁄2 part of the tank in 1 hour and the second pipe can fill 1⁄3 part in hour. The third pipe is for making the tank empty. When all the three pipes are open, 7⁄12 part of the tank is filled in 1 hour. How much time will the third pipe take to empty the completely filled tank?

A.  3 hours

B.  4 hours

C.  5 hours

D.  6 hours

Answer:b

If a man can do a piece of work in x days,

Then his work for one day = 1⁄x

3 pipes one hour work = pipe As + Bs + Cs one hour work

7⁄12= 1⁄2 + 1⁄3 + 1⁄x

One hour work of third pipe = 7⁄12- 1⁄2- 1⁄3

=14-20⁄24

= -1⁄4

Required time = 4 hours.
