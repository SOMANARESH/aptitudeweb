Question :3

18 children can do a piece of work in 12 days. How many children would be required to do the same work in 8 days?

A.  12

B.  18

C.  24

D.  27


18 children can do the work in 12 days

1 child can do work in 12×18 days (less children, more days)

8 children will take = (12×18)÷8

= 27 days.
