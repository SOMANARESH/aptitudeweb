Question :34

A does half as much work as B in one sixth of the time. If together they take 10 days to complete a work, how much time shall B take to do it alone?

A.  70days

B.  30days

C.  40days

D.  50days

Answer:c

Formula Used:

If a man can do a piece of work in x days,

Then his work for one day = 1⁄x

Let B alone can do the work in x days.

A does half as much B in one sixth of time

A does 1⁄2 work in 1⁄6 of Bs work

= 1⁄2 work in x⁄6

1 work in x⁄3

Then A alone can do it in = x⁄3 days

Their work for one day = 3⁄x + 1⁄x = 1⁄10

X = 40 days.
