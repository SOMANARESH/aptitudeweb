Question :54

A certain number of persons can complete a piece of work in 55 days. if there were 6 persons more, the would could be finished in 11 days less. How many persons were originally there?

A.  17

B.  24

C.  30

D.  22


lets x men can complete a work in 55 days

then 1 man can complete a work in 55x days

given if there were 6 persons more, the would could be finished in 11 days less. means

x+6 men can do in 44 days

then 1 men can do it in (x+6)44 days

both are equal because its the same work

(x+6)44 = 55x

44x+264= 55x

55x-44x = 264

11x = 264

x = 24.
