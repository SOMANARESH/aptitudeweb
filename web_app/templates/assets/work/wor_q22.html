Question :22

Four taps can individually fill cistern of water in 1 hour, 2 hours, 3 hours and 6 hours respectively. If all the four taps are opened simultaneously, the cistern can be filled in how many minutes?

A.  20

B.  30

C.  35

D.  40

Answer:b

Formula Used

If a man can do a piece of work in x days,

Then his work for one day = 1⁄x

Tap one can fill in one hour = 60 mins

Then one minute work of tap one = 1⁄60

similarly, second tap one minute work = 1⁄120

3rd tap one minute work = 1⁄180

4th tap one minute work = 1⁄360

Four taps one minute work = 1⁄60 + 1⁄120 + 1⁄180 + 1⁄360

= 6+3+2+1⁄360 = 12⁄360

=1⁄30

four taps take 30 minutes to fill the tank.
