Question :5

12 men can do a piece of work in 15 days. How many men are required to complete the work in 10 days?

A.  10

B.  12

C.  15

D.  18


Used Formula:

If m men can do a piece of work in d days.

Then 1 man can do the same work in '(d×m)'

Then n man can do the same work in (d ×m)÷ n

If 12 man can do a piece of work in 15 days

Then 1 man can do it in 15×12 days

Then 10 man can do it in (15×12)÷10.

=18 men.
