Question :4

56 men can complete a piece of work in 24 days. In how many days can 42 men complete the same piece of work?

A.  18

B.  32

C.  98

D.  48

Answer:b

Used Formula:

If m men can do a piece of work in d days.

Then 1 man can do the same work in '(d×m)'

Then n man can do the same work in (d ×m)÷ n

56 men can complete the work in 24 days.

1 man can complete the work in 24×56 days.(less man, more days)

42 men will take =(24×56)÷42

= 32 days.

Alternate Method:

Formula Used

If a man can do a piece of work in x days,

Then his work for one day = 1⁄x

56 men can complete the work in 24 days

then 56 men one day work = 1⁄24

then 1 man work for one day = 1⁄24×56

then given that x men can do the work in 42 days

1 man one day work = 1⁄42x

both are equal because its same work

1⁄42x= 1⁄24×56

x= 32.
