Question :55

A work could be completed in 100 days by some workers. However, due to the absence of 10 workers, it was completed in 110 days. the original number of worker was:

A.  100

B.  110

C.  55

D.  50


lets x men can complete the work in 100 days

then 1 man complete the work in 100x days

given that due to absense of 10 workers, it was completed in 110 days. means

x-10 workers completed the work in 110 days

1 man will complete the work in 110(x-10) days

both are same because its the same work

110(x-10) = 100x

110x - 1100= 100x

10x = 1100

x = 110.
