Question :18

A man ca do a piece of work in 5 days, but with the help of his son, he can do it in 3 days. in what time can the son do it alone?

A.  7days

B.  8days

C.  7 1⁄2 days

D.  6 1⁄2 days


Formula Used

If a man can do a piece of work in x days,

Then his work for one day = 1⁄x

Man and son can complete a work in = 3 days

Then one day work of man and son = 1⁄3

Man alone can complete the same work in = 5 days

Then one day work of man = 1⁄5

sons one day work =

man and son one day work -

man one day work

1⁄x= 1⁄3- 1⁄5

1⁄x= 5−3⁄15

1⁄x= 2⁄15

x = 15⁄2

x = 7 1⁄2
