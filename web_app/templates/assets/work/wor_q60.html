Question :60

50 people consume 350 kgs of rice in 30 days. in how many approximate days will 35 people consume 50 kgs of rice?

A.  3

B.  5

C.  7

D.  None of these

Answer:d

50 men consume 350 kgs of rice in 30 days

1 man consume 350 kgs of rice in 50 × 30 days

1 man consume 1 kg of rice in 50 × 30 ⁄350

in case two

in x days 35 men consumes 50 kg rice

1 men consumes 50 kg rice in 35 × x days

1 men consumes 1 kg rice in 35 × x⁄50 days

both are same because its one men consumption

50 × 30 ⁄350 = 35 × x⁄50 days

x = 350⁄49 days
