Question :12

A particular job can be completed by a team of 10 men in 12 days. The same job can be completed by a team of 10 women in 6 days. How many days are needed to complete the job if the two teams work together?

A.  4

B.  6

C.  9

D.  8


Formula Used

If a man can do a piece of work in x days,

Then his work for one day = 1⁄x

10 men one day work = 1⁄12

10 women one day work = 1⁄6

lets assume that working together they can complete the work in x days

then one day work = 1⁄x

both are equal because same work for same duration

1⁄x = 1⁄12 + 1⁄6

1⁄x = 1⁄4

x = 4.
