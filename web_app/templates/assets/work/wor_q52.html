Question :52

A certain number of men can do a work in 60 days. if there were eight more men, it could be completed in 10 days less. How many men were there in the beginning?

A.  75men

B.  50men

C.  40men

D.  45men

Answer: C

assume that x men can do a work in 60 days

then 1 man’s work = 60x

eight more men can do it in 10 less days means

x+8 men can do it in 50 days

1 man can do it in 50(x+8) days

both are equal because its the same work

60x= 50(x+8)

60x = 50x+400

10x = 400

x = 40.
