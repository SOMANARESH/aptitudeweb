Question :10

A can do a piece of work in 15 days and B can do it in 10 days. Working together how long will they take to finish the work?

A.  4

B.  5

C.  6

D.  7 1⁄2

Answer:c


Formula Used

If a man can do a piece of work in x days,

Then his work for one day = 1⁄x

A can do a piece of work in 15 days, then his work for one day
=1⁄15
B can do a piece of work in 10 days, then his work for one day

=1⁄10

one day work of A and B = 1⁄15+ 1⁄10

= 2+3⁄30

=5⁄30

One day work of A and B=1⁄6

A and B do work in 6 days.
