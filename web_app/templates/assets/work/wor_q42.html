A sum of money is sufficient to pay A’s wages for 21 days and B’;s wages for 28 days. The same money is sufficient to pay the wages of both for:

A.  12days

B.  121⁄4days

C.  14 days

D.  24 1⁄2 days

Answer:a

Formula Used:

If a man can do a piece of work in x days,

Then his work for one day = 1⁄x

As wages per day = 1⁄21

Bs wages per day = 1⁄28

A and Bs wages for a day = 1⁄21 + 1⁄28

= 1⁄12

wages required to pay for A and B = 12.
