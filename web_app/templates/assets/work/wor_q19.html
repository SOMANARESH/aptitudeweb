Question :19

A tap can empty a tank in 30 minutes while second tap empty it in 20 minutes. If both the taps are kept open, how much time is required to empty the cistern?

A.  12minutes

B.  25minutes

C.  50minutes

D.  1hour


Formula Used

If a man can do a piece of work in x days,

Then his work for one day = 1⁄x

Tap A can empty the tank in 30 minutes

Then one minute work of tap A = 1⁄30

Tap B can empty the same tank in 20 minutes

Then one minute work of tap B = 1⁄20

one minute work of tap A and B

= 1⁄30 + 1⁄20

= 2 + 3⁄60

= 1⁄12

Then entire work of tap A and tap B = 12 minutes.
