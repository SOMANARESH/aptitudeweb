Question :2

A single machine can produce 1 unit in 6 minutes. How many such machines work together if 1450 units are produced from 10 a.m to 12.30 p.m?

A.  2

B.  29

C.  58

D.  None of these

Answer: c

Time taken = 10 a.m. to 12.30 p.m. = 150 minutes

Units produced by 1 machine in 150 minutes = 150÷6

= 25 units

Then Machines required to produce 1450 units = 1450÷25

= 58.
