Question :1

A work can be done by a certain number of workers in 8 days. How many days will they take to finish a work four times the original work?

A.  2

B.  12

C.  22

D.  32


Assume that x men do a work in 8 days

The time they take to finish the work 4 times to the original work

= 8×4 = 32.
