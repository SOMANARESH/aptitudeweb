Question :11

Two pipes can separately fill a tank in 3 hours and 2 hours respectively. If both pipes are in operation, the tank will be filled in.

A.  1.8hour

B.  1hour

C.  1.5hour

D.  1.2hour

Answer:d

Formula Used

If a man can do a piece of work in x days,

Then his work for one day = 1⁄x

Pipe A can fill the tank in 3 hours,

Then one hour work of A = 1⁄3

Pipe B can fill the tank in 2 hours

Then one hour work of B = 1⁄2

Pipe A and B one hour work = 1⁄3+ 1⁄2

=5⁄6

Pipe A and B can fill the tank in 6⁄5 i.e. 1 1⁄5 hours.

= 1.2 hours.
