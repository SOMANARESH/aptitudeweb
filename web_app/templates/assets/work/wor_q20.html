A tap can empty a tank in one hour. A second tap can empty it in 30 minutes. If both the taps operate simultaneously, how much time is needed to empty the tank?

A.  20minutes

B.  30minutes

C.  40minutes

D.  45minutes

Answer:a

Formula Used

If a man can do a piece of work in x days,

Then his work for one day = 1⁄x

Tap A can empty a tank in one hour means in 60 minutes

Then tap A work for one minute = 1⁄60

Tap B can empty the same tank in 30 minutes.

Then one minute work of tab B = 1⁄30

Tap A and Tap B work for one minute = 1⁄60+ 1⁄30

= 1+2⁄60

= 1⁄20

Tap A and B can empty in = 20 minutes.
