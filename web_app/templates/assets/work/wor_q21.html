Question :21

A, B and C can do a piece of work in 12, 15 and 20 days respectively. In how many days working together will they finish the work.

A.  5days

B.  10days

C.  15days

D.  47days


Formula Used

If a man can do a piece of work in x days,

Then his work for one day = 1⁄x

1 day’s work of A, B and C = 1⁄12 + 1⁄15 + 1⁄20

= (5+4+3)÷60

= 12÷60

=1⁄5

Then Days taken to finish the work = 5 days.
