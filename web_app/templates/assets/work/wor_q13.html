Question :13

Two pipes A and B can fill a tank in 20 minutes and 30 minutes respectively. If both pipes are opened together, the time taken to fill the tank is:

A.  50minutes

B.  12minutes

C.  25minutes

D.  15minutes

Answer:b

Formula Used:

If a man can do a piece of work in x days,

Then his work for one day = 1⁄x

Pipe A can fill the tank in 20 mins

Than one minute work of A is = 1⁄20

Pipe B can fill the tank in 30 mins

Than one minute work of B is = 1⁄30

Pipe A and Pipe B combined work for one hour

= 1⁄20 + 1⁄30

= 3+2⁄60

=1⁄12

Pipe A and B take 12 mins to fill the tank.
