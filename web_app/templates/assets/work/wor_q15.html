Question :15

A alone can complete a work n 12 days. A and B together can complete it in 8 days. How long will B alone take to complete the work?

A.  24days

B.  20days

C.  18days

D.  16days


Formula Used

If a man can do a piece of work in x days,

Then his work for one day = 1⁄x

A alone can complete the work in 12 days

Then one day work of A = 1⁄12

A and B together can complete it in 8 days

Then one day work of both A and B = 1⁄8

Lets asume that B alone can complete in x days

Then one day work of B = 1⁄x

Then A and B one day work = A one day work + B one day work

1⁄8= 1⁄12+ 1⁄x

1⁄x = 1⁄8- 1⁄12

= 3-2⁄24

1⁄x= 1⁄24

Than x = 24.
