40 men can do a piece of work in a given time. If only 30 men be engaged. 6 more days area needed to complete the work. In how many days can 60 men do the work?

A.  9

B.  10

C.  12

D.  15


lets assume that 40 men can do a work in x days

1 man can do it in 40x days

given if 30 men do the work it will take 6 more days to complete the work

then 30 men complete the work in x+6 days

then 1 man complete the work in 30(x+6) days

both are same because its the same work

40 x = 30(x+6)

40x = 30x+180

10x = 180

x = 18 days

then 40 men can do it in 18 days then one man can do it in 18× 40 days

then 60 men can do it in 18 ×40⁄60 days

= 12 days.
