Question :12

What will come in place of X in the following

1, 6, 15, X, 45, 66, 91

A.   25

B.   27

C.   26

D.   28

Answer: D

Solution:

Let us calculate the difference between the two successive numbers.

6 - 1 = 5

15 - 6 = 9

x - 15 = y - - - > Eq.1

45 - x = z

66 - 45 = 21

91 - 66 = 25

If you observe the differences obtained in first two and last two,

there is constant difference between two differences

9 - 5 = 4 and 25 - 21 = 4

So similarly y - 9 = 4

y = 4 + 9 = 13

z - y = 4

z = 4 + y = 4 + 13 = 17

So From Eq.1 x - 15 = y

x = y + 15 = 13 + 15 = 28
