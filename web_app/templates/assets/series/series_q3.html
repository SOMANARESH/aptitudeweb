Question :3

Look at the series below

2 , 1 , 1⁄2 , 1⁄4 ,

What should come next

A.   1⁄3

B.   1⁄5

C.   1⁄8

D.   1⁄9

Answer: C

Solution:

1.Let us find the difference between two numbers

1-2 = −1

(1⁄2) −1 = (1−2)⁄2 = − (1⁄2)

(1⁄4) − (1⁄2) = (1−2)⁄4 = − (1⁄4)

If you observe the difference of each calculation,the next difference is obtained by dividing the previous difference by 2

So the next difference is

(− 1⁄4)⁄2 = − (− 1⁄8)

Finally next number can be calculated as

X − (1⁄4) = − (− 1⁄8)

X = − (1⁄8) + (1⁄4)

X = (−1 + 2)⁄8

X = 1⁄8

2.If you observe the sequence, the next number is obtained by dividing the previous number by 2.

So the next number X = (1⁄4)⁄2

X = 1⁄8
