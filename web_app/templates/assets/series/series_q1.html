Question :1

The next number in the given sequence below wil be

1, 3, 5, 7, 9,

A.   8

B.   10

C.   11

D.   13


Solution:

  • Here the given sequence represents the odd numbers.So the next number should be 11.

  • If you observe the sequence, there is constant difference between any two successive numbers.

  • 3-1 = 2

  • 5-3 = 2

  • 7-5 = 2

  • 9-5 = 2

  • So the next number is obtained by adding 2 to the previous number.

  • or the difference between next number and previous number = 2

  • X = 9+2 = 11 or X-2 = 9

  • X = 11
