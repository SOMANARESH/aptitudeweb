Question :11

The next number of the following sequence is

4, 10, 28, 82, 244, ?

A.   344

B.   730

C.   513

D.   496

Answer: B

Solution:

Let us calculate the difference between two successive numbers

10 - 4 = 6

28 - 10 = 18

82 - 28 = 54

244 - 82 = 162

X - 244 = Y - - - > Eq.1

Here if you observe the differences, we can see that the next difference is 3 times that of previous difference

6 * 3 = 18

18 * 3 = 54

54 * 3 = 162

So next difference Y = 162 * 3 = 486

From Eq.1,

X - 244 = 486

X = 486 + 244 = 730
