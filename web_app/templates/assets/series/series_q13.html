Question :13

Find the next number of the sequence

4,4,6,12,30,90,X

A.   180

B.   270

C.   315

D.   345


Solution:

If you observe the sequence,

the relation between previous and next number will be as follows

4 × 1 = 4

4 × 1.5 = 6

6 × 2 = 12

12 × 2.5 = 30

30 × 3 = 90

So based on the previous calculation,

Next number = Previous number × 3.5

X = 90 × 3.5 = 315
