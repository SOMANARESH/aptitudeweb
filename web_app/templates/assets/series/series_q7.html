Question :7

What are the next two numbers of the sequence below

8, 24 , 12, 36, 18, 54, ?, ?

A.   27,45

B.   45,81

C.   27,81

D.   45, 27


Solution:

Here if you observe the sequence, the numbers are increasing and decreasing.

By observing the sequence clearly, you can find the next numbers as follows

Leave first two numbers.

Third number = Second number ⁄ 2 = 24⁄2 = 12

Fourth number = Sum of Last two numbers = 24 + 12 = 36

Next number = Previous number ⁄ 2 = 36⁄2 = 18

Next number = Sum of last two numbers = 36 + 18 = 54

Next number = Previous number⁄2 = 54⁄2 = 27

Next number = Sum of last two numbers = 54 + 27 = 81
