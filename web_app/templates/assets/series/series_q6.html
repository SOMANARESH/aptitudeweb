Question :6

Given the sequence below, what will come in the place of '?'

4, 12, 48, 240, 1440, ?

A.   10020

B.   10160

C.   10080

D.   10800


Solution:

Let us find the difference between two successive numbers

12 - 4 = 6

48 - 12 = 36

240 - 48 = 192

1440 - 240 = 1200

From the differences we can not find the constant differences.Even if look at the differences of difference you can not find the difference.

Now observe the sequence,

12 can be written as 12 = 4 × 3

Similarly, 48 = 12 × 4

240 = 48 × 5

1440 = 240 × 6

Here the Next number of sequence follows

Next Number = Previous number × 7

So Next number = 1440 × 7 = 10080
