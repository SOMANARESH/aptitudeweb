Question :5

What is next number of the given sequence

3, 12, 27, 48, 75, 108, ?

A.   183

B.   192

C.   162

D.   147

Answer: D

Solution:

Now let us consider two successive numbers and their differences as follows

12 − 3 = 9

27 − 12 = 15

48 − 27 = 21

75 − 48 = 27

108 − 75 = 33

Now let us consider the difference between next number and previous number be X and their difference is Y

X − 108 = Y ---> Eq.1

Now if you observe the difference between two successive differences is constant = 6

15 − 9 = 6 & 21 − 15 = 6 & 27 − 21 = 6 & 33 − 27 = 6

So the difference between Y and 33 should be 6

Y − 33 = 6

Y = 33 + 6 = 37

So From Eq.1

X − 108 = 37

X = 37 + 108 = 147
