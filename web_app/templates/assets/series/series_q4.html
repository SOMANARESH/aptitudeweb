Question :4

Find the next number of the series of following

22, 24, 26, 19, 30, 14,

A.   9

B.   34

C.   19

D.   24


Solution:

Let us calculate difference between two successive numbers

24 − 22 = 2

26 − 24 = 2

19 − 26 = − 5

30 − 19 = 11

14 − 30 = − 16

From the differeces, we can not find the constant differece and also there is no multiplication, division relation between any two successive differeces

Now let us chose the two numbers alternatively

26 − 22 = 4

30 − 26 = 4

X − 30 = 4

X = 4 + 30 = 34

For the other group

19 − 24 = − 5

14 − 19 = − 5

So the next number is

Y − 14 = − 5

Y = − 5 + 14 = 9

The next two numbers in the sequence are 34,9
