Question :8

Given the sequence below and find the value of X

7, 26, 63, 124, 215, 342, x

A.   511

B.   481

C.   391

D.   421


Solution:

General Method:

Let us calculate the diffrences between two successive numbers

26 -7 = 19

63- 26 = 37

124 - 63 = 61

215 - 124 = 91

342 - 215 = 127

X - 342 = Y - - - > Eq.1

Now let us find the difference between the differences obtained previously

37 - 19 = 18

61 - 37 = 24

91 - 61 = 30

127 - 91 = 36

Y - 127 = Z Here the differences have difference of 6 from previous.

So next difference number must be 36+6 = 42.

i.e, Z = 42

So Y - 127 = 42

Y = 42 + 127 = 169

From Eq.1

X - 342 = 169

X = 169 + 342 = 511
