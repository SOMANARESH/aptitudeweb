Question :10

Complete the series

3, 15, 35, 63, ?

A.   96

B.   71

C.   73

D.   99

Answer: D

Solution:

Let us calculate the difference between the two successive numbers

15 - 3 = 12

35 - 15 = 20

63 - 35 = 28

X - 63 = Y - - - > Eq.1

If you look at the differences, there is constant difference between the differences

20 - 12 = 8 28 - 20 = 8 Y - 28 = 8

Y = 8 +28 = 36

So, from Eq.1 X - 63 = 36

X = 36 + 63

X = 99
