Seating Arrangement

  •             Seating Arrangement is the way of arranging a group of people to sit in a particular way. In this chapter we will learn about the ways of arranging the people to sit in a particular manner. And also we will learn how to make a arrangement with the help of conditions. From that arrangement you will be able to find the particular person position number from one particular side.

  • There are two types of seating arrangements

    1. Linear Arrangement
    2. Circular Arrangement

  • Linear Arrangement is the arrangement of people in a row(Line) manner.

    Ex: Sitting 5 members on a wall.

  • Circular Arrangement is the arrangement of people in a circular(round) manner.

    Ex: Sitting 5 members on a round table.

  • One of the Important thing to know before solving these types of questions is “Direction faced by a person” that means that the Person facing a direction(East, West, North and South) when he sit on a table.

    The above case is for Linear arrangements.

  • For the case of Circular arrangements, the direction may be “Towards center(Inwards)” or “Outwards center”.

  • For the direction Towards Center, the person’s right hand is in anticlockwise direction whereas for the direction Outwards Center, the person’s right hand is in clockwise direction.
