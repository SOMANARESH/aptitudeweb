Question :36

By what least number 253 must be multiplied to make it a perfect square number?

A.  7

B.  6

C.  5

D.  4

Answer:a

252=2×2×3×3×7

Since 7 occurs once in the factors of 252.

252 must be multiplied by 7 to get square number.

Note: 252 can be divided by 7 to get a perfect square number.
