Question :37

Find the least number which when multiplied by 588 gives a perfect square number

A.  1

B.  2

C.  3

D.  7


588=2×2×3×7×7

Here except for 3 all other factors are in pairs

We have to multiply 588 by 3 to make it a perfect square number

Note: the number 588 can also be divided by 3 to get it a square number.
