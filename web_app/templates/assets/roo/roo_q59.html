Question :59

What is the unit’s digit in the square root of 23409?

A.  1

B.  3

C.  5

D.  7


The number is divided into three parts: 234|0|9

Part I:

234= a2

a = 15

Remainder = 9

Part II:

90 = 2ab

b = 90÷2a

=90÷30=3

Remainder = 0

Part III:

9 = b2 = 32, remainder = 0

a = 15 and b = 3

√ 23409  is 153.
