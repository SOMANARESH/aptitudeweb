Question :39

A general wishing to draw up his 2350 men army in the form of a solid square found that he had 46 men left over. Find the number of men in each row

A.  23

B.  46

C.  48

D.  None of other

Answer: c

The number of men who formed the solid square = 2350-46

=2304

The number of men in each row = √ 2304 

= 48.
