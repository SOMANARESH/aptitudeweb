Question 1 :

LCM of 0.63,1.05 and 2.1?

A.210

B.2.1

C.6.3

D.630

Answer:C

If you find any decimal number make it into whole number by multiplying number with 10.

Note: Not only to one number multiply with each number with 10.

0.36 × 10 = 6.3

1.05 × 10 = 10.5

2.1 × 10 = 21

6.3 × 10 = 63

1.05 × 10 = 105

21 × 10 = 210

Now find the LCM of new numbers


LCM of 63, 105, 210 = 3 × 5 × 7 × 2 × 3

= 2 × 3 2 × 5 × 7;

=630

630 is the LCM of 63,105,210

but we want LCM of 0.63,1.05 and 2.1

So how many times you multiply decimal number before calculating LCM, that many number of times you need to divide the resultant LCM

= 630⁄10 × 10 = 6.3.
