Question 8 :

Product of two numbers is 4107 and HCF of these numbers is 37 then greater number is?

A.37

B.111

C.107

D.187


Let the numbers be x and y

LMC × HCF = 4107 = x × y

4107 = LCM × 37

LCM = 4107 ⁄ 37

= 111

LCM = 3 × 37

HCF = 37.

the numbers are x and y should have common multiple of 37 since their HCF is 37 so x = 37a and y = 37 b

xy = 4107

37a × 37b = 4107

ab = 4107 ⁄ 37 × 37

= 111 ⁄ 37

= 3

ab = 3

possibility a = 1 and b = 3 or a = 3 and b = 1

x = 37(1) = 37. and y = 37(3) = 111

or x = 37(3) = 111 y = 37(1) = 37

Greater number = 111.
