Question 11 :

The LCM of two numbers is 45 times HCF. If one of the numbers is 125 and sum of LCM and HCF is 1150, the other number is?

A.225

B.125

C.25

D.215


Given LCM = 45 × (HCF)

and LCM + HCF = 1150

45(HCF) + HCF = 1150

46HCF = 1150

HCF = 1150 ⁄ 46

HCF = 25

LCM = 45(25) = 1125

We know LCM × HCF = product of two numbers

1125 × 25 = 125 × x

x = 1125 × 25 ⁄ 125

x = 225.
