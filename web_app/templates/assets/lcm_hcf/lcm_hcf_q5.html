Question 5 :

HCF of 9⁄10, 12⁄25, 18⁄35 and 21⁄40

A.3⁄2800

B.3⁄1400

C.7⁄200

D.3⁄200

Answer B:

9⁄10, 12⁄25, 18⁄35 and 21⁄40

LCM of 10,25,35,40


LCM = 23 × 52 × 7 = 1400.

1400 × 9⁄10 = 1260,

1400 × 12⁄25 = 672,

1400 × 18⁄35 = 720 and

1400 × 21⁄40 = 735

HCF of 1260,672,720,735

Ascending order 672,720,735,1260


HCF of 735,1260 = 105

HCF of 672, 105


HCF of 672,105 = 21

HCF of 720, 21


HCF of 720, 21=3

HCF of 1260,672,720,735 = 3

so Now HCF of 9⁄10, 12⁄25, 18⁄35 and 21⁄40

= 3⁄LCM of 10,25,35,40

i.e. 3⁄1400
