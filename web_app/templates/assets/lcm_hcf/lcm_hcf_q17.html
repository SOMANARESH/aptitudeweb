Question 17 :

The least number which when incremented by 5 is divisible by each one of 24,32,36 and 54 is?

A.849

B.859

C.427

D.1723


Let the number be x

then x+5 is the number which is divisible by 24,32,36 and 54

Now we need to find LCM of given number and subtract 5 from result then we will get LCM of 24,32,36 and 54 is


LCM = 2 5 × 3 3= 864

x+5 =864

x = 864 − 5

x = 859.
