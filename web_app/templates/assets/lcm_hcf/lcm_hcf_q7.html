Question 7 :

The product of LCM and HCF is 24. Difference between two numbers is 2, then the numbers are?

A.2 and 4

B.4 and 6

C.6 and 8

D.8 and 10

Answer: B

LCM × HCF = 24

let two numbers be x and y

LMC × HCF = product of x and y

LMC × HCF = xy

difference between x and y = 2

x − y = 2

We know (x+y)2 = (x − y) + 4xy

(x+y)2 = 4+4(24) = 96+4=100

x+y =10
x-y = 2
_______
2x = 12

x = 6

y = x − 2 = 6 − 2 = 4

x = 6 and y = 4.
