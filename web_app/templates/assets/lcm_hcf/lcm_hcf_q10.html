Question 10 :

Three numbers are in the ratio 3:4:5 and LCM is 2400 then HCF of their numbers is?

A.20

B.40

C.80

D.120


Let HCF of 3 numbers be x

Given the ration of numbers = 3:4:5

We know HCF is common highest factor in all numbers

So, HCF should be present in every number, so let the numbers be 3x, 4x, 5x

Now LCM of 3x, 4x, 5x = 31 × x1, 41 × x 1, 5 1 × x1

LCM = 3 1 × 4 1 × 5 1 × x1

= 60x

Given LCM = 2400

60x = 2400

x = 2400 ⁄ 60

x = 40.
