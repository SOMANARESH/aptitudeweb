Arithmetic Progressions (A.P)

The sequence of the numbers in A.P is of the form with a as initial term and d as common difference between two successive numbers in the sequence is

a, a +d, a +2d, a +3d, a +4d, - - - - ,a+(n-1)d

Difference between two successive numbers is

(a +d)-a = d

(a +2d) − (a + d) = d

Here a +(n−1)d is the nth term of the Sequence

Let us assume that the sequence which is in A.P as follows

a, a +d, a +2d, a +3d, a +4d, - - - - ,a+(n-1)d

Let Sn be the Sum of n terms

Then Sn = a + (a +d) + (a +2d) + (a +3d) + (a +4d) + - - - - +(a+(n-1)d)

Sn = (a + a + a + - - - a up to n times)+(d + 2d + 3d + - - - - + (n-1)d)

Sn = (n × a) + d[1 + 2+ + 3 + - - - (n-1)]

Note:

We know that the sum of n natural numbers is

1, 2, 3, 4, - - - , n

Sn = (n⁄2)n(n + 1)⁄ 2

So Sn = (n × a) + d[[n×(n−1)]⁄ 2]

Sn = n[a + (d × (n−1)⁄ 2)]

Arithmetic Mean (A.M)

Given two numbers a and b.The number which can be insert between these two numbers to make the numbers in A.P is called Arithmetic Mean(A.M).

So the sequence with A.M as A is

a, A, b

Here the common difference between two successive numbers is

A − a = b − A

A − A = b + a

2A = a + b

A = (a + b)⁄2

Arithmetic Mean (A.M) = (a + b)⁄2

If the three numbers a, b, c are in A.P then b is called Arithmetic Mean (A.M)

A.M (b) = (a + c)⁄2

Geometric Progressions (G.P) :

The Sequence of the terms in geometric progression are of the form with a as initial term and r as common ratio between two successive numbers is as follows

a, ar, ar2, ar3, ar4, - - - - , ar(n−1)

where first term t1 = a

second term t2 = ar

third term t3 = ar2

nth term tn = ar(n-1)

Now ratio between 2nd and first term is

t2⁄t1 = ar⁄a = r

Similarly ratio between 3rd and 2nd terms

t3⁄t2 = ar2⁄ ar = r

Harmonic Progressions (H.P):

If the sequence is in A.P, then the reciprocal of every term in sequence forms as H.P

If a, b and c are in A.P, then 1⁄a, 1⁄ b, 1⁄c are in H.P
