Question :3

Find the common difference of the sequence which is in Arithmetic Progression where initial term is 19, final term is 96 and number of terms = 12

A.   5

B.   6

C.   7

D.   9


Solution:

Given Initial term a = 19

n = 12

final term tn = 96

We know that nth term is

tn = a + (n − 1)d

96 = 19 + (12 − 1)d

96 − 19 = 11d

77 = 11d

d = 77⁄11

d = 7
