Question :13

The sum of 1st 6 terms of the sequence in G.P with a=3 and common ratio r = 3 is

A.   1029

B.   1082

C.   1092

D.   1638

Answer: C

Solution:

Given a = 3 and r = 3

We know that sum of n terms is Sn

Sn = a[(1 − rn) ⁄ (1− r)]

So the sum of 6 terms is Sn

S6 = 3[(1 − 36) ⁄ (1 − 3)]

S6 = 3[(1 − 729) ⁄ (− 2)]

S6 = 3[(− 728) ⁄ (− 2)]

S6 = 3 (364)

S6 = 1092
