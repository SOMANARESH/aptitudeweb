Question :8

For the Sequence in A.P,Sum of first six terms is 0 and 4th term is 2, then the sum of first 10 terms is

A.   75

B.   80

C.   85

D.   90

Answer: B

Solution:

Let a be the the Initial term and d be the common difference then

The sequence of 1st six numbers is

a, a + d, a + 2d, a + 3d, a + 4d, a + 5d

4th term = 2

We know that nth term is

nth term = a + (n − 1)d

So 4th term = 2

a + (4 − 1)d = 2

a + 3d = 2

a = 2 − 3d −−−−−> Eq.1

Given that sum of first 6 terms = 0

a + (a + d) + (a + 2d) + (a + 3d) + (a + 4d) + (a + 5d) = 0

6a + 15d = 0

Substitute Eq.1 in Above Eq

6(2 − 3d) + 15d = 0

12 − 18d + 15d = 0

12 − 3d = 0

3d = 12

d = 4

Now From Eq.1

a = 2 − 3d

a = 2 − (3 × 4)

a = 2 − 12

a = − 10

Now the sum of first n numbers with a and d is

Sn = (n⁄2)[2a + (n − 1)d]

S10 = (10⁄2)[2(− 10) + (10 − 1)4]

S10 = 5[−20 + 36]

S10 = 5[16]

S10 = 80
