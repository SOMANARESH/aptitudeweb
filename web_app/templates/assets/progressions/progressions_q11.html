Given the sequence 20,30,45,67.5,101.25, which is in G.P then the 10th term will be

A.   512.58

B.   768.87

C.   1153.3

D.   341.72

Answer: B

Solution:

Given that The sequence is in G.P

So 1st term = a = 20

Common ration r = 2nd term⁄ 1st term

r = 30⁄20 = 1.5

Similarly r = 3rd term⁄ 2nd term

r = 45⁄30 = 1.5

So 10th term = S10

We know that the nth term of sequence which is in G.P is

Sn = a r(n − 1)

So

S10 = 20 × (1.5)(10 − 1)

S10 = 20 × (1.5)9

S10 = 20 × 38.44

S10 = 768.866

S10 = 768.87.
