What are the basic terms (1st term and common difference) in A.P of the sequence whose 10th term is 5 and 18th term is 77

A.   a = −67, d = 8

B.   a = −76, d = 8

C.   a = −76, d = 9

D.   a = −67, d = 9

Answer: D

Solution:

Given that

10th term = 5

18th term = 77

Let a be the Initial term and d be the common difference between two successive numbers in the sequence

We know that nth term tn is

tn= a + (n − 1)d

t10 = 5

a + (10 − 1)d = 5

a + 9d = 5 −−−−− > 1

and t18 = 77

a + (18 − 1)d = 77

a + 17d = 77 −−−−− > 2

Now Lets remove Eq.1 from Eq.2

Eq.2 − Eq.1

(a + 17d) − (a + 9d) = 77 - 5

8d = 72

d = 72⁄8

d = 9

From Eq.1

a + 9d = 5

a + 9(8) = 5

a = 5 − 72

a = − 67
