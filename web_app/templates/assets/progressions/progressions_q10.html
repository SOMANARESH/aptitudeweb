Question :10

If the sum of 4 numbers which are in arithmetic progression is 50 and greatest number is 4 times the least number, then the numbers in the series are

A.   4, 8, 12, 16

B.   5, 10, 15, 20

C.   10, 20, 30, 40

D.   6, 12, 18, 24


Solution:

Given a and d be the Initial and difference of the Sequence of numbers which are in A.P

So the 4 numbers in the Sequence will be

a, a+d, a+2d, a+3d

Given that the sum of these 4 numbers is 50

i.e, a + (a+d) + (a+2d) + (a+3d)

4a + 6d = 50 −−−− > Eq.1

Given that greatest term in the sequence is equal to 4 times of least number

Here

Greatest term = a + 3d

Least term = a

According to the condition

a + 3d = 4(a)

4a − a = 3d

3a = 3d

a = d

Substitute a in Eq.1, then

4d + 6d = 50

10d = 50

d = 5

a = d = 5

So sequence is as follows

5, 5 + 5, 5 + 2(5), 5 + 3(5)

5, 10, 15, 20
