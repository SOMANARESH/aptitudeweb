Given 5th term is 50421 and 3rd term is 10299 then 1st term and common ratio in of the Sequence in G.P will be

A.   a = 7, r = 7

B.   a = 21, r = 7

C.   a = 3, r = 7

D.   a = 21, r = 3


Solution:

Given 5th term = t5 = 50421

And 3rd term = t3 = 1029

We know that nth term = tn

tn = ar(n − 1)

where a and r be the initial term and common ratio of the sequence of numbers which are in G.P

Given t5 = 50421

ar4 = 50421 −−−−> Eq.1

t3 = 1029

ar2 = 1029 −−−− > Eq.2

Now Divide Eq.1 by Eq.2

(ar4) ⁄ (ar2) = 50421⁄1029

r2 = 49

r = 7

From Eq.2

ar2 = 1029

a × 72 = 1029

a = 1029⁄49

a = 21.
