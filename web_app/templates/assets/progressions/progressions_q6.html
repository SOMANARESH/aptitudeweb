Question :6

Find the sum of 1st 100 natural numbers is

A.   4040

B.   4545

C.   5050

D.   5555


Solution:

Method 1:

Let S100 be the sum of first 100 numbers.

S100  = 1 + 2 +3 +4 + − − − + 100−− > Eq-1

S100  = 100 + 99 + 98 + 97+  −− − + 1 − − > Eq-2

by adding Eq-1 and Eq-2

2S100 = 101 + 101 + 101 + 101 + - - - - + 101 upto 100 times

2S100 = 101 ×100

S100 = 10100⁄2

S100 = 5050

Method 2:

Here the natural numbers are

1,2,3,4, - - - - 100 these are in A.P

Here Common difference is 1

We know that the Sum of n terms is

Sn = (n⁄2)(2a + (n − 1)d)

For the natural numbers from 1 to 100, there is a common difference of d = 1

S100 = (100⁄2)[2(1) + (100 − 1)1]

S100 = 50 × 101

S100 = 5050
