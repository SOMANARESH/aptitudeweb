Question :16

If the geometric mean of 3 numbers is 64 and one of them is 16 then the remaining one is

A.   16

B.   64

C.   256

D.   4

Answer: C

Solution:

Let the three numbers be a,b and c

Given that the Geometric Mean of three numbers is 64

G.M = b = √ (ac) 

b = 64

Let us assume that the Other number be c = 16

then 64 = √ (a × 16) 

Squaring on both sides

642 = a × 16

a × 16 = 4096

a = 4096 ⁄ 16

a = 256.
