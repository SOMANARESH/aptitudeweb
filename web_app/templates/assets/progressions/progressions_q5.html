Question :5

What is the sum of all 3 digit numbers that leave a remainder of 2 when divided by 3?

A.   149700

B.   164749

C.   897

D.   164850


Solution:

The minimum and maximum three digit numbers are 100 and 999 respectively.

So the minimum and maximum three digit numbers leaving remainder 2 when divisible by 3 is 101 and 998

Here the sequence is as follows

101, 104, 107, 110, - - - , 995, 998 which are in A.P with difference of 3

Initial term a = 101

Here common difference is

d = 104 − 101 = 3

Let the number of terms is n

We know that the last term is 998

nth term = tn = a + (n − 1)d

998 = 101 + (n − 1)3

(n − 1)3 = 998 − 101

(n − 1)3 = 897

n − 1 = 897⁄3

n − 1 = 299

n = 299 + 1

n = 300

Now let us calculate sum of 300 numbers in the sequence

We know that sum of n terms Sn is

Sn= (n⁄2)[2a + (n −1)d]

S300 = (300⁄2)[2(101) + (300 − 1)3]

S300 = (300⁄2)[202 + (299 × 3)]

S300 = 150 [202 + 897]

S300 = 150 × 1099

S300 = 164850
