Question :17

The 1st term of a G.P is 7 and 5th term is 567.All the terms are positive.Find the common ratio and sum of first 6 terms

A.   3 and 22960

B.   3 and 2548

C.   7 and 22960

D.   7 and 2548


Solution:

Given a = 7

5th term = 567

We know that the nth term

tn = ar(n − 1)

So 5th term

t5 = 7 × r(5 − 1)

567 = 7 × r4

r4 = 567⁄ 7

r4 = 81

r4 = 34

r = 3

We know that the sum of first n terms in G.P

Sn = a[(1 − rn)⁄(1 − r)]

SO the sum of 6 terms is

S6 = 7[(1 − 36) ⁄ (1 − 3)]

S6 = 7[(1 − 729) ⁄ (− 2)]

S6 = 7(364)

S6 = 2548
