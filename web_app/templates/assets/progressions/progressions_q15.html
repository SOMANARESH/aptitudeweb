The sum of 6 terms is 6825 and sum of 3 terms is 105 then the sum of 8 terms is

A.   109252

B.   109225

C.   102995

D.   102925

Answer: B

Solution:

Let a and d be the first term and common ratio of G.P

then Sum of n terms is Sn

Sn = a[(1 − rn)⁄(1 − r)]

Given that Sum of 6 terms = S6 = 6825

and Sum of 3 terms = S3 = 105

S6 = a[(1 − r6)⁄(1 − r)]

S3 = a[(1 − r3)⁄(1 − r)]

Now lets divide S6 by S3

S6⁄S3 = 6825⁄105

{a[(1 − r6)⁄(1 − r)]}⁄{a[(1 − r3) ⁄(1 − r)]} = 65

(1 − r6)⁄(1 − r3) = 65

(1 − (r3)2)⁄(1 − r3) = 65

(12 − (r3)2)⁄(1 − r3) = 65

[(1 − r3)× (1 + r3)]⁄(1 − r3) = 65

(1 + r3) = 65

r3 = 65 − 1 = 64

r = 4

Substitute r value in S3

a[(1 − r3)⁄(1 − r)] = 105

a[(1 − 43)⁄(1 − 4)] = 105

a[(1 − 64)⁄(1 − 4)] = 105

a[(−63)⁄(−3)] = 105

a = (105 × 3)⁄63

a = 5

So the Sum of 8 terms of the Sequence with a = 5 and r =4

S8 = a[(1 − r8)⁄(1 − r)]

S8 = 5[(1 − 48)⁄(1 − 4)]

S8 = 5[(1 − 65536)⁄(− 3)]

S8 = 5[(−65535)⁄(−3)]

S8 = (5 ×65535)⁄3

S8 = 109225
