The arrangement of rational numbers -7⁄10, 5⁄-8, 2⁄-3 in ascending order is:

A.   2⁄-3, 5⁄-8, -7⁄10

B.   5⁄-8, -7⁄10, 2⁄-3

C.   -7⁄10, 5⁄-8, 2⁄-3

D.   -7⁄10, 2⁄-3, 5⁄-8


-7⁄10 = -0.7,

5⁄-8= -0.625,

2⁄-3 =-2⁄3,= -0.66.

Since -0.7 < -0.66 < -0.625,

so -7⁄10 < 2⁄-3, <5⁄-8.
