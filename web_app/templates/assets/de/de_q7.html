Question :7

Which of the following are in descending order of their value?

A.   5⁄9, 7⁄11, 8⁄15, 11⁄17

B.   5⁄9, 8⁄15, 11⁄17, 7⁄11

C.   11⁄17, 7⁄11, 8⁄15, 5⁄9

D.   11⁄17, 7⁄11, 5⁄9, 8⁄15


Converting each of the given fractions into decimal form, we get :

5⁄9 = 0.55,

7⁄11 = 0.63,

8⁄15 = 0.533,

11⁄17 = 0.647

Clearly,

0.647 > 0.63 > 0.55 > 0.533

So, 11⁄17 > 7⁄11 > 5⁄9 > 8⁄15 .
