Question :13

Which of the following numbers does not lie between 4⁄5 and 7⁄13?

A.  1⁄2

B.  2⁄3

C.  5⁄4

D.  5⁄7


4⁄5 = 0.8,

7⁄13 = 0.53,

1⁄2= 0.5,

2⁄3 = 0.66,

3⁄4 = 0.75,

5⁄7 = 0.714.

Clearly, 0.5 does not lies between 0.53 and 0.8.

Then 1⁄2 does not lies between 4⁄5 and 7⁄13.
