Question :8

What is the difference between the biggest and the smallest fraction among 2⁄3 , 3⁄4, 4⁄5 and 5⁄6?

A.   1⁄6

B.   1⁄12

C.   1⁄20

D.   1⁄30

Answer: A

Converting each of the given fractions into decimal form, we get :

2⁄3= 0.66

3⁄4 = 0.75,

4⁄5 = 0.8,

5⁄6 = 0.833.

Since

0.833 > 0.8 > 0.75 > 0.66,

so 5⁄6 > 4⁄5 > 3⁄4, > 2⁄3.

Required difference = (5⁄6 − 2⁄3)

= 1⁄6
