Question :12

Which of the following fractions is less than 7/8 and greater than 1/3?

A.   1⁄4

B.   23⁄24

C.   11⁄12

D.   17⁄24

Answer:d

7⁄8= = 0.875,

1⁄3 = 0.333,

1⁄4 = 0.25,

23⁄24 = 0.958,

11⁄12= 0.916,

17⁄24= 0.708.

Clearly, 0.708 lies between 0.333 and 0.875.

Then 17⁄24 lies between 1⁄3 and 7⁄8.
