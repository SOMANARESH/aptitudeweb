Out of two numbers, 40% of the greater number is equal to 60% of the smaller. If the sum of the numbers is 150, then the greater number is:

A.  70

B.  80

C.  90

D.  60

Answer: C

Let the numbers be X and Y (X>Y)

Given that 40% of greater number = 60% of smaller number

(40⁄100)X = (60⁄100)Y

Y = ((40×100)⁄(100×60))X

Y = (2⁄3)X

Given that the sum of numbers = 150

X+Y = 150

X+(2⁄3)X = 150

(3X+2X)⁄3 = 150

5X⁄3 = 150

X = 150×3⁄5

X = 30×3

X = 90
