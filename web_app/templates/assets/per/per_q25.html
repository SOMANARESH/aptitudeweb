A spends 90% of his salary and B spends 85% of his salary. But savings of both are equal. Find the income of B, if sum of their incomes is 5000.

A.  2000

B.  2500

C.  3000

D.  3500

Answer: A

Savings of A = (100-90)% = 10% of his salary

Savings of B = (100-85)% = 15% of his salary

But both savings are equal

10% of As Salary = 15% of Bs salary

As Salary⁄Bs Salary = 10⁄15

As Salary⁄Bs Salary = 2⁄3

As Salary = As portion⁄Sum of ratio×Total income

As Salary = 2⁄5×5000

As Salary = 2×1000

As Salary = 2000
