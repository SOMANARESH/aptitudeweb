Question :3

18% of which number is equal to 12% of 75?

A.  50

B.  100

C.  2

D.  3⁄2


Let the required number be X

Given that 18% of X is equal to 12% of 75.

Initially 12% of 75 can be calculated as

12% of 75 = (12⁄100)×75

12% of 75 = 3×75⁄25

12% of 75 = 3×3

12% of 75 = 9

Now 18% of X = (18⁄100)×X

Based on condition

18% of X = 12% of 75

(18⁄100)×X = 9

X = 9×(100⁄18)

X = 100⁄2

X = 50
