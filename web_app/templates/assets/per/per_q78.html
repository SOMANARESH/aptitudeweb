The difference of two numbers is 45% of their sum. The ratio of the larger number to the smaller number is:

A.  20:9

B.  9:20

C.  29:11

D.  11:29

Answer: C

Let the numbers be X and Y (X>Y)

Given that difference of two numbers is 45% of their sum

i.e. X-Y = 45%(X+Y)

X-Y = (45⁄100)(X+Y)

X-Y = (9⁄20)(X+Y)

20(X-Y) = 9(X+Y)

20X-20Y = 9X+9Y

20X-9X = 9Y+20Y

11X = 29Y

X⁄Y = 29⁄11
