Rubina decided to donate 16% of her monthly salary to an NGO. On the day of donation she changed her mind and donated 6567, which was 75% of what she had decided earlier. How much is rubinas monthly salary?

A.  8756

B.  54725

C.  656700

D.  45696

Answer: B

Let monthly income of rubina be X

Then 16% of 75% of X = 6567

16⁄100 × 75⁄100 × X = 6567

((16×75)⁄(100×100)) X = 6567

(1200⁄10000)) X = 6567

X = 6567×100⁄12

X = 656700⁄12

X = 54725
