Question :26

15 liter of a mixture contains alcohol and water in the ratio of 1:4. If 3 litres of water is mixed in it, the percentage of alcohol in the new mixture will be:

A.  15

B.  16 2⁄3

C.  17

D.  18 1⁄2


Given that the alcohol and water ratio in 15 litres of mixture is 1:4

Alcohol⁄Water = 1⁄4

Water in mixture = (4⁄5)×Total mixture

Water in mixture = (4⁄5)×15

Water in mixture = 12liters

Now After adding of 3 litres of water to 15 litres of mixture, we have 15 litres of water in 18 litres of mixture and 3 litres of alcohol

Alcohol % = Total Alcohol⁄Total Mixture

Alcohol % = 3⁄18

Alcohol % = 1⁄6

Alcohol % = (1⁄6)×100⁄100

Alcohol % = (162⁄3)%
