The sum of 5% of a number and 4% of the other number is equal to 2⁄3 of sum of 6% of the first number and 8% of the second number. The ratio of the first number to the second is:

A.  2:3

B.  3:2

C.  3:4

D.  4:3

Answer: D

Let the numbers be x and y

Then

5% of x+4% of y = 2⁄3×(6%of x +8% of y )

3(5% of x+4% of y) = 2×(6%of x +8% of y )

15% of x + 12 % of y = 12 % of x + 16% of y

3% of x = 4% of y

X:y = 4:3.
