If 40% of a number is equal to 3⁄7 of other number and sum of these numbers is 145, what is the larger number?

A.  85

B.  70

C.  75

D.  None of these

Answer: C

Let the two numbers be X and Y

Given that 40% of X = 3⁄7 of Y

(40⁄100) X = (3⁄7)Y

(2⁄5)X = (3⁄7)Y

X⁄Y = (3×5)⁄(7×2)

X⁄Y = 15⁄14

X = (15⁄14)Y

Given that Sum of numbers is 145

X+Y = 145

(15⁄14)Y+Y = 145

(15Y+14Y)⁄14 = 145

29Y⁄14 = 145

Y = 145×14⁄29

Y = 5×14

Y = 70

X+Y = 145

X+70 = 145

X = 145-70

X = 75
