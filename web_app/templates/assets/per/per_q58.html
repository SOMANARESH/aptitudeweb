Given that 10% of As income = 15% of Bs income = 20% of Cs income. If sum of their income is 7800, then Bs income is:

A.  3600

B.  3000

C.  2400

D.  1800


10% of A = 15% of B = 20% of C

(10⁄100)A = (15⁄100)B = (20⁄100)C

10A = 15B = 20C

10A = 15B

B = (10⁄15)A

10A = 20C

C = (10⁄20)A

A:B:C = A : (10⁄15)A : (10⁄20)A

{ L.C.M of 15 and 20 = 60}

A:B:C = (60⁄60)A : (40⁄60)A : (30⁄60)A

A:B:C = 6:4:3

Bs income = 4⁄13×7800

Bs income = 2400.
