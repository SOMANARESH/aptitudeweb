Question :71

If two numbers are respectively 20% and 50% of a third number, what is the ratio between the two numbers?

A.  5:2

B.  2:5

C.  1:5

D.  1:2

Answer: B

Let the numbers be X , Y and Z

Given that

X = 20% of Z and Y = 50% of Z

X = 20% of Z

X = (20⁄100)Z

X = (1⁄5)Z

Z = 5X--->(1)

Y = 50% of Z

Y = (50⁄100)Z

Y = (1⁄2)Z

Z = 2Y--->(2)

From (1) and (2)

5X = 2Y

X⁄Y = 2⁄5

X:Y = 2:5
