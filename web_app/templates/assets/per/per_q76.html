Question :76

If the sales tax be reduced from 3 1⁄2 % to 3 1⁄3 %, what difference does it make to a person who purchase an article whose marked pricei is 8400?

A.  20

B.  15

C.  14

D.  10

Answer: C

Tax paid for 8400 at 3 1⁄2 % is

= 8400×3 1⁄2 %

= 8400×7⁄200

= 42×7

= 294

Tax paid for 8400 at 3 1⁄3 % is

= 8400×3 1⁄3 %

= 8400×10⁄300

= 28×10

= 280

Difference between Taxes is

294-280 = 14
