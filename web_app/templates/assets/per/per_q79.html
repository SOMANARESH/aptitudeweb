The difference of two numbers is 15% of their sum. The ratio of the larger number to the smaller number is:

A.  23:17

B.  11:9

C.  17:11

D.  23:11

Answer: A

Let the numbers be X and Y (X>Y)

Gicen that difference of two numbers is 15% of their sum

i.e. X-Y = 15%(X+Y)

X-Y = (15⁄100)(X+Y)

X-Y = (3⁄20)(X+Y)

20(X-Y) = 3(X+Y)

20X-20Y = 3X+3Y

20X-3X = 3Y+20Y

17X = 23Y

X⁄Y = 23⁄17
