Question :37

A fruit seller had some apples. He sells 40% apples and still has 420 apples. Originally he had:

A.  588 apples

B.  600 apples

C.  672 apples

D.  700 apples

Answer: D

Stock left unsold = (100-40)%

= 60%

60% = 420 apples

1% = 420⁄60

1% = 7

100% = 7×100

100% = 700

Total apples = 700
