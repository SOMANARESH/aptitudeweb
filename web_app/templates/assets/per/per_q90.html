Question :90

In a test consisting of 75 questions carrying one mark each. Samir answered 75% of the first 40 questions correctly. What approximate percent of the other 35 questions does he need to answer correctly to score 80% on the entire test?

A.  90

B.  75

C.  86

D.  60


Given that the total number of questions = 75

Given Each question carries one mark

For the first 40 questions samir answered 75% correctly

i.e. Total marks by samir = 75% of 40

Total marks by samir = (75⁄100)40

Total marks by samir = (3⁄4)40

Total marks by samir = 3×10

Total marks by samir = 30

Number of questions to answered for getting 80% of score is

80% of 75 = (80⁄100)75

80% of 75 = (4⁄5)75

80% of 75 = 4×15

80% of 75 = 60

Remaining marks to be get = 60-30 = 30

So Samir has to score 30 marks out of 35 questions

Required percentage = (30⁄35)×100

Required percentage = 0.8571×100

Required percentage = 85.7

Required percentage = 86
