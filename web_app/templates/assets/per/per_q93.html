Vipul decided to donate 5% of his salary. On the day of donation he changed his mind and donated 1687.50 which was 75% of what he had decided earlier. How much is vipuls salary?

A.  37500

B.  45000

C.  33750

D.  None of these

Answer: B

Let vipul salary = X

Then 5% of 75% of X = 1687.50

5⁄100 × 75⁄100 × X = 1687.50

((5×75)⁄(100×100)) X = 1687.50

(375⁄10000)) X = 1687.50

X = 1687.50×10000⁄375)

X = 16875000⁄375)

X = 45000
