Question :54

Three fourth of one number is equal to 60% of another number. What is the ratio of the first number to the second number?

A.  3:4

B.  5:4

C.  6:7

D.  None of these

Answer: D

Let the numbers be X and Y

Then 3⁄4 of X = 60% of Y

(3⁄4)X = 60⁄100 of Y

(3⁄4)X = 3⁄5 of Y

X⁄Y = (3×4)⁄ (3×5)

X⁄Y = 4⁄ 5
