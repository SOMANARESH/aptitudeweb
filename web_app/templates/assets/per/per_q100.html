A man had a certain amount with him. He spent 20% of that to buy an article and 5% of the remaining on transport. Then he gifted 120. If he is left with 1400, the amount he spent on transport is:

A.  95

B.  80

C.  76

D.  61


Let the total amount be X

Given that a man spends 20% of amount on an article. So he has 80% remaining

Money spend on an article = 20% of X

Money spend on an article = (20⁄100)X

Remaining amount = 80% of total amount

Remaining amount = 80% of X

Remaining amount = (80⁄100)X

Given that he spend 5% of remaining amount on transport

Money spend on transport = 5% of remaining amount

= 5% (80⁄100)X

= (5⁄100)×(80⁄100)X

= ((5×80)⁄(100×100))X

= (400⁄10000)X

Money spend on transport = (4⁄100)X

Given that he gifted 120 from remaining.

Now total remaining amount = X - (money on an article+ money on transport + gift)

Given that remaining amount = 1400

X - (20⁄100)X - (4⁄100)X-120 = 1400

(100X-20X-4X)⁄100 = 1400+120

76X⁄100 = 1520

X = 1520×(100⁄76)

X = 20×100

X = 2000

So Money spend on transport = (4⁄100)X

Money spend on transport = (4⁄100)×2000

Money spend on transport = 4×20 = 80
