Question :38

If a man after spending 97% of his income, saves 300 per month, his annual income is:

A.  10000

B.  12000

C.  36000

D.  120000

Answer: D

Savings = (100-97)% = 3% of income

3% of income = 300

1% of income = 300⁄3

1% of income = 100

100% of income = 100×100

Total income = 10000 per month

For 12 months Total income = 10000×12

Annual Total income = 120000
