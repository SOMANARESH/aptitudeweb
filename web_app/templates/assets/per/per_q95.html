Question :95

Radha spends 40% of her salary on food 20% on house rent, 10% on entertainment and 10% on conveyance. If her savings at the end of a month are 1500, then her salary per month is:

A.  8000

B.  7500

C.  6000

D.  10000

Answer: B

Total amount spend

= (40+20+10+10)%

= 80%

Savings = 100%-80% = 20%

20% of income = 1500

(20⁄100)Total income = 1500

Total income = 1500×100⁄20

Total income = 75×100

Total income = 7500
