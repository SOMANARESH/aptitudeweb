If population of women in a village is 80% of population of men, what is the population of men as a percentage of population of women?

A.  80%

B.  100%

C.  112.5%

D.  125%

Answer: D

Given that population of women in a village is 80% of population of men

Population of women = 80⁄100×Population of men

Population of men = 100⁄80×Population of women

Population of men = 1.25×Population of women

Population of men = 1.25×100⁄100×Population of women

Population of men = 125⁄100×Population of women

Population of men = 125% of Population of women
