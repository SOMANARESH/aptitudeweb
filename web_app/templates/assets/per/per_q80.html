If 60% of As income is equal to 75% of Bs income, then Bs income is equal to x% of As income. The value of x is:

A.  70

B.  60

C.  80

D.  90

Answer: C

Let us assume that P and Q be incomes of A and B

Given that 60% of As income = 75% of Bs income

(60⁄100)P = (75⁄100)Q

P = ((75×100)⁄(100×60))Q

P = (75⁄60)Q

P = (5⁄4)Q

Q = (4⁄5)P

Q = 0.8P

Q = 80% of P

Bs income = 80% of As income

x = 80
