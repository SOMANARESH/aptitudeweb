What is 20% of 25% of 300?

A.  150

B.  60

C.  45

D.  15

Answer: D

25% of 300 can be calculated as

25% of 300 = (25⁄100)×300

25% of 300 = 25×3

25% of 300 = 75

Now 20% of 25 % 300 is

20%(25%of 300) = (20⁄100)×75

20% of 25 % 300 = (2×75)⁄10

20% of 25 % 300 = 150⁄10

20% of 25 % 300 = 15
