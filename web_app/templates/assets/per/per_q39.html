Question :39

A person spends 30% of monthly salary on rent, 25% on food, 20% on childrens education and 12% on electricity, and the balance of 1040 on the remaining items. What is the monthly salary of the person?

A.  8000

B.  9000

C.  10600

D.  None of these

Answer:a

Expenditure on remaining items

= 100% -(30+25+20+12)%

= 13%

=1040

Monthly salary = 1040⁄13×100

= 8000.
