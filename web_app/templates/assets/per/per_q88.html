Difference of two numbers is 1660. If 6 1⁄2% of one number is 8 1⁄2 of the other number, the smaller number is:

A.  7055

B.  5395

C.  3735

D.  2075


Let us assume that two numbers be X and Y (X>Y)

Given that difference between two numbers is 1660

i.e., X-Y = 1660--->(1)

Given 6 1⁄2% of one number is 8 1⁄2 % of the other number

6 1⁄2% of X = 8 1⁄2 of Y

(13⁄2)X = (17⁄2)Y

13X = 17Y

X = (17⁄13)Y

Substitute X in (1)

(17⁄13)Y-Y = 1660

((17-13)⁄13)Y = 1660

(4⁄13)Y = 1660

Y = 1660×13⁄4

Y = 415×13

Y = 5395
