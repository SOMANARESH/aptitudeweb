Question :33

A plot of 640 sq. ft. is available at the rate of 4600 per sq ft. if 30% of the total cost of the plot is payable at booking of the plot, how much is the booking amount?

A.  8,83,200

B.  8,38,200

C.  9,83,200

D.  None of these

Answer: A

The amount for booking 1 sq.ft is 4600.

But we have 640 sq.ft.

So total amount for booking 640 sq.ft is

= 640×4600

= 2944000

Now total booking amount is 30% of total cost

30% of total cost = 30% of 2944000

30% of total cost = (30⁄100)×2944000

30% of total cost = (30×2944000)⁄100

30% of total cost = 88320000⁄100

30% of total cost = 883200
