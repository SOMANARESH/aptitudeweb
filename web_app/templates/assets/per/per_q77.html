Question :77

If present price of sugar after 10% reduction is 18 per kg. find the original price of sugar per kg.

A.  1.80

B.  10.80

C.  16.20

D.  20


Let original price of sugar X per kg

After 10% reduction the price becomes 18

i.e. X - (10% of X) = 18

{ 10% = 10⁄100 = 1⁄10 = 0.1}

X - 0.1X = 18

0.9X = 18

X = 18⁄0.9

X = 180⁄9

X = 20
