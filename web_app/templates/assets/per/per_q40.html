One fifth of a number is 18. What will be 30% of the same number?

A.  5.4

B.  24

C.  60

D.  None of these

Answer: D

Let the number be X

One fifth of the number = 18

(1⁄5)×X = 18

X = 18×5

X = 90

Now 30% of a number is

30% of X = (30⁄100)×X

30% of X = (30⁄100)×90

30% of X = ((30×90)⁄100)

30% of X = (2700⁄100)

30% of X = 27
