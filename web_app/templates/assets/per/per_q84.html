Question :84

If 50% of (x - y) = 30% of ( x + y ), then what percent of x is y?

A.  25

B.  33 1⁄3

C.  40

D.  400


Given 50% of (X-Y) = 30% of (X+Y)

(50⁄100)(X-Y) = (30⁄100)(X+Y)

(1⁄2)(X-Y) = (3⁄10)(X+Y)

10(X-Y) = 6(X+Y)

10X-10Y = 6X+6Y

10X-6X = 6Y+10Y

4X = 16Y

Y⁄X = 4⁄16

Y⁄X = 1⁄4

Y = 0.25X

Y = 25% of X
