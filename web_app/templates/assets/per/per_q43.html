Question :43

In a class of 65 students and 4 teachers, each students got sweets that are 20% of total number of students and each teacher got sweets that are 40% of the total number of students. How many sweets were there?

A.  845

B.  897

C.  949

D.  104

Answer:c

Each student got = 20% of 65

= 13 sweets

Each teacher got = 40% of 65

= 26 sweets

Total number of sweets = 65×13+4×26

=845+104

=949.
