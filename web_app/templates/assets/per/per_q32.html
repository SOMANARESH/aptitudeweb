Question :32

Salaries of A and B are in the ratio of 3:4. Both of them spend 75% of their salaries and save rest of the money. Find the ratio of their savings.

A.  3:4

B.  4:3

C.  1:1

D.  Cant say


Tota amount spend by A and B is 75%

So total amount saved by A and B is (100-75)%

So total amount saved by A and B is 25%

Ratio of savings = 25% of 3 : 25% of 4 = 3:4.
