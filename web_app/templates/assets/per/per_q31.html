Question :31

If rate of income tax is increased from 20% to 25%. Find the additional amount of tax, a person has to pay, if his total income is 40,000.

A.  2000

B.  2500

C.  8000

D.  10000


Total tax paid by a person for 40000 at 20% is

= (20⁄100)×40000

= (20×40000⁄100)

= (800000⁄100)

= 8000

Total tax paid by a person for 40000 at 25% is

= (25⁄100)×40000

= (25×40000⁄100)

= (1000000⁄100)

= 10000

So the additional amount paid by a person is

= 10000-8000

= 2000
