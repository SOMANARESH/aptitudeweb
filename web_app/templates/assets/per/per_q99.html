A man spends 12 1⁄2 % of her salary on items of daily use and 30% of the remainder on house rent. After that he is left with 2940. How much is his salary?

A.  4800

B.  5200

C.  4500

D.  4000


Let the total income be X

Given that he 12 1⁄2 % of income on daily use items.

Amount spend on daily use items = 25⁄2% of X

Amount spend on daily use items = (25⁄200)X

Remaining amount = X - (25⁄200)X

Remaining amount = ((200X-25X)⁄200)

Remaining amount = (175X⁄200)X

Given that he spend 30% of remaining amount on house rent

Money spend on rent = 30% of remaining amount

Money spend on rent = 35% (175X⁄200)

Money spend on rent = (30⁄100)×(175X⁄200)

Money spend on rent = ((30×175X)⁄(100×200))X

Money spend on rent = (5250⁄20000)X

Money spend on rent = (525⁄2000)X

Now total remaining amount = X - (money on daily use items + money on rent)

Given that remaining amount = 2940

X - (25⁄200)X - (525⁄2000)X = 2940

(2000X-250X-525X)⁄2000 = 2940

1225X⁄2000 = 2940

X = 2940×(2000⁄1225)

X = 5880000⁄1225

X = 4800
