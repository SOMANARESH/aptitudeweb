Question :62

Sum of 5% of a number and 9% of other number is equal to sum of 8% of first number and 7% of the second number. Find ratio between the numbers:

A.  2:3

B.  3:2

C.  5:7

D.  8:9

Answer: A

Let the numbers be x and y

Given that

5% of x +9% of y = 8% of x + 7% of y

3% of x =2% of y

3x = 2y

x:y = 2:3.
