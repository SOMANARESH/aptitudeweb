Question :73

Two numbers are more than the third number by 20% and 50% respectively. First number is percent of the second number?

A.  100%

B.  150%

C.  80%

D.  120%

Answer: C

Let the numbers be X , Y and Z

Given that

X = 20% more than Z and Y = 50% more than Z

X = 20% more than Z

X = Z + (20⁄100)Z

X = ((100Z+20Z)⁄100)

X = (120Z⁄100)

X = (6Z⁄5)

Z = (5⁄6)X--->(1)

Y = 50% more than Z

Y = Z + (50⁄100)Z

Y = ((100Z+50Z)⁄100)

Y = (150Z⁄100)

Y = (3Z⁄2)

Z = (2⁄3)Y--->(2)

From (1) and (2)

(5⁄6)X = (2⁄3)Y

X⁄Y = (2×6)⁄(5×3)

X⁄Y = 12⁄15

X = Y×12⁄15

X = 0.8Y

X = 80% of Y
