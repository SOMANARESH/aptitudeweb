Question :46

Deepa bought a calculator with 30% discount on the listed price. Had she not got the discount, she would have paid 82.50 extra. At what price did she buy the calculator?

A.  192.50

B.  275

C.  117.85

D.  None of these

Answer: A

Discount = 30% of list price

Let the list price be X

30% Of list price = 82.50

30% of X = 82.50

(30⁄100)×X = 82.50

X = (100⁄30)×82.50

X = 100×82.50⁄30

X = 8250⁄30

X = 275

So total price paid by deepa = Lised price - Discount

Total price paid by deepa = 275-82.50

Total price paid by deepa = 192.5
