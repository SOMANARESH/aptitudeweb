Mr.giridhar spends 50% of his monthly income on household items and out of the remaining he spends 50% on transport, 25% on entertainment, 10% on sports and remaining amount of 900 is saved. What is Mr.giridhars monthly income?

A.  6000

B.  12000

C.  9000

D.  None of these


Let the total income be X

Given that 50% of his income spent on household items. So he has 90% remaining

Money spend on household items

= 50% of X

= (50⁄100)X

Remaining amount = 50% of income

Remaining amount = 50% of X

Remaining amount = (50⁄100)X

Given that he spend 50% on transport,25% on entertainment, 10% on sports of remaining amount.

Money spend on others = (50%+25%+10%) of remaining amount

= 85% of remaining amount

= 85% (50⁄100)X

= (85⁄100)×(50⁄100)X

Money spend on others = ((85×50)⁄(100×100))X

= (4250⁄10000)X

=(425⁄1000)X

Now total remaining amount = X - (money on household+money on others)

Given that remaining amount = 900

X - (50⁄100)X - (425⁄1000)X = 900

(1000X-500X-425X)⁄1000 = 900

75X⁄1000 = 900

X = 900×(1000⁄75)

X = 12×1000

X = 12000
