Question :47

A reduction in the price of apples enables a person to purchase 3 apples for 1 instead of 1.25. what is the % of reduction in price(approximately)

A.  20

B.  25

C.  30

D.  33


Total amount for 3 apples at Rs.1 = 3×1

Total amount for 3 apples at Rs.1 = 3

Total amount for 3 apples at Rs.1.25 = 3×1.25

Total amount for 3 apples at Rs.1 = 3.75

Reduction in amount = 3.75-3 = 0.75

% of reduction =

(Reduction in amount⁄Total amount for 3 apples at Rs.1.25 )×100

% of reduction = (0.75⁄3.75)×100

% of reduction = (0.75×100⁄3.75)

% of reduction = (75⁄3.75)

% reduction = 20
