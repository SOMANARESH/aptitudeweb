Percentage.

  • The rules mentioned in this chapter are widely used in solving problems on discount, profit and loss, simple interest, compound interest, menstruation etc.

Percent:

  • Percent means per hundred or out of fraction, x% can be written as x⁄100.
  • To convert percent into a fraction or decimal, divide the given number by 100.
  • To concert a fraction or decimal into percentage, multiply the given fraction or decimal by 100.

Calculating the percentage:

  • To convert x out of y into % form, divide x by y and multiply the result by 100.
    x out of y = x⁄y × 100%.
  • Both x and y should be in the same unit.
