Question :51

A salesman receives salary of 5000 month and 20% commission on total sales. What is the amount of sales, if his total earnings in a month is 9000?

A.  15000

B.  20000

C.  50000

D.  60000


Total earnings = 9000

Salary = 5000

We know that

Total earnings = Commission+Salary

Commission = total earnings − salary

Commission = 9000-5000

Commission = 4000.

Given that Commission = 20% of sales

20% of Sales = 4000

(20⁄100)×Sales = 4000

(1⁄5)×Sales = 4000

Sales = 5×4000

Sales = 20000
