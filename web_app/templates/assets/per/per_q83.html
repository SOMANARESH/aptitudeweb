If 15% of (A + B ) = 25% of ( A - B ), then what percent of B is equal to A?

A.  10

B.  60

C.  200

D.  400

Answer: D

Given 15% of(A+B) = 25% of (A-B)

(15⁄100)(A+B) = (25⁄100)(A-B)

(3⁄20)(A+B) = (1⁄4)(A-B)

12(A+B) = 20(A-B)

12A+12B = 20A-20B

20A-12A = 20B+12B

8A = 32B

A⁄B = 32⁄8

A⁄B = 4

A = 4B

A = 400% of B
