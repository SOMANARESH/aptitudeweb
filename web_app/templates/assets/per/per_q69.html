Bovina spent 44668 on her air tickets, 56732 on buying gifts for the family members and the remaining 22% of the total amount she had as cash with her. What was the total amount?

A.  28600

B.  130000

C.  101400

D.  None of these


Given that She spent 44668 and 56732 from total amount for tickets and gifts.

Total money spent on others = 44668+56732

Total money spent on others = 101400

Given that 22% amount kept with her i.e he spent 78% for others

78% of Total amount = 101400

(78⁄100)×Total amount = 101400

Total Amount = 101400 ×(100⁄78)

Total Amount = (10140000⁄78)

Total Amount = 130000
