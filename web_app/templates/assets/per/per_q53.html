Question :53

If 30% of a number is equal to four fifth of the another number. What is the ratio between the two numbers?

A.  3:4

B.  3:8

C.  8:3

D.  None of these


Let the two numbers be X and Y

Given that 30% of X = four fifth of Y

(30⁄100)X = (4⁄5)Y

(3⁄10)X = (4⁄5)Y

X⁄Y = (4×10)⁄(5×3)

X⁄Y = 40⁄15

X⁄Y = 8⁄3
