Question :75

Two numbers are less than the third number by 50% and 54% respectively. By how much percent is the second number less than the first number?

A.  13

B.  10

C.  12

D.  None of these


Let the numbers be X , Y and Z

Given that X = 50% less than Z and Y = 54% less than Z

X = 50% less than Z

X = Z - (50⁄100)Z

X = ((100Z-50Z)⁄100)

X = (50Z⁄100)

X = (Z⁄2)

Z = 2X--->(1)

Y = 54% less than Z

Y = Z - (54⁄100)Z

Y = ((100Z-54Z)⁄100)

Y = (46Z⁄100)

Z = (100⁄46)Y--->(2)

From (1) and (2)

2X = (100⁄46)Y

Y⁄X = (46×2)⁄100

Y⁄X = 92⁄100

Y = X×(100-8)⁄100

Y = X-0.08X

Y = X - 8% of X

X = 80% of Y
