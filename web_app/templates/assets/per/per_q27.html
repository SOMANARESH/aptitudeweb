If 70% of the students in a school are boys and the number of girls be 504, the number of boys is:

A.  1176

B.  1008

C.  1208

D.  3024


Girls = 30% of students = 504

(30⁄100)×Total Students = 504

Total Students = 504×100⁄36

Total Students = 50400⁄36

Total Students = 1680

Boys = 70% of students = (70⁄100)×Total Students

Boys = (70⁄100)×1680

Boys = (70×1680)⁄100

Boys = 117600⁄100

Boys = 1176
