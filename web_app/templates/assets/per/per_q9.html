Question :9

38 litres of milk was poured into a tub and the tub was found to be 5% empty. To completely fill the tub, what amount of additional milk must be poured:

A.  1 liter

B.  2 liters

C.  3 liters

D.   4 liters


Given that the tub was filled by 38 litres of milk that results 5 % empty

i.e. 95% of tub was filled by 38 litres of milk

95% = 38

1% = 38⁄95

1% = 0.4litres

So to fill the tub we require 5% of milk

i.e. 5% of milk = 5×0.4

5% of milk = 2litres.
