Question :96

A man spends 10% of his income on food and 80%, of the remaining income on clothing. If he still has a balance of 180, what is his total income?

A.  2000

B.  1800

C.  1000

D.  900


Let the total income be X

Given that a man spends 10% of income on food. So he has 90% remaining

Money spend on food = 10% of X

Money spend on food = (10⁄100)X

Remaining amount = 90% of income

Remaining amount = 90% of X

Remaining amount = (90⁄100)X

Given that he spend 80% of remaining amount on clothing

Money spend on clothing

= 80% of remaining amount

= 80% (90⁄100)X

= (80⁄100)×(90⁄100)X

= ((80×90)⁄(100×100))X

= (7200⁄10000)X

Money spend on clothing = (72⁄100)X

Now total remaining amount = X - (money on food+money on clothing)

Given that remaining amount = 180

X - (10⁄100)X - (72⁄100)X = 180

(100X-10X-72X)⁄100 = 180

18X⁄100 = 180

X = 180×(100⁄18)

X = 10×100

X = 1000
