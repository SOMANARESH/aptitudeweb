Question :72

Two numbers are respectively 25% and 20% less than a third number. What percent is the first number of the second?

A.  5%

B.  75%

C.  80%

D.  93.75%

Answer: D

Let the numbers be X , Y and Z

Given that

X = 25% less than Z and Y = 20% less than Z

X = 25% less than Z

X = Z - (25⁄100)Z

X = ((100Z-25Z)⁄100)

X = (75Z⁄100)

X = (3Z⁄4)

Z = (4⁄3)X--->(1)

Y = 20% less than Z

Y = Z - (20⁄100)Z

Y = ((100Z-20Z)⁄100)

Y = (80Z⁄100)

Y = (4Z⁄5)

Z = (5⁄4)Y--->(2)

From (1) and (2)

(4⁄3)X = (5⁄4)Y

X⁄Y = (5×3)⁄(4×4)

X⁄Y = 15⁄16

X = Y×15⁄16

X = 0.9375Y

X = 93.75% of Y
