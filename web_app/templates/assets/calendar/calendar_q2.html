Question 2 :

If the first day(today) of the Week is Monday, what will be the day on 99th day after today

A.Tuesday

B.Monday

C.Saturday

D.Sunday

Answer: A

Solution:

  • Given that Monday is the starting day of the week.

  • So the numbering of days is as follows
      0 → Monday
      1 → Tuesday
      2 → Wednesday
      3 → Thursday
      4 → Friday
      5 → Saturday
      6 → Sunday

  • Given that total number of days = 99

  • So Total number of odd days = total days ⁄ 7

  • Total odd days = 99 % 7

  • Total odd days = 1

  • So 99th day is Tuesday
