Question 3 :

The last day of the century should not be

A.Friday

B.Monday

C.Wednesday

D.Tuesday


Solution:

  • Last day of the century depends on the number of odd days in that year.

  • The numbering of days is as follows
      0 → Sunday
      1 → Monday
      2 → Tuesday
      3 → Wednesday
      4 → Thursday
      5 → Friday
      6 → Saturday

  • As per the tutorial, total number of odd days for 100 years = 5

  • Odd day 5 = Friday

  • Total number of odd days for 200 years = 3

  • Odd day 3 = Wednesday

  • Total number of odd days for 300 years = 1

  • Odd day 1 = Monday

  • Total number of odd days for 400 years = 0

  • Odd day 0 = Sunday

  • So the last of the century cannot be Tuesday,Thursday,Saturday
