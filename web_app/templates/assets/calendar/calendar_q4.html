Question 4 :

What will be the day of the week 15th August, 2010?

A.Tuesday

B.Monday

C.Saturday

D.Sunday


Solution:

  • Divide the given date into parts like below

  • 15th August, 2010 = (2009 years + Period 1.1.2010 to 15.8.2010)

  • Divide the 2009 years into parts of years where odd days to be zero like below

  • 2009 = 1600 + 400 + 9

  • For 1600 years, number of odd days = 0

  • For 400 years, number of odd days = 0

  • 9 years = (2 leap years + 7 ordinary years)

  • For Ordinary Year,number of odd days = 1

  • For Leap Year,number of odd days = 2

  • Total odd days = [(2×2) + (7×1)] = 11 odd days = 4 odd days(Remainder of (11⁄7))

  • Let us calculate total number of days from 1.1.2010 to 15.8.2010

  • Since 2010 is not a leap year,there should be 28 days in February

  • Number of days by month as follows

      • January → 31
      February → 28
      March → 31
      April → 30
      May → 31
      June → 30
      July → 31
      August → 15 (Up to given date)

  • Total number of days = 31 + 28 + 31 + 30 + 31 + 30 + 31 + 15 = 227

  • Total number of odd days = Remainder(227⁄7) = 3 days

  • So total number of odd days for the given date 15.8.2010 = 4 + 3 = 7

  • Total odd days = Remainder(7⁄7) = 0

  • As per the number of days like below

      0 → Sunday
      1 → Monday
      2 → Tuesday
      3 → Wednesday
      4 → Thursday
      5 → Friday
      6 → Saturday

  • The given date is Sunday
