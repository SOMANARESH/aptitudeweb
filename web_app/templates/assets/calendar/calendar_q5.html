Question 5 :

What was the day of the week on 17th June, 1998?

A.Monday

B.Tuesday

C.Wednesday

D.Thursday

Answer: C

Solution:

  • Divide the given date into parts like below

  • 17th June, 1998 = (1997 years + Period from 1.1.1998 to 17.6.1998)

  • Divide the 1997 years into parts of years where odd days to be zero like below

  • 1997 = 1600 + 300 + 97

  • For 1600 years, number of odd days = 0

  • For 300 years, number of odd days = (3 × 5) = 15 = 1 odd day

  • 97 years has 24 leap years + 73 ordinary years.

  • 97 years = (24 leap years + 73 ordinary years) = (24×2 + 73×1) = 48 + 73 = 121 = 2 odd days

    • Note:

    • For Ordinary Year,number of odd days = 1

    • For Leap Year,number of odd days = 2

  • Let us calculate total number of days from 1.1.1998 to 17.6.1998

  • Since 1998 is not a leap year,February has 28 days.

  • Number of days by month as follows

      • January → 31
      February → 28
      March → 31
      April → 30
      May → 31
      June → 17 (Up to given date)

  • Total number of days = 31 + 28 + 31 + 30 + 31 + 17 = 168

  • Total number of odd days = Remainder(168⁄7) = 0 days

  • So total number of odd days for the given date 17.6.1998 = 0 + 1 + 2 + 0 = 3 odd days

  • Total odd days = Remainder(3⁄7) = 3

  • As per the numbering of days like below

      0 → Sunday
      1 → Monday
      2 → Tuesday
      3 → Wednesday
      4 → Thursday
      5 → Friday
      6 → Saturday

  • The given date is on Wednesday
