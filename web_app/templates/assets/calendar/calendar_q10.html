Question 10 :

How many times does the 29th days of the month occur in 400 consecutive years?
A.97 times

B.4800 times

C.4497 times

D.4400 times

Answer: C

Solution:

  • Given that there are 400 consecutive years.
  • In 400 years, there are 400%4 = 97 leap years and remaining normal years.
  • Total number of times that 29th day occur in (400-97) Normal years = 303 ×11 = 3333
Note:
-Each Normal year contains 11 months having 29th day (February contains 28 days)
-Each leap year contains 12 months having 29th Day (February contains 29 days)
  • Total number of times that 29th day occur in 97 leap years = 97 ×12 = 1164
  • Total number of times that 400 years having 29th day in a month = 3333 + 1164 = 4497
