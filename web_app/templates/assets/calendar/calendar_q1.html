Question 1 :

How many odd days will return for the total of 199 days

A.2

B.3

C.4

D.5


Solution:

We know that odd days will be obtained by dividing the total number of days with number of days in a week(7)

So total odd days = total days % 7

Total odd days = 199 % 7

Total odd days = 3
