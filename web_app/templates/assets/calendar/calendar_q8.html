Question 8 :

On what dates of April, 2001 did Wednesday fall?

A.1st, 8th, 15th, 22nd, 29th

B.2nd, 9th, 16th, 23rd, 30th

C.4th, 11th, 18th, 25th

D.3rd, 10th, 17th, 24th


Solution:

First we have to find the day on 1st April, 2001.

1st April, 2001 = (2000 years + Period from 1.1.2001 to 1.4.2001)

Odd days in 2000 years = 0

Note:

     2000 year is perfectly divisible by both 4 and 400

Number of days in Each month

    January → 31
    February → 28
    March → 31
    April → 1

Total number of odd days = (0 + 0 + 0) = 0

On 1st April, 2001 it was Sunday.(As per the numbering of days)

In April, 2001 Wednesday falls on 4th, 11th, 18th and 25th.
