The sum of all natural numbers between 100 and 200, which are multiples of 3 is:

A.  5000

B.  4950

C.  4980

D.  4900


The number which are multiples of 3 between 100 and 200 are

102,105,108,....195,198

Total number of terms between 100 and 200 are 33

Sum of numbers = 102+105+108+....+195+198

= 102+(102+3)+(102+6)+(102+9)+....

....(102+93)+(102+96)

= (102×33)+(3+6+9+....93+96)

= 3366+3(1+2+3+..31+32)

{ Sum of first n terms is n(n+1)⁄2 }

= 3366+3(32×33/2)

= 3366+3(1056/2)

= 3366+3(528)

= 3366+1584

= 4950
