The average of 21 consecutive odd numbers is n. if next two numbers are also included, the average of all the 23 numbers will be:

A.  23

B.  n-1

C.  n+1

D.  n+2

Answer: D

Let 2m+1,2m+3,2m+5....2m+41 be the 21 consecutive odd numbers.

Sum of these 21 consecutive odd numbers is

= 2m+1+2m+3+2m+5....+2m+41

= 21(2m)+(1+3+5+7+....41)

{ Let a,a+d,a+2d,a+3d....a+(n-1)d be numbers with common differense of d and first number is a then Sum of these n numbers is given by

(n⁄2)×(2a+(n-1)d) }

= 42m+[(21⁄2)×(2(1)+(21-1)2)]

= 42m+[(21⁄2)×42]

= 42m+441

Average of 21 consecutive odd numbers is n

i.e. (Sum of these 21 consecutive odd numbers)⁄21 = n

Sum of these 21 consecutive odd numbers = 21n

42m+441 = 21n

2m+21 = n

2m = n-21

m = (n-21)/2

The next two numbers will be 2m+43, 2m+45

Average of 23 consecutive odd numbers is

= (sum of 23 consecutive odd numbers)⁄23

= (42m+441+4m+88)⁄23

= (46m+529)⁄23

= (46m+529)⁄23

= (46((n-21)/2)+529)⁄23

= (23(n-21)+529)⁄23

= (23n-483+529)⁄23

= (23n+46)⁄23

= n+2
