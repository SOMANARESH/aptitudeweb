Question :17

Sum of three consecutive even numbers is 32 more than the average of the numbers. Find the middle number in the series.

A.  14

B.  16

C.  18

D.  20

Answer: B

Let the three consecutive even numbers be 2n,2n+2,2n+4

Given that sum of these numbers is 32 more than the average of these numbers.

Sum of numbers = 2n+2n+2+2n+4

Sum of numbers = 6n+6

Average of numbers is = Sum of numbers⁄3

= (6n+6)⁄3

= 2n+2

Based on given condition

i.e.Sum of numbers = 32+Average of numbers

6n+6 = 32+2n+2

6n+6 = 2n+34

6n-2n = 34-6

4n = 28

n = 48⁄4

n = 7

So middle number in series is 2n+2

= 2(7)+2

= 14+2

= 16
