If the average of x and 1⁄x is M, then the average of x2 and 1⁄x2 is:

A.  1-M2

B.  1-2M

C.  2M2-1

D.  2M2 + 1

Answer: C

Given average of x,1⁄x is M

i.e.(x+1⁄x)⁄2 = M

So x+1⁄x = 2×M

Squaring on both sides we get

(x+1⁄x)2 = (2M)2

(x2)+(1⁄x2)+(2.x.1⁄x) = 4M2

(x2)+(1⁄x2) = 4M2-2

Now we want average of x2 and 1⁄x2

So Average of x2 and 1⁄x2 is given by

(x2 +1⁄x2 ) ⁄2 = (4M2-2)⁄2

(x2 +1⁄x2 ) ⁄2 = 2M2-1

Average of x2 and 1⁄x2 is 2M2-1
