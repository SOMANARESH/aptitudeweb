The average of five consecutive even numbers A, B, C, D and E is 42. What will be the product of B and D?

A.  1722

B.  1760

C.  1764

D.  None of these


Given A,B,C,D and E are 5 consecutive even numbers

Let us assume that the five consecutive even numbers be 2n,2n+2,2n+4,2n+6,2n+8

So A = 2n, B = 2n+2, C = 2n+4 D = 2n+6, E = 2n+8.

Given that the average of these 5 numbers is 42

i.e.(Sum of 5 consecutive even numbers)⁄5 = 105

(2n+2n+2+2n+4+2n+6+2n+8)⁄5 = 42

10n+20 = 5×42

10n = 210-20

n = 190⁄10

n = 19

Product of B and D is

= (2n+2)(2n+6)

= (38+2)(38+6)

= 40×44

= 1760
