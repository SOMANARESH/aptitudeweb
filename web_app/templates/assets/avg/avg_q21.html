The average of five consecutive numbers A, B, C, D and E is 105. What is the product of B and E?

A.  11025

B.  12024

C.  11128

D.  Cant say

Answer: C

Given A,B,C,D and E are 5 consecutive numbers

Let us assume that the five consecutive numbers be n,n+1,n+2,n+3,n+4

So A = n, B = n+1, C = n+2 D = n+3, E = n+4.

Given that the average of these 5 numbers is 105

i.e.(Sum of 5 consecutive numbers)⁄5 = 105

(n+n+1+n+2+n+3+n+4)⁄5 = 105

5n+10 = 5×105

5n = 525-10

n = 515⁄5

n = 103

So product of B and E is

= (n+1)(n+4)

= (104)(107)

= 11128
