Question :60

The average age of 4 brothers is 6 years. If age of their father is included, the average is increased by 5 years. Find the age of the father.

A.  24

B.  31

C.  35

D.  38


Given that the average of 4 brothers is 6

(Sum of ages of four brothers)⁄4 = 6

Sum of ages of four brothers = 4×6

Sum of ages of four brothers = 24

Let us assume that the fathers age be X

If father is also included then the average age of four brothers and father is increased by 5

(Sum of ages of four brothers and age of father) ÷5

= 6+5 = 11

24+X = 5×11

24+X = 55

X = 55-24

X = 31
