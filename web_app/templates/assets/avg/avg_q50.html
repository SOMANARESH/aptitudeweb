Question :50

The average of 4 numbers is 67.5. the highest and the smallest numbers are 108 and 23 respectively. If difference between other two numbers is 31, find the bigger number of the two.

A.  139

B.  108

C.  85

D.  54


Sum of 4 numbers s= 4×67.5 = 270

Sum of two given numbers = 108+23

= 131

sum of remaining two numbers = 270-131

=139

Difference of the two numbers = 31

The bigger number = 139+31⁄2

= 85.
