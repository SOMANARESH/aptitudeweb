Question :26

Of the three numbers, the first is twice the second and the second is 3 times the third. If their average is 100, the largest of the three number is:

A.  120

B.  150

C.  180

D.  300


Let us assume that third number is X3=n

From given condition second and first numbers can be written as

X2 = 3(X3)

X2 = 3n

X1 = 2(X2)

X1 = 2(3n) = 6n

The average of these numbers is 100

i.e. (sum of the three numbers)⁄3 = 100

X1+X2+X3 = 3×100

6n+3n+n = 300

10n = 300

n = 300⁄10

n = 30

So largest of three numbers is

= 6n

= 6(30)

= 180
