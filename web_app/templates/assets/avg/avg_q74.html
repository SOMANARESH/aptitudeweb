Question :74

A cricketer player after playing 10 tests scored 100 runs in the 11th test. As a result, the average of this runs is increased by 5. The present average of runs is:

A.  45

B.  40

C.  55

D.  50


Let the average score by 10 innings be X

Then (Sum of 10 innings score)⁄10 = X

Sum of 10 innings score = 10X

Given that 11th innings score = 100

By adding of 11th innings the average increases by 5

New average for 11 innings = X+5

(Sum of 11 innings score)⁄11 = X+5

Sum of 11 innings score = 11(X+5)

Sum of 10 innings score+11th inings score = 11X+55

10X+100 = 11X+55

100-55 = 11X-10X

X = 45

Average score after 11 innings is X+5

= 45+5

= 50
