The average weight of A, B, C is 45 kg. if the average weight of A and B be 40 kg and that of B and C 43 kg, then the weight of B is:

A.  17kg

B.  20 kg

C.  26 kg

D.  31 kg

Answer: D

Given that the average weight of A and B is 40

(Sum of weights of A and B)⁄2 = 40

A+B = 2×40 = 80 Kg

The average weight of B and C is 43

(Sum of weights of B and C)⁄2 = 43

B+C= 2×43 = 86Kg

A+B+C= 3×45= 135

80+C = 135

C = 135-80 = 55

We know that

B+C = 86

B+55 = 86

B = 86-55

B = 31
