Question :15

The sum of three consecutive odd natural numbers is 87. The smallest of these numbers is:

A.  29

B.  31

C.  23

D.  27


Let the three consecutive odd numbers be 2n-1,2n+1,2n+3

Given Sum of three consecutive odd numbers is 87

2n-1+2n+1+2n+3 = 87

6n+3 = 87

6n = 87-3

6n = 84

n = 84⁄6

n = 14

So smallest of 3 numbers is 2n-1

= 2(14)-1

= 28-1

= 27
