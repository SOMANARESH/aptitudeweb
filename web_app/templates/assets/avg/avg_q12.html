Question :12

The sum of three consecutive odd natural numbers is 147. Then, the middle number is:

A.  47

B.  48

C.  49

D.  51

Answer: C

Let the three consecutive odd numbers be 2n-1,2n+1,2n+3

Given Sum of three consecutive odd numbers is 147

2n-1+2n+1+2n+3 = 147

6n+3 = 147

6n = 147-3

6n = 144

n = 144⁄6

n = 24

So middle term is given by 2n+1

= 2(24)+1

= 48+1

= 49
