Question :72

A batsman in his 17th innings make a score of 85, and there by increased his average by 3, what is his average after 17 innings?

A.  32

B.  34

C.  37

D.  None of these


Let the average score by 16 innings be X

Then (Sum of 16 innings score)⁄16 = X

Sum of 16 innings score = 16X

Given that 17th innings score = 85

By adding of 17th innings the average increases by 3

New average for 17 innings = X+3

(Sum of 17 innings score)⁄17 = X+3

Sum of 17 innings score = 17(X+3)

Sum of 16 innings score+17th inings score = 17X+51

16X+85 = 17X+51

85-51 = 17X-16X

X = 34

Average score after 17 innings is X+3

= 34+3

= 37
