The average of runs of a cricketer player of 10 innings was 32. How many runs must be make in his next innings so as to increase his average of runs by 4?

A.  76

B.  70

C.  4

D.  2

Answer: A

Given that the average score for 10 innings is 32

(Total score for 10 innings)⁄10 = 32

Total runs for 10 innings = 10×32

Total runs for 10 innings = 320

Let us assume that X be the runs scored by 11th innings

Given that If the next innings score is added his average runs increased by 4

i.e. (total score by 11 innings)⁄11 = 32+4

total score by 10 innings+11th innings score

= 11×36

320+X = 396

X = 396-320

X = 76
