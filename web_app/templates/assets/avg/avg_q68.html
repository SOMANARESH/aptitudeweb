The average of runs of a cricket player in 15 matches is 33. If the average of the first 10 matches is 45, then the average of the last 5 matches is:

A.  15

B.  23

C.  9

D.  13.5


Given that the average runs by 10 matches is 45

(Sum of runs by 10 matches)⁄10 = 45

Sum of runs by 10 matches = 450

Now the average runs by 15 matches = 33

(Sum of runs by 15 matches)⁄15 = 33

Sum of runs by 10 matches+Last 5 match runs = 15×33

450+Last 5 match runs = 495

Last 5 match runs = 45

Average runs by 5 matches

= (Sum of runs by last 5 matches)⁄5

= 45⁄5

= 9
