The average age of A, B and C is 26 years. If the average age of A and C is 29 years, what is the age of B in years?

A.  26

B.  20

C.  29

D.  23

Answer: B

Given that the average of A,B and C is 26

(Sum of ages of A,B and C)⁄3 = 26

Sum of ages of A,B and C = 3×26

A+B+C = 78 years--->(1)

(Sum of ages of A and C)⁄2 = 29

Sum of ages of A and C = 2×29

A + C = 58 years--->(2)

Now let us calculate (1)-(2)

(A+B+C) − (A+C)

= 78-58

= 20 years.
