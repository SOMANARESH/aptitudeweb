Question :19

A sum of Rs. 700 has to be used to give seven cash prizes to the students of a school for their overall academic performance. If each prize is Rs.20 less than its preceding prize, what is the least value of the prize?

A.  30

B.  40

C.  60

D.  80


Let us assume that amount of first prize be N

Based on condition that is amount of next prize is Rs.20 less by first prize

Then Amount of 7 prizes can be written as

N, N-20, N-40, N-60, N-80, N-100, N-120.

So total sum of prizes is 700

i.e N+N-20+N-40+N-60+N-80+N-100+N-120 = 700

7N-420 = 700

7N = 700+420

7N = 1120

N = 1120⁄7

N = 160

Least prize is given by N-120

= 160-120

= 40
