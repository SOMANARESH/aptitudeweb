Question :65

The average of 9 items is 79. If one new item whose value is 59 is also included in the series, find average of all the 10 items.

A.  77

B.  80

C.  81

D.  83

Answer: A

Given that Average of 9items = 79

(Sum of 9 items)⁄9 = 79

Sum of 9 items = 9×79 = 711

Given that a new item with 59 is also included to group

New average of 10 items is

= (Sum of 10 items)⁄10

= (Sum of 9 items+10th value)⁄10

= (711+59)⁄10

= 770⁄10

= 77
