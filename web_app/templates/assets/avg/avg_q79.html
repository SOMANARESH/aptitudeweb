Question :79

The average of 100 members is 44. The average of these 100 numbers and 4 other numbers is 50. The average of 4 new numbers will be:

A.  800

B.  200

C.  176

D.  24


Given that the average of 100 numbers is 44

(Sum of 100 numbers)⁄100 = 44

Sum of 100 numbers = 100×44

Sum of 100 numbers = 4400

Given that the average of 100 and further 4 numbers is 50

(Sum of 104 numbers)⁄104 = 50

Sum of 104 numbers = 104×50

Sum of 104 numbers = 5200

Sum of 100 numbers + Sum of 4 numbers = 5200

4400+Sum of 4 numbers = 5200

Sum of 4 numbers = 5200-4400

Sum of 4 numbers = 800

Average of 4 numbers is

= (Sum of 4 numbers)⁄4

= 800⁄4

= 200
