Question :29

Of the three numbers whose average is 60, the first is one fourth of the sum of the others. the first number is:

A.  30

B.  36

C.  42

D.  45


Let us assume that three numbers be a,b and c

Given that first number is one fourth of sum of other two numbers

i.e. a = (b+c)⁄4

b+c = 4a

The average of three numbers is 60

(Sum of three numbers)⁄3 = 60

a+b+c = 3×60

a+4a = 180

5a = 180

a = 180⁄5

a = 36
