Question :33

Average of first seven multiples of 7 is:

A.  7

B.  14

C.  28

D.  35


Sum of first 7 multiples of 7

= (7×1)+(7×2)+‐‐‐‐‐+(7×7)

= 7×(1+2+‐‐‐‐‐+7)

{Sum of first n numbers is given by (n)(n+1)⁄2}

= 7×7×8⁄2

= 7×56 ⁄2

= 7×28

= 196

Average of first 7 multiples of 7

(Sum of first 7 multiples of 7)⁄7

= 196 ⁄ 7

= 28.
