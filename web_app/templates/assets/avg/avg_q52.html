The average marks obtained by ten students is 45 and that of eleven students is 48. How many marks did the eleventh student obtain?

A.  76

B.  78

C.  80

D.  86

Answer: B

Given that the average marks by 10 students is 45

(Sum of marks by 10 students)⁄10 = 45

Sum of marks by 10 students = 10×45

Sum of marks by 10 students = 450

Given that the average marks by 11 students is 48

(Sum of Sum of marks by 11 students )⁄11 = 48

Sum of marks by 11 students =11×48

Sum of marks by 10 students + 11th student marks

= 528

450+11th student marks = 528

11th student Marks = 528-450

11th student Marks = 78
