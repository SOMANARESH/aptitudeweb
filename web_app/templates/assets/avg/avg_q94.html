The average marks obtained by 9 students was calculated to be 65. Later on it was found that the marks of one student was wrongly read as 76 instead of 67. The correct average is:

A.  56

B.  64

C.  66

D.  74

Answer: B

Given that average marks by 10 students is 65

(Sum of 9 students)⁄9 = 65

Sum of marks by 8 students+wrongly readed marks= 9×65

Sum of 8 students+76 = 585

Sum of 8 students = 585-76 = 509

Now average marks by 9 students by replacing 76 by 67 is

= (Sum of marks by 9 students)⁄9

= (Sum of marks by 8 students+Original marks)⁄9

= (509+67)⁄9

= 576⁄9

= 64
