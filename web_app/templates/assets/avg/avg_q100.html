Average age of 7 members of a family is 29 years. If present age of the youngest member is 5 years, find average age of the remaining members at the time of birth of the youngest member.

A.  22 years

B.  24 years

C.  26 years

D.  28 years


Given Average of 7 members is 29

(Sum of 7 members)⁄7 = 29

(Sum of younger age+ Sum of remaining members age)⁄7 = 29

Sum of younger age + Sum of remaining members age = 7×29

Given that Younger age = 5

5+Sum of ages of 6 member = 203

Sum of ages of 6 members = 203-5 = 198

The average age of 6 members before 5 years is

= (Sum of ages of 6 members before 5 years)⁄6

= (Sum of present ages of 6 members-(6×5))⁄6

= [198-(6×5)]⁄6

= (198-30)⁄6

= 168⁄6

= 28
