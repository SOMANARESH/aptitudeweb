Question :37

Average of n observations is 35, average of n other observations is 37 and average of remaining n observations is 42. Average of all the observations is:

A.  36

B.  38

C.  40

D.  57

Answer: B

Given that the average of first n observations is 35

(sum of first n observations)⁄n = 35

Sum of first n observations = 35n

Given that the average of another group of n observations is 37

(sum of other n observations)⁄n = 37

sum of other n observations = 37n

And also Given that the average of remaining n observations is 42

(sum of remaining n observations)⁄n = 42

Sum of remaining n observations = 42n

Now the average of all numbers is

= (sum of all observations)⁄(Total number of observations)

= (35n+37n+42n)⁄(n+n+n)

= 114n⁄3n

= 38
