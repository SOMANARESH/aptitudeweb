The average of n numbers is x. when 36 is subtracted from two of the numbers, the new average becomes (x-8). The value of n is:

A.  6

B.  8

C.  9

D.  72


Given that average of n numbers is x

(Sum of n numbers)⁄n = x

Sum of n numbers = xn

When two numbers are subtracted by 36

New average = x-8

(Sum of n numbers-36-36)⁄n = x-8

xn-72 = n(x-8)

xn-72 = xn-8n

8n = 72

n = 9
