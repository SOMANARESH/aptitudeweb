The average monthly income of X and Y is Rs.5050. the average monthly income of Y and Z is Rs.6250 and the average monthly income of X and Z is Rs.5200. the monthly income of X is:

A.  4050

B.  3050

C.  4000

D.  5000

Answer: c

Given that the average income of X and Y is 5050

(Sum of incomes of X and Y)⁄2 = 5050

X+Y = 2×5050

X+Y = 10100--->(1)

The average income of Y and Z is 6250

(Sum of incomes of Y and Z)⁄2 = 5050

Y+Z = 2×6250

Y+Z = 12500--->(2)

The average income of X and Z is 5200

(Sum of incomes of X and Z)⁄2 = 5200

X+Z = 2×5200

X+Z = 10400--->(3)

Now Let us calculate (3)-(2)

(X+Z)-(Y+Z)

= 10400-12500

X-Y = -2100--->(4)

Now let us calculate (1)+(4)

(X+Y)+(X-Y)

= 10100-2100

2X = 8000

X = 4000
