Question :61

Average age of 6 persons is decreased by 1 years when one new person Is included in the group find the age of new man, if average age of 6 persons as 39 years.

A.  32

B.  33

C.  38

D.  40

Answer: A

Given that the average of 6 persons is 39

(Sum of ages of 6 persons)⁄6 = 39

Sum of ages of 6 persons 6×39 = 234

The average age decreased by 1 when new person included in the group

i.e.(Sum of ages of 6 persons+new persons age)⁄7 = 39-1

234+new persons age = 7×38

234+new persons age = 266

new persons age = 266-234

new persons age = 32
