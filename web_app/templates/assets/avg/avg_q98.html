Question :98

Five numbers are arranged in ascending order. If average of first two numbers is 8 less than the average of last two numbers and sum of last two numbers is 56, what is the sum of the first two numbers?

A.  40

B.  28

C.  20

D.  None of these

Answer: A

Let five numbers be a,b,c,d and e in ascending order

Given that Average of a,b is 8 less than that of average of d and e

Let Sum of a and b be X

Average of a and b is

= (Sum of a and b)⁄2

= X⁄2

Given that Sum of d and e is 56

Average of d and e is

= (Sum of d and e)⁄2

= 56⁄2

= 28

Based on condition that Average of a and b = (average of d and e) - 8

X⁄2 = 28-8

X⁄2 = 20

X = 2×20

X = 40
