Question :84

Average age of 6 persons in a group is same as it was 5 years ago, when an old man in the group is replaced by a young man, what is the age of the new person if the man replaced is of 70 years.

A.  30

B.  35

C.  40

D.  45


Let X1,...X6 be the present ages then Average of their ages before 5 years is P(Assume)

(Sum of their ages before 5 years)×6 = P

X1-5+X2-5+...+X6-5 = 6P

X1+X2+...+X6 = 6P+30

Given that old man of 70 years age in a group is replaced by young man(Y)

Sum of their ages =X1+X2+...+X6-70+Y

So now Average of their ages is as that of their ages before replacement(P)

(Sum of their ages)×6 =P

X1+X2+...+X6-70+Y = 6P

6P+30-70+Y = 6P

Y = 40
