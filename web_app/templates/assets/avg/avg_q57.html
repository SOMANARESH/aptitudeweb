Question :57

Four years ago the average age of A, B, and C was 25 years. Five years ago the average age of B and C was 20 years. As present age is:

A.  60

B.  62

C.  15

D.  37

Answer: D

Let A,B and C represents present ages

Then Their ages before 4 years should be A-4,B-4 and C-4

Their average before 4 years is 25

(Sum of their ages before 4 years)⁄3 = 25

A-4+B-4+C-4 = 3×25

A+B+C-12 = 75

A+B+C = 75+12

A+B+C = 87--->(1)

The ages of B and C before 5 years are B-5 and C-5

Average of their ages before 5 years is 20

(Sum of their ages before 5 years)⁄2 = 20

B-5+C-5 = 2×20

B+C-10 = 40

B+C = 50

From (1)

A+50 = 87

A = 87-50

A = 37
