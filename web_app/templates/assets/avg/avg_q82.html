Question :82

The average wight of 12 persons is 50 kg. on replacing a man whose weight is 53 kg with a new man, new average becomes 49kg. the weight of the new man is:

A.  41

B.  46

C.  54

D.  65


Given average weight of 12 persons is 50

(Sum of weight of 12 persons)⁄12 = 50

Sum of weight of 12 persons = 12 ×50

Sum of weight of 12 persons = 600

Given that one persons with weight of 53kg is replaced by new persons weight of Y(Assume)

Now Sum of 12 persons = 600-53+Y

Sum of 12 persons = 547+Y

Average weight of 12 persons after replacing = 49

(Sum of weight of 12 persons after replacing)⁄12 = 49

Sum of weight of 12 persons after replacing = 12×49

547+Y = 588

Y = 588-547

Y = 41
