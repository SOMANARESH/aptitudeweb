The average age of 8 persons is increased by 2 years, when one of them, whose age is 24 years, is replaced by a new person. The age of the new person is:

A.  42

B.  40

C.  38

D.  45


Let the average weight of 8 persons be X

(Sum of weights of 8 persons)⁄8 = X

Sum of weights of 8 persons = 8X

Given that one persons with weight of 24 is replaced by new personsweight of Y(Assume)

Then average weight increased by 2

Total sum of 8 persons = 8X-24+Y

Average weight of 8 persons including new persons weight is X+2

(Sum of weights of 8 persons including new persons weight)÷8= X+2

Sum of weights of 8 persons including new persons weight = 8(X+2)

8X-24+Y = 8X+16

Y = 16+24

Y = 40
