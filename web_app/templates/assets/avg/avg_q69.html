Question :69

The average of 50 numbers is 38. If two numbers namely 45 and 55 are discarded, the average of the remaining numbers is :

A.  36

B.  35

C.  32.5

D.  37.5


Given that the average of 50 numbers is 45

(Sum of 50 numbers)⁄50 = 38

Sum of 50 numbers = 50 ×38

Sum of 50 numbers = 1900

If two numbers namely 45 and 55 are eliminated then tota sum becomes

= 1900-45-55

= 1800

Sum of 48 numbers = 1800

Now the average of 48 numbers is

= (Sum of 48 numbers)⁄48

= 1800⁄48

= 37.5
