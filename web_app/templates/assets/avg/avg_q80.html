Average weight of 25 persons is increased by 1 by kg when one man weighting 60 kg is replaced by a new person. Weight of new person is:

A.  50

B.  61

C.  86

D.  85

Answer: D

Let the average weight of 25 persons be X

(Sum of weights of 25 persons)⁄25 = X

Sum of weights of 25 persons = 25X

Given that one persons with weight of 60 is replaced by new personsweight of Y(Assume)

Then average weight increased by 1

Total sum of 25 persons = 25X-60+Y

Average weight of 25 persons including new persons weight is X+1

(Sum of weights of 25 persons including new persons weight)÷25= X+1

Sum of weights of 25 persons including new persons weight = 25(X+1)

25X-60+Y = 25X+25

Y = 25+60

Y = 85

New persons weight = 85
