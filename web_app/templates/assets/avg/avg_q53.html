Question :53

The average of 11 numbers is 70 and that of first 10 members is 69. The 11th number is:

A.  59

B.  79

C.  80

D.  81


Given that the average of 11 numbers is 70

(Sum of 11 numbers)⁄11 = 70

Sum of 11 numbers = 11×70

Sum of 11 numbers = 770--->(1)

Given that the average of first 10 numbers is 69

(Sum of first 10 numbers)⁄10 = 69

Sum of first 10 numbers =10×69

Sum of first 10 numbers = 690--->(2)

From (1)

Sum of 11 numbers = 770

Sum of first 10 numbers + 11th number = 770

From (2)

690+11th number = 770

11th number = 770-690

11th number = 80
