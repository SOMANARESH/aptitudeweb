The average age of 20 boys in a class is 12 years. What will be average age of the class if 5 new boys take admission whose average age is 7 years?

A.  11 1⁄2 years

B.  11 years

C.  101⁄2 years

D.  10


Given that Average age of 20 boys = 12

(Sum of ages of 20 boys)⁄20 = 12

Sum ages of 20 boys = 20×12 = 240

Given that 5 boys whose average age of 7 also included then total boys in class is 25

Avarage age of 5 boys is 7

(Sum of ages of 5 boys)⁄5 = 7

Sum of ages of 5 boys = 5×7 = 35

Now the average age of 25 boys is

= (Sum of ages of 25 boys)⁄25

= (Sum of ages of 20 boys+Sum of ages of 5 new boys)⁄25

= (240+35)⁄25

= 275⁄25

= 11
