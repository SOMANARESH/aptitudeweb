Question :18

if the sum of three consecutive numbers is more than the middle by 150, then the middle number is:

A.  50

B.  60

C.  75

D.  None of these


Let us assume that the three consecutive numbers be n,n+1,n+2

Sum of these numbers is

n+n+1+n+2 = 3n+3

Given that Sum of these numbers is more than the mmiddle term by 150

i.e. Sum of numbers = middle term+150

3n+3 = n+1+150

3n-n = 151-3

2n = 148

n = 148⁄2

n = 74

So middle term is

n+1 = 74+1 = 75
