Average weight of 30 students in a class is increased by 200 grams when one students whose weight is 20 kg. is replaced by a new student. Find the weight of the new student admitted.

A.  10

B.  25

C.  26

D.  30

Answer: C

Let average weight of 30 students is X

(Sum of weight of 30 students)⁄30 = X

Sum of weight of 30 students = 30X

Given that one students with weight of 20kg is replaced by new students weight of Y(Assume)

Now Sum of 30 students = 30X-20+Y

Average weight of 30 students after replacing is increased by 200grms = 200+0.2kg

(Sum of weight of 30 students after replacing)⁄30 = 200.2

Sum of weight of 30 students after replacing = 30(X+0.2)

30X-20+Y = 30X+6

Y = 6+20

Y = 26
