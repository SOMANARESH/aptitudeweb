If A,B,C and D are four consecutive odd numbers and their average is 52, what is the product of B and D?

A.  2756

B.  2805

C.  2703

D.  Data insufficient

Answer: B

Given A,B,C and D are 4 consecutive odd numbers

Let us assume that the four consecutive odd numbers be

2n-1,2n+1,2n+3,2n+5

So A = 2n-1, B = 2n+1, C = 2n+3 D = 2n+5

Given that the average of these 4 numbers is 52

i.e.(Sum of 4 consecutive odd numbers)⁄4 = 52

(2n-1+2n+1+2n+3+2n+5)⁄4 = 52

8n+8 = 4×52

8n = 208-8

n = 200⁄8

n = 25

Product of B and D is

= (2n+1)(2n+5)

= (50+1)(50+5)

= 51×55

= 2805
