Of the three numbers, second is twice the first and is also thrice the third. If the average of three numbers is 44, the largest number is:

A.  24

B.  72

C.  36

D.  108

i.e.X2 = 2(X1)

X2 = 3(X3)

So 3(X3) = 2(X1)

X1 = 3(X3)⁄2

Then average of these numbers is 44

(sum of the three numbers)⁄3 = 44

X1+X2+X3 = 3×44

(3(X3)⁄2)+3X 3+X3 = 132

X3 = 24
