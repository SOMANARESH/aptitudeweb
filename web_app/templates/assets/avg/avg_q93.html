The arithmetic mean of 10 numbers was computed as 7.6. it was later discovered that a number8 was wrongly read as 3 during the computation. What should be the correct mean?

A.  7.1

B.  7.6

C.  8.1

D.  8.6


Given that average of 10 numbers is 7.6

(Sum of 10 numbers)⁄10 = 7.6

Sum of 9numbers+wrongly number= 10×7.6

Sum of 9 numbers+3 = 76

Sum of 9 numbers = 76-3 = 73

Now average of 10 numbers by replacing 3 by 8 is

= (Sum of 10 numbers)⁄10

= (Sum of 9 numbers+Original number)⁄10

= (73+8)⁄10

= 81⁄10

= 8.1
