The average of ten numbers is 30. If 5 is added to every number, the new average is:

A.  30

B.  30.5

C.  35

D.  53

Answer: C

Given that The average of ten numbers is 30

Total sum of 10 numbers⁄Total numbers(10) = 30

(Total sum of 10 numbers)⁄10 = 30

Total sum of 10 numbers = 30×10

Total sum of 10 numbers = 300

Given that 5 is added to every number.

So total number should be 10×5 = 50

Now total Sum of numbers

= 300+50

= 350

So the new average obtained after adding of 5 to every number is

=( Total Sum of 10 numbers after addition of 5) ÷10

= 350⁄10

= 35
