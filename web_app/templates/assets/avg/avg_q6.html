Question :6

The average age of a man and his son is 18 years. The ratio of their ages is 5:1. What will be the ratio of their ages after 6 yeas?

A.  5:2

B.  3:1

C.  6:1

D.  None of these


Given the average of a man and his son is 18

i.e. (Sum of ages of a man and his son⁄2 ) = 18

So sum of ages of a man and his son = 18×2 = 36

Given the ratio of their ages is 5:1

Fathers age can be calculated by

= Sum of their ages×
( Fathers fraction⁄Sum of fraction values)

= 36×(5⁄6)

= 30

So age of his son = 36-30 = 6

After 6 years thier ages becomes

Fathers age = 30+6 = 36

Son age = 6+6 = 12

Their ratio after 6 years is

= 36⁄12

= 3⁄1

= 3:1
