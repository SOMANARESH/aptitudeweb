Question :64

The average marks obtained by a group of 10 students is 41 marks. Find the new average if a new student who scored 63 marks is also included in the group.

A.  39

B.  40

C.  43

D.  45

Answer: C

Given that Average of 10 students = 41 marks

(Sum of 10 student marks)⁄10 = 41

Sum of 10 student marks = 410

Given that a new student score 63 marks and included to group

New average marks by 11 students is

= (Sum of marks by 11 students)⁄11

= (Sum of marks by 10 students +
11th student marks)⁄11

= (410+63)⁄11

= 473⁄11

= 43
