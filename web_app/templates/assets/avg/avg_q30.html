Out of three numbers, the first is twice the second and is half of the third. If the average of the three numbers is 56, then difference of first and third numbers is:

A.  48

B.  52

C.  44

D.  40


Given that first number is twice that of second and half that of third

i.e.X1 = 2(X2)

X1 = (X3)⁄2

So (X3)⁄2 = 2(X2)

X2 = (X3)⁄4

Then average of these numbers is 56

(sum of the three numbers)⁄3 = 56

X1+X2+X3 = 3×56

(X3)⁄2+(X3)⁄4+X3 = 168

(2X3+X3+4X3 ) ⁄4 = 168 7X3 = 4×168

7X3 = 672

X3 = 672⁄7

X3 = 96

So X1 = (X3)⁄2

X1 = (96)⁄2 = 48

X2 = X3⁄4

X2 = 96⁄4

X2 = 24

Finally the difference between first and third number is

X1-X3 = 96-48

X1-X3 = 48
