Question :43

The sum of five numbers is 555. The average of first two numbers is 75 and the third number is 115. What is the average of last two numbers?

A.  145

B.  290

C.  265

D.  150

Answer: A

Sum of five numbers = 555

Average of first two numbers is 75

(Sum of first two numbers)⁄2 = 75

Sum of first two numbers = 2×75 = 150

Third number = 115

Sum of last two numbers = Sum of all numbers - Sum of three numbers

Sum of last two numbers

= 555-(150+115)

= 555-265

= 290

Average of last two numbers

= 290÷2

= 145.
