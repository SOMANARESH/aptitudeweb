Out of 10 terms, the average of first 5 terms is 34 and that of last 5 terms is 38. If the average of first 9 terms is 35, find the 10th term.

A.  26

B.  35

C.  44

D.  45

Answer: D

Given that the average of first 5 terms is 34

(Sum of first 5 terms)⁄5 = 34

Sum of first 5 terms = 5×34

Sum of first 5 terms = 170

Given that the average of oyher 5 terms is 38

(Sum of other 5 terms)⁄5 = 38

Sum of other 5 terms = 5×38

Sum of other 5 terms = 190

Given that the average of first 9 terms is 35

(Sum of first 9 terms)⁄9 = 35

Sum of first 9 terms = 9×35

Sum of first 9 terms = 315

Sum of ten terms = Sum of first 5 terms+ Sum of other 5 terms

Sum of first 9 terms + 10 th term = (170+190)

315+10th term = 360

10th term = 360-315

10th term = 45
