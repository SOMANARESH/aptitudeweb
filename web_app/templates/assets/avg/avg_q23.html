Question :23

A,B,C and D are four consecutive even numbers and their average is 65. What is the product of A and D?

A.  3698

B.  4216

C.  4092

D.  4352

Answer: B

Given A,B,C and D are 4 consecutive even numbers

Let us assume that the four consecutive even numbers be 2n,2n+2,2n+4,2n+6

So A = 2n, B = 2n+2, C = 2n+4 D = 2n+6

Given that the average of these 4 numbers is 65

i.e.(Sum of 4 consecutive even numbers)⁄4 = 65

(2n+2n+2+2n+4+2n+6)⁄4 = 65

8n+14 = 4×65

8n = 260-12

n = 248⁄8

n = 31

Product of A and D is

= (2n)(2n+6)

= (62)(62+6)

= 62×68

= 4216
