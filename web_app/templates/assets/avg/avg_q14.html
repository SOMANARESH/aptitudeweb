Question :14

Sum of three consecutive even integers is 54. Find the least among them.

A.  18

B.  15

C.  14

D.  16


Let the three consecutive even numbers be 2n,2n+2,2n+4

Given Sum of three consecutive even numbers is 54

2n+2n+2+2n+4 = 54

6n+6 = 54

6n = 54-6

6n = 48

n = 48⁄6

n = 8

So least among the 3 numbers is 2n

= 2×8

= 16
