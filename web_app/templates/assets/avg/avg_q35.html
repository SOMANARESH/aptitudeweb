Question :35

Average of first five odd multiple of 3 is:

A.  12

B.  15

C.  18

D.  21


Sum of first 5 odd multiples of 3 = (3×1)+(3×3)+(3×5)+(3×7)+(3×9)

= 3×(1+3+5+7+9)

= 3×25

= 75

The average of first 5 odd multiples of 3

= (Sum of first 5 odd multiples of 3)⁄5

= 75⁄5

= 15.
