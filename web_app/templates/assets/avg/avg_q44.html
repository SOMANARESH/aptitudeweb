Question :44

The average of eight numbers is 20. The average of first two numbers is 15 1⁄2, and that of next three is 211⁄3, seventh and eighth number exceed the sixth number by 4 and 7 respectively. What is the eighth number?

A.  11

B.  18

C.  22

D.  25

Answer: D

Let 8 numbers be n1,n2,n3, n4,n5,n6, n7,n8

Average of 8 numbers is 20

(Sum of 8 numbers)⁄8 = 20

Sum of 8 numbers = 8×20

Sum of 8 numbers = 160

Average of first two numbers = 151⁄2

(n1+n2)⁄2 = 31⁄2

n1+n2 = 31

Average of next three numbers = 211⁄3

(n3+n4+n5)⁄3 = 64⁄3

n3+n4+n5 = 64

Seventh and eighth numbers can be written as

n7 = n6+4

n8 = n6+7

Sum of all numbers = 160

n1+n2+n3+n4+n5+n6+n7+n8 = 160

31+64+n6+n6+4n6+7 = 160

3n6 = 160-31-64-4-7

3n6 = 54

n6 = 18

Eighth nnumber = n6+7

Eighth nnumber = 18+7 = 25
