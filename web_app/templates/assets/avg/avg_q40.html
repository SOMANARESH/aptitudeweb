One year ago, the population of a town was 12000. In one year, male population is increased by 8% and female population by 6% but the total population is increased by 7%. What was the size of the male population one year ago

A.  5000

B.  5500

C.  6000

D.  7000

Answer: C

Let us assume that the population of male and female be X and Y

Given that the total population before one year is 12000

X+Y = 12000

After one year male population increased by 8% and female population increased by 6% and total population increased by 7%

Male population = X+(X×8⁄100)

Female population = Y+(Y×6⁄100)

Total population = 12000+(12000×7/100)

Total population = 12000+840

Total population = 12840

Male population + Female population = 12840

X+(X×8⁄100)+Y+(Y×6⁄100) = 12840

(X+Y)+((8X+6Y)⁄100) = 12840

12000+((8X+6Y)/100)) = 12840

(8X+6Y)⁄100 = 840

6X+2X+6Y = 100×840

6(X+Y)+2X = 84000

6(12000)+2X = 84000

72000+2X = 84000

2X = 84000-72000

X = 12000/2

X = 6000
