The mean of 50 observations was 36. It was found later that an observation 48 was wrongly taken as 23. The corrected (new) mean is:

A.  35.2

B.  36.1

C.  36.5

D.  39.1


Given that average of 50 observations is 36

(Sum of 50 observations)⁄50 = 36

Sum of 49observations+one observation= 50×36

Sum of 49 observations+23 = 1800

Sum of 49 observations = 1800-23 = 1777

Now average of 50 observations by replacing 23 by 48 is

= (Sum of 50 observations)⁄50

= (Sum of 49 observations+Original observation)⁄50

= (1777+48)⁄50

= 1825⁄50

= 36.5
