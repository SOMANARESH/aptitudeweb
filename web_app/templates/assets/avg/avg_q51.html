Question :51

In a group of 135 persons, 50% people contributed Rs.40 each, 30% contributed Rs.60 each and remaining persons Rs 80 each. Find the average contribution for the group.

A.  50

B.  54

C.  60

D.  62


Average contribution = 50% of 40 + 30% of 60 + 20% of 80

= 20+18+16

= 54.
