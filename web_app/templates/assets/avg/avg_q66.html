Average age of 9 members of a club is 29 years. If 2 more persons with the average age of 40 years become the members of the club, find average age of all the 11 members.

A.  30

B.  31

C.  32

D.  35


Given that Average age of 9 members = 29

(Sum of ages of 9 members)⁄9 = 29

Sum ages of 9 members = 9×29 = 261

Given that two persons whose average of 40 also included then total members in club is 11

Average of two persons is 40

(Sum of ages of 2 members)⁄2 = 40

Sum of ages of 2 members = 80

Now the average of 11 members is

= (Sum of ages of 11 members)⁄11

= (Sum of ages of 9 members+Sum of ages of two new members)⁄11

= (261+80)⁄11

= 341)⁄11

= 31
