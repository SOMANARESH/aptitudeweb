Question :97

Four years ago, the average age of 4 persons A,B,C and D was 38 years. With joining of E now, the present average age of 5 persons becomes 36 years. Find Es present age.

A.  26 years

B.  12 years

C.  30 years

D.  32 years

Answer: B

Let us assume that A,B,C and D be represent present ages.

Then before 4 year their ages are

A-4, B-4,C-4,D-4

Average age of 4 persons before 4 years is 38

(Sum of ages before 4 years)⁄4 = 38

Sum of ages before 4 years = 4×38

A-4+B-4+C-4+D-4 = 152

A+B+C+D = 152+16

A+B+C+D = 168

Given that E added to group then average becomes 36

Average age of 5 persons is 38

(Sum of ages 5 persons)⁄5 = 36

Sum of ages 5 persons = 5×36;

A+B+C+D+E = 180

168+E = 180

E = 180-168

E = 12
