Question :54

The average age of 14 girls and their teachers age is 15 years. If the teachers age is excluded, the average reduces by 1. What is teachers age?

A.  35

B.  32

C.  30

D.  29

Answer: D

Given that the average age of 14 girls and teachers age is 15

i.e. (Sum of ages of 14 girls and teachers age)⁄15 = 15

Sum of ages of 14 girls and teachers age = 15×15

Sum of ages of 14 girls+teachers age = 225--->(1)

Given that the average age gets reduced by 1 when teachers age excluded.

i.e.(Sum of ages of 14 girls)⁄14= 15-1

Sum of ages of 14 girls = 14×14

Sum of ages of 14 girls = 196

196+teachers age = 225

Teachers age = 225-196

Teachers age = 29
