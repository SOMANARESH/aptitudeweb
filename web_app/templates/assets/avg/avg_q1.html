The ratio of ducks and frogs in a pond is 37:41. The average of ducks and frogs is 156. What is the number of frogs in the pond?

A.  78

B.  74

C.  82

D.  164

Answer: D

Given the ratio of bucks and frogs is 37:41

B⁄F = 37⁄41

Given that the Average number of bucks and frogs = 156

We Know that Average is given by the formula

= (Total Sum of all quantities)⁄ (Total No. of quantities)

Average number of bucks and frogs = 156

(Total Sum of number of bucks and frogs)⁄ (No.of quantities(2))

= 156

Then Total number of bucks and frogs = 156×2

Total number of bucks and frogs = 312

Number of frogs = Total number of bucks and frogs ×
( Frogs fraction⁄Sum of the fraction )

Number of frogs = 312 ×41⁄(37+41)

= 312 ×41⁄78

= 164
