A cricketer batsman had a certain average of runs for 11 innings. In the 12th innings, he made a score of 90 runs and thereby his average of runs was decreased by 5. His average of runs after 12th innings is:

A.  155

B.  150

C.  145

D.  140

Answer: C

Let the average score by 11 innings be X

Then (Sum of 11 innings score)⁄11 = X

Sum of 11 innings score = 11X

Given that 12th innings score = 90

By adding of 12th innings the average decreases by 5

New average for 12 innings = X-5

(Sum of 12 innings score)⁄12 = X-5

Sum of 12 innings score = 12(X-5)

Sum of 11 innings score+12th inings score = 12X-60

11X+90 = 12X-60

90+60 = 12X-11X

X = 150

Average score after 12 innings is X-5

= 150-5

= 145
