The average of marks of 28 students in Mathematics was 50:8 students left the school, then this average increased by 5. What is the average of marks obtained by the students who left the school?

A.  50.5

B.  37.5

C.  42.5

D.  45


Given that Average marks of 28 students = 50 marks

(Sum of marks of 28 students)⁄28 = 50

Sum of 28 student marks = 28×50

Sum of 28 student marks = 1400--->(1)

Given that the average increased by 5 when 8 students left the school

New average of 20 student marks = 55

(Sum of marks of 20 students)⁄20 = 55

Sum of 20 student marks = 20×55

Sum of 20 student marks = 1100--->(2)

From (1)

Sum of marks by 28 students = Sum of marks by 20 students+Sum of marks by 8 students

1400 = 1100 + Sum of marks by 8 students

Sum of marks by 8 students = 1400-1100

Sum of marks by 8 students = 300

Now average of 8 students who left the schoo is

= (Sum of marks of 8 students)⁄8

= 300⁄8

= 37.5
