Question :20

a, b, c, d and e are five consecutive even numbers. If the sum of a and b is 162, what is the sum of all the numbers?

A.  400

B.  380

C.  420

D.  None of these

Answer: C

Given that a, b, c, d and e are five consecutive even numbers

Then we can written them as a = 2n, b=2n+2,

c=2n+4, d=2n+6, e=2n+8

Sum of a and b is 162

a+b = 162

2n+2n+2 = 162

4n+2 = 162

4n = 160

n = 40

So sum of all nnumbers is

2n+2n+2+2n+4+2n+6+2n+8

= 10n+20

= 10(40)+20

= 400+20

= 420
