The average age of four brothers is 12 years. If the age of their mother is also included, the average is increased by 5 yeasrs. The age of the mother is:

A.  37

B.  43

C.  48

D.  53

Answer: A

Given that the average of 4 brothers is 12

(Sum of ages of four brothers)⁄4 = 12

Sum of ages of four brothers = 4×12

Sum of ages of four brothers = 48

Let us assume that the mothers age be X

If mother is also included then the average age of four brothers and mother is increased by 5

(Sum of ages of four brothers and age of mother)÷5

= 12+5 = 17

48+X = 5×17

48+X = 85

X = 85-48

X = 37
