Question :63

A batsman makes a score of 58 runs in the 15th innings and thus increases his average by 3 runs. What is the average after 15th innings?

A.  12

B.  14

C.  16

D.  18

Answer: C

Let the average score for 14 innings be X

Then (Sum of 14 innings score)⁄14 = X

Sum of 14 innings score = 14X

Given that 15th innings score = 58

Now average increases by 3

New average for 15 innings = X+3

(Sum of 15 innings score)⁄15 = X+3

Sum of 15 innings score = 15(X+3)

Sum of 14 innings score+15th inings score = 15X+45

14X+58 = 15X+45

58-45 = 15X-14X

X = 13

Average score after 15 innings is X+3

= 13+3

= 16
