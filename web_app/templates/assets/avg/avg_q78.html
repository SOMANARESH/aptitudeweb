Average age of 6 sons of a family is 8 years. Average age of sons gohether with their parents is 22 yeass. If father is older than mother by 8 years, the age of mother is:

A.  44 years

B.  52 years

C.  60 years

D.  68 years

Answer: C

Given that the average age of 6 sons is 8

(Sum of ages of 6 sons)⁄6 = 8

Sum of ages of 6 sons = 6×8

Sum of ages of 6 sons = 48

Given the average age of 6 sons with their parents is 22

(Sum of ages of 6 sons with their parents)⁄8 = 22

Sum of ages of 6 sons with their parents = 8×22

Sum of ages of 6 sons with their parents = 176

Sum of ages of 6 sons + Sum ofages of parents = 176

48 +Sum of ages of parents = 176

Sum ofAges of parents = 176-48

Sum of Ages of parents = 128

Let us assume that mothers age be X

then according to problem fathers age is 8 years more than mother

Sum of Ages of parents = 128

X+X+8 = 128

2X = 128-8

X = 120/2

X = 60

Mothers age = 60
