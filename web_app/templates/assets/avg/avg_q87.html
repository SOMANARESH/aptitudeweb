Out of 10 teachers of a school, one teacher retires and in his place, a new teacher of age 25 years joins. As a result, average age of teachers is reduced by 3 years. The age (in years) of the retired teacher is:

A.  50

B.  58

C.  60

D.  55


Given average age of 10 teachers is X

(Sum of age of 10 teachers)⁄10 = X

Sum of age of 10 teachers = 10X

Sum of age of 9 teachers+ Retaired teachers age = 10X--->(1)

Given that one teachers retairs and replaced by new teacher with age of 25years.

Average age of 10 teachers after replacing recreases average by 3

Average age of 10 teachers after replacing = X-3

(Sum of age of 10 teachers after replacing)⁄10 = X-3

Sum of ages of 10 teachers after replacing = 10(X-3)

Sum of ages of 9 teachers + new teachers age = 10X-30

Sum of ages of 9 teachers+25 = 10X-30

Sum of ages of 9 teachers = 10X-30-25

Sum of ages of 9 teachers = 10X-55--->(2)

From (1) and (2)

10X-55+Retaired teachers age = 10X

Retaired teachers age = 55
