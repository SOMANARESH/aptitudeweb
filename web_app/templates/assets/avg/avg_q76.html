Question :76

The batting average of a cricketer for 78 innings is 38 runs. His highest score exceeds the lowest score by 226 runs. If these two innings are excluded, the average of the remaining innings is 36 runs. What is his highest score?

A.  156

B.  158

C.  227

D.  228

Answer: C

Let the lowest score be X

Then According to problem Highest score exceeds lowest by 226

i.e.Highest score = X+226

Given that the average runs by 78 innings is 38

Then (Sum of 78 innings score)⁄78 = 38

Sum of 78 innings score = 78×38

Sum of 78 innings score = 2964

Sum of 76 innings score + Lowest score+highest score = 2964

Sum of 76 innings score + X+X+226 = 2964

Sum of 76 innings score + 2X= 2964-226

Sum of 76 innings score + 2X= 2738--->(1)

Given that the average runs by 76 innings is 36

Then (Sum of 76 innings score)⁄76 = 36

Sum of 76 innings score = 76×36

Sum of 76 innings score = 2736

From(1)

2736+2X = 2738

2X = 2

X = 1

Highest score = X+226

Highest score = 1+226

Highest score = 227
