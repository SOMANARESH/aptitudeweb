Question :41

If the average of m numbers is n2 and that of n numbers is m2, find the average of (m +n) number.

A.  m-n

B.  m+n

C.  m⁄n

D.  mn


Average of m numbers is n2

(Sum of m numbers)⁄m = n2

Sum of m numbers = mn2

Average of n numbers is m2

(Sum of n numbers)⁄n = m2

Sum of n numbers = nm2

sum of (m+n) numbers = mn2 + m2n

= mn(n+m)

Average of (m+n)numbers = (sum of m+n numbers)⁄m+n

= mn(m+n)⁄m+n

= mn.
