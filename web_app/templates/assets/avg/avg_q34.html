Question :34

Find the average of first eight multiple of 6.

A.  18

B.  24

C.  27

D.  30

Answer: C

Sum of first eight multiples of 6

= (6×1)+(6×2)‐‐‐‐‐+(6×8)

= 6×(1+2+3+‐‐‐‐‐+8)

{Sum of first n numbers is given by (n)(n+1)⁄2}

= (6×8×9)⁄2

= 432⁄2

= 216

Average of first eight multiples of 6

= (Sum of first 8 multiples of 6)⁄8

= 216⁄8

= 27
