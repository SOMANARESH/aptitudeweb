Question :10

The average of first 47 natural numbers is:

A.  23

B.  24

C.  25

D.  47

Answer: B

Average = a⁄b

where

a = sum of the numbers

b = number of terms

We know that the sum of first n natural numbers is given by

n(n+1)⁄2

Average of 47 numbers

= (Sum of first 47 natural numbers ) ⁄47

= ( (47 × 48)⁄2) ⁄47

= ( 2256⁄2) ⁄47

= 1128 ⁄47

= 24
