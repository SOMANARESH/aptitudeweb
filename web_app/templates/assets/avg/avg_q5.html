The arithmetic mean of a set of 10 numbers is 20. If each number is first multiplied by 2 and then increased by 5, then what is the mean of new numbers?

A.  20

B.  25

C.  40

D.  45

Answer: D

Given that the arithmatic mean of 10 numbers is 20

We can write Arithmatic mean of 10 numbers as

= Total sum of 10 numbers⁄10

Total Sum of 10 numbers = 20×10

Total Sum of 10 numbers = 200

Given that each number is multiplied with 2

Then New sum of 10 numbers = 2×(Sum of numbers before multiplication)

New sum of 10 numbers = 2×200 = 400

Given 5 is added to every number

So total number added to Sum

= 10×5

= 50

Now new sum of 10 Numbers = Sum of numbers after mutiplication + Total number to be added after addition of 5 to every number

i.e. New sum of 10 Numbers

= 400+50

= 450

The arithmatic mean of 10 numbers after multiplication and addition is

= Total sum of 10 numbers⁄10

= 450⁄10

= 45
