The average age of 8 men is increased by 2 years when two of them whose ages are 21 and 23 years are replaced by two new men. The average age of the two new men is:

A.  22

B.  24

C.  28

D.  30

Answer: D

Let average age of 8 men be X

(Sum of ages of 4 men)⁄4 = X

Sum of ages of 8 men = 8X--->(1)

Given that the average gets increased by 2 when two of them of 21 and 23 years replaced by new mens age(Sum =Y)

Now average age becomes X+2

(Sum of ages of 8 men)⁄8 = X+2

Sum of ages of 6 men+Sum of ages of two new mens = 8(X+2)

Sum of ages of 6 men+Y= 8X+16

Sum of ages of 6 men = 8X+16-Y--->(2)

From (1) and (2)

Sum of ages of 8 men = 8X

Sum of ages of 6men+sum of ages of two mens = 8X

8X+16-Y+21+23 = 8X

Y = 60

Average of two new mens is

= (Sum of two new mens)⁄2

= 60⁄2

= 30
