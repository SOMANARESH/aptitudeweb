Average age of 15 men is increased by 1 year when one man in the group is replaced with other man whose age is 49 years. Find the age of the man replaced.

A.  34

B.  48

C.  50

D.  64


Given average age of 15 persons is X

(Sum of age of 15 persons)⁄15 = X

Sum of age of 15 persons = 15X--->(1)

Given that one persons with age of 49years is replaced by new persons.

Average age of 15 persons after replacing increases average by 1

Average age of 15 men after replacing = X+1

(Sum of age of 15 persons after replacing)⁄15 = X+1

Sum of ages of 15 persons after replacing = 15(X+1)

Sum of ages of 14 men + 49 = 15X+15

Sum of ages of 14 men = 15X-34

From (1)

Sum of ages of 14 men + 1man = 15X

15X-34+Mans age = 15X

Mans age = 34
