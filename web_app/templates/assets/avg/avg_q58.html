Question :58

A cricketer played 80 innings and scored an average of 99 runs. His score in the last inning is zero run. To have an average of 100 at the end, his score in the last innings should have been

A.  60

B.  80

C.  10

D.  1


Given that the average score for 80 innings is 99

(Total score for 80 innings)⁄80 = 99

Total runs for 80 innings = 80×99

Total runs for 80 innings = 7920

If runs scored by 80th innings is 0

Then Runs Scored by 79 innings = 7920

Now Let us assume that X be the runs scored on 80th match then his average becomes 100

(Total score for 80 innings)⁄80 = 100

Total score for 80 innings = 80×100

Total score for 80 innings = Total score for 79 innings + Score by 80th innings

7920+X = 8000

X = 8000-7920

X = 80
