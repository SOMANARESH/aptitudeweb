The average age of three boys is 24 years. If ratio of their ages is 3:4:5, then the age of the youngest boy is:

A.  6 years

B.  12 years

C.  18 years

D.  24 years

Answer: C

Given that the average age of 3 boys is 24

So we can write as

Average age of 3 boys

= (Total sum of ages of 3 boys)⁄
(Total number of boys(3))

24 = (Total sum of ages of 3 boys)⁄3

Total Sum of ages of 3 boys = 3×24 years

Total Sum of ages of 3 boys = 72 years

Given that the ratio of ages of 3 boys is 3:4:5

So youngest boy fraction is 3

Age of the youngest boy = Total sum of ages of 3 boys ×

( fraction of youngest boy⁄Sum of fraction )

Age of the youngest boy = 72×3⁄12

= 18 years.
