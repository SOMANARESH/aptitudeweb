12345679×9=?

A.  111111111

B.  21212121

C.  111111112

D.  2222222221


unit digit of the solution must be 1 i.e. units digit in 9 × 9

C: is ruled out

since 12 × 9 = 108

B and D are ruled out as first three digits (left hand side) are much greater than 108

sum of digits of solution must be 9 as the sum of digits of the multiplier is 9.

A is the correct answer
