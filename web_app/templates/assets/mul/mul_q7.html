Question :7

On multiplying a number by 153, 102325 is obtained as result. It is found that both the 2s in result are wrong. Find the correct result

A.  104345

B.  107375

C.  108385

D.  109395

Answer: d

153 is a multiple of 9 as sum of its digits is 9

The result must also be a multiple of 9

sum of digits (other than the wrong digits )

= 1+0+3+5 = 9

the number 10Y3Y5 is divisible by 9 (ignoring both Y)

the number will be divisible by 9 when Y + Y is divisible by 9

sum of replaced digits = 4+4,7+7,8+8,9+9, or 8,14,16,18

only 18 is divisible by 9

Y is to be replaced by 9

109395 is divisible by 153.
