The taxi charges in a city contain fixed charges and additional charge/km. the fixed charge is for a distance of upo 5km and additional charge/km thereafter. The charge for a distance if 10km is 350 and for 25km is 800. The charge for a distance of 30km is:

A.  900

B.  950

C.  800

D.  750

Answer: B

Additional km charges for 15 km

(i.e. 25-10)km = 800-350

= 450

Additional km charges for 1 km = 450÷15

= 30

Additional km charges for 5 km = 30×5

= 150

Total charges for 30 km = total charges for 25 km + additional km charges for 5km

= 800+150

= 950.
