Question :35

In a division problem, the divisor is 4 times the quotient and 3 times the remainder. If the remainder is 4, the dividend is:

A.  36

B.  40

C.  12

D.  30


Remainder = 4

Divisor = 3×4=12

Quotient = divisor÷4

= 12÷4=3

Dividend = (divisor×quotient)+remainder

= (12×3)+4

=36+4

=40.
