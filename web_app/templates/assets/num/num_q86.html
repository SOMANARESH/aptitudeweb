Question :86

Mohan gets 3 marks for each correct sum and loses 2 marks for each wrong sum. He attempted 20 sums and obtains 40 marks. The number of sums solved correctly is:

A.  10

B.  16

C.  20

D.  25

Answer: B

Let he attempted X sums correctly

Then his total marks = 3X-2(20-X)=40

5X = 80

X=16.
