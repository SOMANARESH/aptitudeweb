Question :59

With a two digit prime number, if 18 is added, we get another prime number with digits reversed. How many such numbers are possible?

A.  2

B.  3

C.  0

D.  1

Answer: A

On adding 18, the digits are reversed

Difference in digits = 18÷2=9

Prime numbers with difference of digits 2 = 13,79

Prime numbers on reversing the digits = 31,97.
