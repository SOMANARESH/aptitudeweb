A man engaged a servant on the condition that he would pay him 3000 and one uniform after one year of service. He served only for 9 months and got 2200 and a uniform. What is the price of uniform?

A.  100

B.  200

C.  250

D.  400

Answer: B

12 months wages = 3000+one uniform −−−−−(1)

9 months wages = 2200+ one uniform −−−−−(2)

(1)-(2)

3 months wages = 800

12 months wages

= 4×800 = 3200

From (1): cost of uniform

= 3200-3000

=200.
