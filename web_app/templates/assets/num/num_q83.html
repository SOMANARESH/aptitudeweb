Question :83

Mohan bought two varieties of wheat first at 9.50 per kg and second at 10 per kg. if he bought 50 kg at a total cost of 480, what is the quantity of wheat bough at 10 per kg.

A.  10

B.  20

C.  30

D.  40

Answer: A

Let whole quantity bought at 10 per kg

Then total cost = 10×50=500

Actual cost is short by 500-480=20

Difference between two rates = 10-9.50=0.50 per kg

Quantity bought at 9.50 per kg = 20÷0.50= 40 kg

Quantity bought at 10 per kg = (50-40)kg = 10 kg.
