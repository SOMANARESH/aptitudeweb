Find he maximum number of trees which can be planted, 20 meters apart , on the two sides of a straight road 1760 meters long.

A.  176

B.  180

C.  178

D.  174

Answer: C

number of trees can be planted on one side on road = 1760÷20+1

= 88+1

= 89

number of trees on both side

= 89×2

= 178.
