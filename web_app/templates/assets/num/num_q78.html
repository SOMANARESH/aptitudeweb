Question :78

Krishna has some hens and some goats. If the total number of animal heads are 81 and total number of animal legs are 234, how many goats does Krishna has?

A.  45

B.  24

C.  36

D.  None of these

Answer: C

Let krishna has hens only

Then total number of heads = 81×1=81

And legs = 81×2=162 which are short by 72 from the actual legs i.e. 234

Replacement of one hen with one goat means same number of heads and two more legs.

Hens replaced with goats = 72÷2=36.
