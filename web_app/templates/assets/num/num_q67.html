The numbers 1,3,5,7,−−−−− 99 and 128 are multiplied together. The number of zeros at the end of the product must be:

A.  19

B.  22

C.  7

D.  Nill


There are 10 numbers up to 100 viz. 5,15,25,35‐‐‐95

which have 5 at the units place and therefore are divisible by 5 but 25 and 75 are divisible by 25.

The product of odd numbers up to 100 can be divided

10+2=12 times by 5.

And 128 = 27

We know that 2×5 = 10

i.e. product of one pair of 2 and 5 gives one zero.

In the given question, 7 such pairs can be made.
