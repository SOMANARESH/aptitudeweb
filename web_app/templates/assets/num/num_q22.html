Atul is 12th from the right and 4th from the left in a queue of boys. The number of boys to be added to the queue such that there are 28 boys in the queue is:

A.  12

B.  13

C.  14

D.  16

Answer: B

Atul 12th from the right side

number of students in right side of Atul = 11 (not calculating Atul)

number of students in left side of Atul= 3 (not calculating Atul)

total boys in the queue

= 11+3+Atul

=15

Boys to be added

= 28−15

=13.
