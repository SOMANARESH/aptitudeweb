Question :58

A two digit number is five times the sum of is digits. If 9 is added to the number, the digits interchange their positions. The sum of digits of the number is:

A.  11

B.  9

C.  7

D.  6

Answer: B

Let the number be 10x+y

Then 10x+y = 5×(x+y)

5x = 4y

Since the digits can be natural number only.

Possible values of x = 4 and y = 5

The number is 45. [note 45+9=54]

Sum of digits = 4+5=9.
