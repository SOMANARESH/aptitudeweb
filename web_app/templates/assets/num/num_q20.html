Question 20:

Students of a class stand in a queue. If Ramesh is 19th in order from both ends, how many students are there in the queue?

A.  20

B.  37

C.  38

D.  39

Answer: B

Ramesh is 19th from both the sides

There are 18 students on his each side+ Ramesh

Total students in the queue = 18×2+1= 37.
