Question :81

20 is divided in two parts in such a way that 6 times of first part when added o 4 times of second part gives 104. Find the first part.

A.  8

B.  10

C.  11

D.  12

Answer: D

Let us assume that the number of quantities in first part and second part be X and Y

then the total number of quantity is 20

X+Y = 20---> 1

Given that on adding 6 times of first part to 4 parts of second part we will get 104

6X+4Y = 104 --->2

By solving 1 and 2 we get

2X = 24

X = 12 So the first part is 12
