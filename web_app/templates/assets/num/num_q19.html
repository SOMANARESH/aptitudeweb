Question 19:

In a two digit number, the digit in units place is three times the digit in tens place and sum of the digits is equal to 12. Find the number.

A.  39

B.  93

C.  Data inadequate

D.  None of these

Answer: A

Lets tens place digit be x.

Then units place digit = 3x

Now

x +3x = 12

4x = 12

x = 3

tens digit place = 3

units place digit = 3x

= 9

The required number is 39.
