Question :36

In a division sum, the divisor is 10 times the quotient and 5 times the reminder. If the remainder is 24, the dividend is:

A.  1440

B.  1464

C.  2892

D.  None of these

Answer: B

Divisor = 5×24=120

Quotient = 120÷10=12

Dividend = (divisor×quotient)+remainder

= 120×12+24

= 1440+24

= 1464.
