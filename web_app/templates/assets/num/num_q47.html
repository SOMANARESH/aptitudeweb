Question :47

A vessel contains 12 liters of water. If water is drained from the vessel until it holds 6 liters more then the quantity drained away. Find the quantity of the water(in liters) drained away

A.  3

B.  4

C.  6

D.  9

Answer: A

Let X liters of water is drained away

Then water remaining in the vessel is X + 6

X +(X+6) = 12

X = 3 liters.
