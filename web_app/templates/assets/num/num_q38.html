Question :38

A divisor is 25 times the quotient and 5 times the remainder if the quotient is 16 the dividend is:

A.  6440

B.  6480

C.  400

D.  480

Answer: B

Divisor = 25×quotient

= 25×16 = 400

Remainder = ( 400⁄5 ) = 80

Dividend = (divisor ×quotient)+remainder

= 400 × 16+80

= 6400+80

= 6480.
