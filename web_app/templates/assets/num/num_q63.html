Question :63

If a two digit number is three times the sum of its digits, the number formed by interchainging the digit is how many times of their sum?

A.  7

B.  8

C.  9

D.  10

Answer: B

We know that sum of two such numbers is 11 times the sum of its digit.

Number on interchanging = 11-3

=8 times of sum of the digits.
