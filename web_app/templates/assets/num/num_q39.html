In a question of division, the dividend is 37693, quotient is 52 and remainder lies between 52 and 104. Divisor will be :

A.  724

B.  723

C.  722

D.  721


( 37693⁄52 ) = 724+(remainder 45)

37693 = 724×52+45

= 723×52+(45+52)

= 723×52+97

= 723 is the required divisor.
