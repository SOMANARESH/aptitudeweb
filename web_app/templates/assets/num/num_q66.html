The difference between two numbers is 1375. On dividing the bigger number by he smaller number, the quotient is 6 and remainder is 25. Find the smaller number.

A.  250

B.  270

C.  275

D.  300

Answer: B

Let the smaller number = x

Then bigger number = x+1375

6x+25=x+1375

5x = 1350

X = 270.
