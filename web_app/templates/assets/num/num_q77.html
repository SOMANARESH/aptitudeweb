Question :77

A man has some hens and some cows. If the number of heads is 50 and number of feet is 142, the number of cows is:

A.  21

B.  25

C.  27

D.  29

Answer: A

Let the man has hens only

Then total heads = 50×1=50

And legs = 50×2 = 100 which is short by 42 from the actual legs i.e. 142

Replacement of one hen with one cow means same number of heads and two more legs.

Hens replaced with cows =42÷2=21

Cows = 21.
