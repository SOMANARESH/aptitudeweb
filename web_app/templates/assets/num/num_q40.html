Question :40

A bucket full of water weighs 50 kg, while half-filled bucket weighs 30kg. Find the weight of the empty bucket.

A.  10 kg

B.  15 kg

C.  20 kg

D.  25 kg

Answer: A

Weight of bucket + full water = 50 kg−−−−−>(1)

And weight of bucket + half water = 30 kg −−−−−>(2)

(1)-(2): weight of half water = (50-30)kg = 20 kg

Weight of empty bucket = 30 kg − 20 kg = 10 kg.
