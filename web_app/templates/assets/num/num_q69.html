Question :69

The numbers 1,2,3,4,−−1000 are multiplied together. The number of zeros at the end(on right) of the product must be:

A.  30

B.  200

C.  211

D.  249


2×5=10 i.e product of 2 and 5 gives one zero.

Numbers divisible by 5 = 1000÷5=200

Numbers divisible by 52 = 1000÷52 = 40

Numbers divisible by 53 = 1000÷ 53 = 8

Numbers divisible by 5 4 = 1000÷54 = 1

Total multiples of 5

= 200+40+8+1

= 249

Number divisible by 2

= 1000÷2

= 500

Total number of zeros of 249(minimum of numbers divisible by 2 or 5).
