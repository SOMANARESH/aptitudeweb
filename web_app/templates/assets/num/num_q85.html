Question :85

In an examination, a student is awarded 4 marks for every correct answer and losses 2 marks for every wrong answer. If a student attempted all 75 questions and secured 150 marks, the number of questions he attempted correct is:

A.  45

B.  50

C.  55

D.  48


Let he attempted X sums correctly

Then his total marks = 4X-2(75-X)=150

6X = 300

X = 50.
