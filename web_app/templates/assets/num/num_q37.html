Question :37

In a question of division, the divisor is 7 times the quotient and 3 times the remainder if the remainder is 28, then the divided is:

A.  588

B.  784

C.  823

D.  1036


Divisor = 28×3=84

Quotient = 84÷7=12

Dividend = (divisor×quotient)+remainder

= 84×12+28

= 1008+28

= 1036.
