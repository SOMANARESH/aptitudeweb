Question :80

A sum of 350 made up of 110 coins , which are either 1 or 5 denomination. How many coins are of 5?

A.  50

B.  55

C.  60

D.  175


Let all the coins are of 1 denomination

Then total value of 110 coins = 110×1 = 110 i.e. 240 less than 350

Replacing 1 one rupee coin with five rupee coin means 4 extra with same number of coins.

Five rupee coins = 240÷4=60 coins.
