Question :68

All the even numbers up to 100 i.e. 2,4,6−−−− 100 are multiplied together. The number of zeros at the end of the product will be:

A.  13

B.  12

C.  11

D.  10


There are 10 multiples of 10 up to 100 viz. 10, 20, 30−−−−−100

These numbers have 0 at the unit place but 100 has two zeros

Zero in the product of these numbers

= 10+1=11

Now 50=5×10

Product of 5 and an even number is also divisible by 10

Total number of zero in the product

= 11+1=12.
