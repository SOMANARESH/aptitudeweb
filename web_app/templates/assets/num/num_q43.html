Question :43

A student was asked to multiply a number by 3. But he divided the number by 3 and got 6.9 as an answer. what is the correct answer?

A.  2.3

B.  20.7

C.  62.1

D.  None of these.


Let X be the given number

Then students answer = X⁄3

Correct answer = 3X = 9×( X⁄3 )

=9×6.9

=62.1.
