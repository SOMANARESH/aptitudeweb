Question :79

380 mangoes are distributed among some boys and girls who are 85 in number. Each boy gets four mangoes and each girl gets five. The number of boys is:

A.  15

B.  38

C.  40

D.  45


Let us assume that the number of boys and girls be B and G

then B+G = 85--->1

Given that each boy gets 4 mangoes and each girl gets 5 mangoes

Then B boys will get B×4 and G girls will get G×5

So sum of the total is 384

i.e. 4B+5G = 384--->2

By solving eq.1 and eq.2 we will get

G = 40

B+G = 85

B = 85-G

B = 85-40

B = 45
