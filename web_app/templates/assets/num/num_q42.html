Question :42

A single person takes 6 minutes to fill a bottle. If from 11 a.m to 12.30 p.m, 1845 bottles are to be filled, how many should be employed on this job?

A.  121

B.  123

C.  131

D.  137


Bottles filled by a single person from 11 a.m. to 12.30 p.m.

= 90÷6

=15

Persons required to fill 1845 bottles

= 1845÷15

=123.
