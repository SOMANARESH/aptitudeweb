Question :84

A wire when cut into pieces of 14 metre each, leaves a piece of 11 metre. When another wire of same length is cut into pieces of 15 mere each, a piece of 3 metre is left. The minimum length of the wire is:

A.  18 meters

B.  25 meters

C.  123 meters

D.  221 meters


Difference between the pieces left = 11-3= 8 meters

Difference in length of two types of pieces = 15-14

= 1 meter each

number of pieces cut from the wire = 8 ÷ 1

= 1

length of the wire = 14×8+11

= 123 meters

or Length of the wire = 15×8+3

=123 meter.
