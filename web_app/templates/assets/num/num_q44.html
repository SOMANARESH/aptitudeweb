Question :44

Pooja started her job with certain monthly salary and gets a fixed pay increment every year. If her salary was 4200 after 3 years and 6800 after 8 years of service, then what are her initial salary and the annual increment respectively?

A.  2640,320

B.  2460,320

C.  2450,520

D.  2640,520


Five increments = 6800-4200 = 2600

One increment = 2600⁄5 = 520

Three increments = 520×3= 1560

Salary after 3 years = initial salary + 3 increments

Initial salary = salary after 3 years − 3 increments

= 4200-1560

= 2640.
