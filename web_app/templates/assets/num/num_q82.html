Question :82

A sum of 80 made up of 100 coins, which are either 1 or 50 paise denomination. How many coins are of 50 paise?

A.  80

B.  75

C.  60

D.  40

Answer: D

Let all the coins are of 50 paise denomination

Then total value of 100 coins = 100×1÷2=50 i.e 30 less than 80.

Replacement of one fifty paise coin with one rupee coin means 50 paise extra.

One rupee coin = 30÷0.50 = 30×2 = 60 coins

50 paise coins = 100-60

= 40.
