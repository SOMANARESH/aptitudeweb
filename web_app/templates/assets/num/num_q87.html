Question :87

The minimum value of (x-2)(x-9) is:

A.  -11⁄4

B.  -49⁄4

C.  0

D.  2

Answer: B

The expression will have minimum value when the product of two factors is maximum and the product sign is negative.

(x-2) = -(x-9)

x-2 = -x+9

2x =11

X = 11÷2

(x-2)(x-9) = ((11÷2)-2)((11÷2)-9)

= (7÷2)(-7/2)

= -49⁄4.
