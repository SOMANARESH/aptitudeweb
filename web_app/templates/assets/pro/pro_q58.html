Question :58

On selling 17 balls at 720, there is a loss equal to the cost price of 5 balls. The cost price of a ball is:

A.  45

B.  50

C.  60

D.  55


Cost price of 17 balls = selling price of 17 balls + cost price of 5 balls

Cost price of 12 balls = 720

Cost price of 1 ball = 60.
