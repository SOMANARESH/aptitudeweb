Question :50

Mahesh purchased a radio at 9⁄10 of its selling price and sold it at 8% more than its original selling price. His gain percent is:

A.  20%

B.  18%

C.  10%

D.  8%

Answer:a

Let marked selling price = 100

Then buying price = 100× 9⁄10

=90

Actual selling price = 100+8% of 100

= 108

Profit = 108-90

= 18 on 90

Profit%= 18⁄90

= 1⁄5

=20%.
