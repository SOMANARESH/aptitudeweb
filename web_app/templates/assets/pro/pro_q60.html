Question :60

Some quantity of tea is sold at 22 per kg, making 10% profit. If total gain is 88, what is the quantity of tea sold?

A.  4kg

B.  40kg

C.  44kg

D.  50kg


Profit = 10% of cost price = 1⁄10 of cost price

= 1⁄11 of selling price

= 1⁄11×22

= 2 per kg

But actual gain = 88

Quantity sold = 88÷2

= 44 kg.
