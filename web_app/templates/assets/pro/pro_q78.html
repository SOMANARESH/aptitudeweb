Question :78

By selling an article for 69, there is a loss of 8%, when the article is sold for 78, the gain or loss percent is:

A.  Neither gain or loss

B.  40%gain

C.  4%gain

D.  4%loss


Selling price = 69

= 100% -8%

= 92% of the cost price

new selling price = 78

= 92% × 78⁄69

= 104% of cost price

profit = 104% - 100%

=4% of the cost price.
