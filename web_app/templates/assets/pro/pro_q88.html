Profit after selling a commodity for 524 is the same as loss after selling it for 452. The cost price of the commodity is:

A.  480

B.  500

C.  488

D.  485


Let loss = profit =x

Then cost price = 524-x

=452+x

2x = 524-452

=72

X= 36

Cost price = 452+36

=488.
