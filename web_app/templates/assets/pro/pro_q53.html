Raghavan purchased a scooter at 13⁄15 of its selling price and sold it at 12% more than its selling price. His gain is:

A.  20%

B.  30%

C.  30 1⁄13 %

D.  29 3⁄13 %.

Answer:d

Let marked price be x

Then cost price = 13⁄15x

Selling price = 112⁄100x

=28⁄25x

SP⁄CP× 100 = 28⁄25x ÷ 13⁄15x × 100

=28⁄25× 15⁄13× 100

=1680⁄13

=129 3⁄13%

Profit % = 129 3⁄13% - 100%

= 29 3⁄13 %.
