Question :28

A merchant professes to sell goods at 20% profit but uses weight of 800 grams in place of a kilogram. What is his actual profit percent?

A.  20%

B.  25%

C.  50%

D.  33.33%


The merchant gives 800 grams charging the price of 1200 grams.

His gain is 400 grams on every 800 grams

Profit percent = 400÷800×100

= 50%.
