Question :32

Profit margin of a merchant is increased by 10%, if he sells an article for 650, instead of 600. What is the cost price of that article?

A.  50

B.  500

C.  800

D.  1000


Difference in two selling prices = 10% of cost price

Actual difference in two selling prices = 650-600

=50(i.e. 5 times of 10)

Cost price = 5×100

=500.
