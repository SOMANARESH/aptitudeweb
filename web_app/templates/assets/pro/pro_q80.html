Question :80

By selling an article at 80% of its marked price, a trader makes a loss of 10%. What will be the profit percentage if he sells it at 95% of its marked price?

A.  5.9

B.  12.5

C.  6.9

D.  5

Answer: c

80% of marked price = (100-10)%

= 90% of the cost price

95% of the marked price = 95÷80×90

=106.9% (approx) of the cost price

Profit = (106.9 -100%)

= 6.9% of the cost price.
