Question :48

If selling price of 4⁄5 of the stock of oranges is equal to cost price of the whole stock, find profit percent.

A.  20%

B.  25%

C.  30%

D.  33.33%

Answer:b

Let cost price of 1 orange = 1

Cost price of 4 oranges = 4

Selling price of 4 oranges = cost price of 5 oragnes = 5

Profit = 5-4=1 on 4

Profit% = 1⁄4

= 25%.
