Question :21

A man gains 20% by selling an article for a certain price. If he sells it at double the price then percentage of profit will be:

A.  40%

B.  140%

C.  100%

D.  120%

Answer:b

Selling price = 100%+20%

= 120% of cost price

New selling price = 2×120% of cost price

= 240% of cost price

New profit = 240%-100%

= 140% of cost price.
