Question :34

A bicycle is sold at 10% profit. Had it been sold for 10 less, the profit would have been 5% only. What is the cost price of the bicycle?

A.  100

B.  180

C.  190

D.  200


Difference in two selling prices = 10%-5%

= 5% of cost price

Actual difference in two sellin prices = 10 (i.e. 2 times of 5)

Cost price = 2×100

= 200.
