Question :27

A merchant professes to sell goods at the loss of 5% but uses weight of 900 grams in place of one kilogram. What is his profit percent?

A.  5%

B.  5 5⁄9%.

C.  6%

D.  10%


The merchant gives 900 grams charging the price of 950 grams.

His gain is 50 grams on every 900 grams.

Profit percent = 50⁄900 ×100

= 5 5⁄9%.
