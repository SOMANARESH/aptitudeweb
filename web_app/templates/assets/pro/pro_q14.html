A shopkeeper purchased a chair marked at 800, at two successive discounts of 10% and 15% respectively. He spent 28 on transportation and sold the chair for 800. His gain percent is:

A.  40%

B.  30%

C.  25%

D.  14%

Answer:c

Cost price = 800× 90⁄100 × 85⁄100 + 28

= 612+28

=640

Profit = 800-640

= 160

% profit = 160⁄640 ×100

= 25%.
