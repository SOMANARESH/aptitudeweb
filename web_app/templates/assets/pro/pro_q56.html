Question :56

12 copies of a book were sold for 1800 there by gaining cost price of 3 copies. The cost price of a copy is:

A.  120

B.  150

C.  1200

D.  1500


1800 = selling price of 12 books = cost price of 12 books + profit

= cost price of 12 books + cost price of 3 books

= cost price of 15 books

Cost price of 1 book = 1800⁄15

= 120.
