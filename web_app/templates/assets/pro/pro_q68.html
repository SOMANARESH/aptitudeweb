Question :68

A merchant fixed selling price of his articles at 700 after adding 40% profit to the cost price. As the sale was very low at this price level, he decided to fix the selling price at 10% profit. Find the new selling price:

A.  450

B.  490

C.  500

D.  550

Answer:d

New selling price = 700× (100+10)⁄(100+40)

= 700× 110⁄140

=550.
