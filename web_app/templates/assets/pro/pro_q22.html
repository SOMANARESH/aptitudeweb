Question :22

On selling an article for a certain price, a man loses 30%. What is his loss⁄profit percent, if he sells the article for double the price?

A.  60%loss

B.  15%loss

C.  40%profit

D.  30%profit

Answer: c

Selling price = (100-30)%

= 70% of cost price

New selling price = 2×70%

= 140% of cost price.

Profit = 140%-100%

= 40% of cost price.
