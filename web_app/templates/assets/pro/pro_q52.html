Question :52

A person bought an article on 40% discount and sold it at 50% more than the marked price. What profit did he get?

A.  50%

B.  90%

C.  150%

D.  250%


let the marked price be 100

then cost price = 100-40

= 60

selling price = 100+50

= 150

profit = 150-60

= 90

profit % = 90÷60×100

= 150%.
