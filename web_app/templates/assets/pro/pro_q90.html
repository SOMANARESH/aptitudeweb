Question :90

If the selling price of an article is doubled, then its loss percent is converted into equal profit percent. The loss percent on the article is:

A.  26 2⁄3%

B.  33 1⁄3%

C.  50%

D.  20%


Let cost price of the article be 100 and profit/loss percent be x%

Then (100+x)= 2(100-x)

100+x=200-2x

3x = 100

Or x = 33 1⁄3%.
