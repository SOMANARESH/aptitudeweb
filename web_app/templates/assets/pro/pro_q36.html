Question :36

An article is sold at 10% loss. Had it been sold for 30 more, the loss would have been 5% only. What is the cost price of the article?

A.  200

B.  300

C.  500

D.  600

Answer:d

Difference in two selling prices = 10% -5% of cost price

Actual difference in two selling prices = 30 (i.e. 6 times of 5)

Cost price = 6×100

=600.
