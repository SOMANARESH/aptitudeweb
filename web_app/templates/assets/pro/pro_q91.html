Question :91

The percentage profit earned by selling an article for 1730 is equal to the percentage loss incurred by selling the same article for 1270. At what price the article should be sold to earn 20% profit?

A.  1500

B.  1650

C.  1800

D.  Data in sufficient


Let cost price of the article be x

Then cost price = 1730-x

= x-1270

2x=1730+1270=3000

X=1500

Profit = 20% of 1500

= 300

Required selling price = 1500+300

=1800.
