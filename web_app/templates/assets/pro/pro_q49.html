Question :49

A retailer buys goods at 9⁄10 of its marked price and sells them at 12⁄10 of the marked price, find his profit percent.

A.  10%

B.  20%

C.  25%

D.  33.33%


Let the marked price of the goods = 10

Then cost price = (10×9)÷10

=9

Selling price = (10×12)÷10

= 12

Profit = 3 on 9

= 3⁄9

= 1⁄3

= 33.33.
