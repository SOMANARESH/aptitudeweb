Question :81

On selling an article for 64, a man gains 1⁄7 of the cost price. Find his profit percentage, if the article is sold for 63.

A.  12.5%

B.  20%

C.  25%

D.  33.33%


Gain =1⁄7of cost price

Selling price = 1+ 1⁄7

=8⁄7 of cost price

64 = 8⁄7 of cost price

63=8⁄7 × 63⁄64 = 9⁄8 of cost price

Profit = 9⁄8 -1

= 1⁄8

=12.5% of cost price.
