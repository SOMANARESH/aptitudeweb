Question :74

On selling 7 pens for 44, a man gains 10% of the cost price, what should be the selling price of 10 pens, if desired profit is 40%

A.  50

B.  56

C.  80

D.  100


Selling price of 1 pen = 44⁄7

Selling price of 10 pens = 10 × 44⁄7

Selling price of 10 pens at new rate

= 10 × 44⁄7 × 140⁄110

=80.
