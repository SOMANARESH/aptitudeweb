Question :25

A man buys milk at 45 p per liter, add one fourth of water to it and sells the mixture at 54 p per liter. The profit per cent is:

A.  20%

B.  30%

C.  40%

D.  50%


Cost price of one liter of mixture = 45× 4⁄5 = 36 p

Profit per liter = (54-36)p

= 18 p

Profit % =18⁄36 ×100

= 50%.
