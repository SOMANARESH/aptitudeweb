Question :94

On selling an article for 440, the amount of loss suffered is double of profit made on selling the article at 6% profit. What is the cost price of the article?

A.  400

B.  450

C.  500

D.  550

Answer: c

Loss suffered = double of profit made

Loss suffered = 2×6% of the cost price = 12% of cost price

Selling price = 440

=88% of the cost price

Cost price = (440×100)÷88

=500.
