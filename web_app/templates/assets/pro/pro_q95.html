Question :95

An article is sold for 190, hence gaining a certain amount. Had the article been sold for 175, he would have suffered loss equal to 50% of the gain in the first case. Find cost price of the article.

A.  178

B.  180

C.  185

D.  188


let gain in the first case = 2x

then loss in the second case = x

difference between two selling prices = 2x − (− x)

=2x+x=3x

actual difference between the two selling prices = 190-175

=15

3x = 15

x =5.

cost price = 190-2x

=190-10=180

or cost price = 175+x=175+5

=180.
