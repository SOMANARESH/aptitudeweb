Question :38

A loss of 19% gets converted into a profit of 17% when the selling price is increased by 162. The cost price of the article is:

A.  360

B.  540

C.  450

D.  600

Answer:c

Difference in two selling prices = 17%-(-19%)

= 36% of cost price

Actual difference in two selling prices = 162

Cost price = 162⁄36 ×100

=450.
