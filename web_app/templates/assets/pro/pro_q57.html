Question :57

A man bought 25 km of rice for 750. On selling them for a certain price, he suffered loss equal to the selling price of 5 kg. find the selling price of 1 kg of rice.

A.  25

B.  30

C.  35

D.  37.50


Selling price + loss = cost price

Selling price of 25 kg + selling price of 5 kg = 750

Selling price of 1 kg = 750⁄30

= 25.
