Question :10

A man bought toffees at the rate of 15 for a rupee and sold them at the rate of 12 for a rupee. His gain percent is:

A.  20%

B.  25%

C.  33.33%

D.  None of these


Ratio of cost price and selling price = 1÷15:1÷12

= 12:15

= 4:5

Profit percent = 1⁄4×100

= 25%.
