Question :93

On selling an article for 270, the amount of loss suffered is as much as profit earned on selling the article at 10% profit. What is the selling price if the desired profit is 20%?

A.  290

B.  300

C.  330

D.  360


Loss suffered = profit made = 10% of the cost price

Selling price = 270=90% of cost price

Selling price at 20% profit = 120% of cost price

= 270× 120⁄90

=360.
