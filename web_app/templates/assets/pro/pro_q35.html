Question :35

By selling a table for 350 instead of 400, loss percent increases by 5%. The cost price of the table is:

A.  1050

B.  417.50

C.  435

D.  1000


Difference in two selling prices = 400-350=50

Difference in two selling prices = 5% of cost price

= 50(i.e. 10 times)

Cost price = 10×100

= 1000.
