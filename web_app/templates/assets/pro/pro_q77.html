Question :77

On selling an article for 600, a man gains 20%. What will be the profit if the article is sold for 800?

A.  60%

B.  50%

C.  40%

D.  30%

Answer: a

Selling price = 600=120% of cost price

New selling price = 120% × 800⁄600

=160% of cost price

Profit = 160% -100%

= 60% of the cost price.
