Question :55

Ram bought a bat with 20% discount on the marked price. He sold the bat for 1200 with a profit of 80. The marked price of the bat was:

A.  2400

B.  1600

C.  1400

D.  1250

Answer:c

Cost price of bat = selling price − profit

= 1200 − 80

= 1120

80% of marked price = 1120

Marked price = 1120×100÷80

= 1400.
