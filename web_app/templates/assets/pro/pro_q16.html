Question :16

A grocer purchased 2 kg of rice at the rate of 15 per kg. and 3 kg of rice at the rate of 13 per kg. at what price per kg should he sell the mixture to earn 33 1⁄3 % profit on the cost price?

A.  28

B.  20

C.  18.40

D.  17.40

Answer:c

Total quantity bought = 2+3 =5 kg

Total cost price = 2×15+3×13

=30+39

=69

Desired profit = 33 1⁄3% of 69

= 1⁄3 of 69

= 23

total selling price = 69+23

=92

Selling price per kg. = 92÷5

= 18.40.
