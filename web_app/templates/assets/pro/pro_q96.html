Question :96

A watch is sold at 10% discount on its marked price of 480. If the retailer makes 20% profit on the cost price, find the cost price of the watch?

A.  300

B.  360

C.  450

D.  540

Answer: b

If marked price is 100, selling price

= 100-10

=90

If cost price is 100, selling price = 100+20

=120

Cost price = 480× 90⁄100 × 100⁄120

=360.
