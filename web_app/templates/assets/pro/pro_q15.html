Question :15

Ramesh purchased two types of wheat, one costing 20 per kg and the other 25 per kg in quantities having ratio5:4. He mixed the two and sold the mixture at 24 per kg. in the transaction, he got:

A.  4%Loss

B.  4%gain

C.  8%Loss

D.  8%gain

Answer:d

Cost price of 9 kg of mixture = 5×20+4×25

=200

Selling price of 9kg of mixture = 9×24

=216

Profit = 216-200

=16

Profit percent = 16⁄200 ×100

= 8%.
