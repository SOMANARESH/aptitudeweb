Question :46

Arvind purchased a wrist watch with 30% discount on the labeled price. He sold it with 40% profit on the price he bought. What was his percent loss on the labeled price?

A.  8

B.  2

C.  6

D.  10


Let labeled price = 100

Then cost price = 70

Selling price = 70+40% of 70

=98

Loss on labeled price = 2.
