Question :33

An article is sold at a profit of 20%. If it had been sold at a profit of 25%, it would have fetched 35 more. The cost price of the article is:

A.  650

B.  700

C.  750

D.  800

Answer:b

Difference in two selling prices = 25% -20% = 5% of cost price

Actual difference in two selling price = 35(i.e. 7 times of 5)

Cost price = 7×100

= 700.
