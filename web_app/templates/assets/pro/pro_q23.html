Question :23

A man gains 10% by selling an article for a certain price. What is profit/loss if the article is sold for half the price?

A.  45%loss

B.  5%loss

C.  50%loss

D.  55%loss


Selling price = (100 + 10)%

= 110% of cost price.

New selling price = 1⁄2 ×110%

= 55% of cost price

Loss = 100%-55%

= 45% of cost price.
