Question :73

On selling apples at 36 for a rupee, a shopkeeper loses 10%. How many apples should he sell for a rupee in order to gain 8%?

A.  25

B.  30

C.  32

D.  35


Selling price of 1 apple = 1⁄36

New selling price = 1⁄36 × 108⁄90

=1⁄30

For one rupee, he should sell 30 apples.
