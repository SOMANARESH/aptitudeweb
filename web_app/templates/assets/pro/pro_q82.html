Question :82

On selling an article for 50, a man lose 1⁄6 of his cost price. Find his profit percentage, if the article is sold for 66.

A.  5%

B.  10%

C.  20%

D.  25%

Answer:b

Loss = 1⁄6 of cost price

Selling price = 1 - 1⁄6

= 5⁄6 of cost price

50 =5⁄6 of cost price

66=5⁄6 × 66⁄50 of cost price

=11⁄10 of cost price

Profit = 11⁄10-1

= 10% of cost price.
