Question :51

A retailer buys goods at 10% discount on its marked price and sells them at 20% higher than the marked price. What is his profit percent?

A.  20%

B.  25%

C.  30%

D.  33.33%

Answer: d

Let marked price of the article = 100

Then its cost price = 100-10

=90

And selling price = 100+20

=120

Profit = 30 on 90 i.e. 1⁄3

= 33.33%
