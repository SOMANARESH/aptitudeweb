Question :69

A shopkeeper bought some eggs at the rate of 16 per dozen. Due to mishandling 20% of the eggs bought were broken during the transportation. At what rate of per dozen should he sell the remaining eggs so as to gain 30% on the total cost price?

A.  16

B.  18

C.  24

D.  26


Since 20% of the quantity is spoiled, selling the eggs at cost price will result in 20% loss.

We are to find the selling price which gives him 30% profit instead of 20% loss.

Selling price = 16× 130⁄80

= 26 per dozen.
