Question :79

By selling a tape recorder for 950. I lose 5%. What percent shall I gain by selling it for 1040?

A.  5

B.  4

C.  4.5

D.  9


Selling price = 950-100% -5%

=95% of the cost price

New selling price = 1040

=95%×1040÷950

= 104% of cost price

Profit = 104% - 100%

= 4% of the cost price.
