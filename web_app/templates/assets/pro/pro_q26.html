Question :26

A dishonest merchant professes to sell his goods at cost price, but uses a weight of 900 grams for one kg. what is his profit percent?

A.  10%

B.  11 1⁄9%.

C.  6%

D.  10%

Answer:b

The merchant gives 900 grams charging the price of 1000 grams.

His gain is 100 grams on every 900 grams

Profit percent = 100⁄900%. ×100

=11 1⁄9%.
