Question :84

A sells an article to B at a profit of 10%. B sells the article back to A at a loss of 10%. In the transaction:

A.  A neither gains nor loses

B.  A gains 11%

C.  A gains 20%

D.  A gains 21%


Let cost price of the article be 100

Cost price of the article to B = 110

B sold it to A for 110-10% of 110=99

A gains = 110-99 = 11.
