Question :59

A man bought 25 pens for 4800 and sold them at profit equal to the selling price of one pen. What is the selling price of a pen?

A.  192

B.  196

C.  200

D.  cant say


Selling price of 25 pens = cost price of 25 pens + selling price of 1 pen

Selling price of 24 pens = 4800

Selling price of 1 pen = 4800÷24

= 200.
