The profit earned after selling an article for 1754 is same as loss incurred after selling the article for 1492. What is the cost price of the article?

A.  1623

B.  1523

C.  1689

D.  1589


Let the loss and gain be x in each case

Then difference in two selling prices = 2x =1754-1492

2x=262

X=262÷2=131

Cost price = selling price (second)+ Loss

=1492+131=1623

Or cost price selling price (first)-profit =1754-131

=1623.
