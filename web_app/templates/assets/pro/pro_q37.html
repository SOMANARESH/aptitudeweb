Question :37

A tradesman sold an article at a loss of 20%. If the selling price had been increased by 100, there would have been a gain of 5%. The cost price of the article was:

A.  200

B.  25

C.  400

D.  250


Difference in two selling prices = 5%-(-20%)

=25% of cost price

Actual difference in two selling prices = 100(i.e. 4 times of 25)

Cost price = 4×100

=400.
