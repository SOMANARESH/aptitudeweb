Question :1

A man’s expenditure on food is 3⁄5 of his expenditure on clothes, find ratio between his expenditure on the two item.

A.  2:5

B.  3:5

C.  2:3

D.  5:3


Let the expenditure on clothes is 5. Then expenditure on food is 3

Ratio of expenditure on food and clothes

= 3:5
