Question :8

The sum of two numbers is twice the difference between them. If the smaller number is 20. Find the bigger number.

A.  5

B.  40

C.  60

D.  80


Ratio between sum and difference of the numbers is 2:1

Ratio between the numbers

= (2+1):(2-1) i.e. 3:1

Bigger number = 3×20

=60.
