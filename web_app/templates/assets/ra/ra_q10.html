Question :10

Ratio between two numbers is 3:4 and sum of their squares is 625. The numbers are:

A.  6,8

B.  9,12

C.  12,16

D.  15,20


Let the numbers be 3x and 4x

Then 3x2 + 4x2 = 625

9x2 + 16x2= 625

X = root of (625/25)

= √ 25 

=5

The numbers are 3×5 and 4×5 i.e. 15 and 20.
