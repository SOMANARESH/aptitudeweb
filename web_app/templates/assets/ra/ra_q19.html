Question :19

Same quantity of rice is required for each member of a family of 15 members. On a particular day due to the absence of some members of the family, the consumption of rice was reduced in the ratio 5:3. The number of members absent on that day was:

A.  3

B.  6

C.  8

D.  9

Answer:b

Ratio of consumption = 5:3

Ratio of members = 3:5

Members present on other day = 3⁄5×15

= 9

Members absent = 15-9

=6.
