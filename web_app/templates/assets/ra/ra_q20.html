Question :20

A mixture contains spirit and water in the ratio 3:2. If it contains 3 litres more spirit than water, the quantity of spirit in the mixture is:

A.  10 liters

B.  12 liters

C.  8 liters

D.  9 liters


Difference of quantities = (3-2)ratio

= 1 ratio

= 3 litres

Quantity of spirit = 3 ratio

= 3×3 liters

= 9 liters.
