Question :11

Ratio between two numbers is 5:3 and difference between their squares is 144. Find the numbers.

A.  5,3

B.  10,6

C.  15,9

D.  20,12

Answer:c

Let the numbers be 5x and 3x

Then difference between their squares

= (5x)2-(3x)2= 144

25x2 − 9x2 = 16x2 = 144

X2 = 144⁄16 = 9

X2 = 9

X =3

The numbers are 5×3 and 3×3 i.e. 15 and 9.
