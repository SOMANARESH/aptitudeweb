Question :10

The ratio of present ages of sita and her son is 5:2 respectively. Seven years hence, the ratio will be 2:1. What was the age of sita when her son was born?

A.  28 years

B.  35 years

C.  21 years

D.  Data insufficient


The ratio of present ages of sita and her son is 5:2

=> Si⁄So = 5⁄2

Then So=Si×2⁄5---> 1

The ratio of their ages After 7 years is 2:1

=> (Si+7)⁄(So+7) = 2⁄1

=> Si+7 = 2×(So+7)

=> Si+7 = (2×So)+14

Substitute 1 in the above eq. we will get

=> Si+7 = (2×Si×2⁄5) + 14

By Calculations we will get Si = 35

=> So = Si×2⁄5

=> So = 14.

Then Difference is 35-14 = 21.
