Question :27

Present ages of A and B are 50 years and 18 years respectively. In howmany years will A be twice as old as B?

A.  14 years

B.  15 years

C.  16 years

D.  18 years

Answer:a

Given that the present ages of A and B are 50 and 18

A=50 & B=18

Let the required time be X years

Given that As ages becomes double that of B after X years

Then (50+X)=2×(18+X)

50+X=36+2X

X=14 years

A will be twice of Bs age after 14 years.
