Question :28

The sum of ages of mohan and his father is 35 years. When mohans age will be equal to present age of his father, then sum of their ages will be 85 years. Find present age of the father.

A.  20 years

B.  25 years

C.  30 years

D.  32 years

Answer: c

Let us assume that the ages of mohan and his father be M and F

Sum of their present ages is 35

i.e. M+F=35 ---> 1

And also given that Mohans age is equal to present of his Father after Some years.

So Let assume The time is X years then

M+X=F ---> 2

And sum of their ages after X years is 85

M+X+F+X=85

M+F+(2×X)=85

From 1 we get

35+(2×X)=85

2×X=50

X=25

Substitute 2 into 1 we get

M+M+X=35

2×M=35-25

2×M=10

M=5

The present age of father is

F=M+X

F=5+25

F=30
