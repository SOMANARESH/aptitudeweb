Question :21

In 30 years, age of a person becomes 3 times of his present age. What is his present age?

A.  7.5 years

B.  10 years

C.  12.5 years

D.  15 years


Let his present age be x years

Then his age after 30 years will be 3x years

Given x + 30 = 3x

2x = 30

Present age = x = 30÷2

= 15 years.
