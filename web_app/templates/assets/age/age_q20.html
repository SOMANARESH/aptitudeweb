Harsha is 40 years old and ritu is 60 years old. How many years ago was the ratio of their ages 3:5?

A.  10 years

B.  20 years

C.  37 years

D.  5 years

Then the ratio of their ages before x years is

40-x⁄60-x = 3⁄5
