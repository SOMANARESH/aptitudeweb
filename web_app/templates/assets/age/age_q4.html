Question :4

Present ages of ram and sita are in the ratio 13:12 respectively. 8 yeasrs later, their ages will be in the ratio 15:14. What is sitas present age?

A.  50 years

B.  54 years

C.  48 years

D.  None of these

Answer: c

The Ratio between Present ages of ram and sita is 13:12

R:S = 13:12

=> S = 12×(R⁄13)---> 1

The Ratio between Present ages of ram and sita after 8 years becomes 15:14

=> (R+8):(S+8) = 15:14

=>14R+112 = 15S+120

Substitute 1 in above eq. then we will get

=> 14R+112

=>15×(12×R⁄13)+120

=>R = 52

=>S = 12×52⁄13

=48.
