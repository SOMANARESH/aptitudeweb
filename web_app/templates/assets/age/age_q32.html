Question :32

At the time of marriage, a man was 6 years older to his wife. 12 years after their marriage, his age is 6/5 times the age of his wife. What was wifes age at the time of marriage?

A.  18 years

B.  24 years

C.  30 years

D.  36 years


Let us assume that the age of a Man and his Wife at the time of marriage be M and W

Given that a man was 6 years older to his wife then

M=W+6

After 12 years the Mans age 6⁄5 times that of his Wife then

M+12 = (6⁄5 )×(W+12)

Substitute M in above eq. we get

W+6+12 = (6⁄5)×(W+12)

W+18 = (6⁄5)×(W+12)

5×(W+18) = 6×(W+12)

(5×W)+90 = (6×W)+72

W=18

Wifes age at the time of marriage is 18 years
