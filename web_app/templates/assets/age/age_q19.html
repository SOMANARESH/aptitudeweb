Question :19

Ratio between present ages of A and B is 3:4. What is the present age of A, if 4 years ago, the ratio was 5:7?

A.  20 years

B.  24 years

C.  28 years

D.  32 years


The Ratio between present ages of A and B is

A:B=3:4

=> B = 4×A⁄3--->1

The ratio between their ages before 4 years is 5:7

(A-4):(B-4) = 5:7

=> 7×(A-4) = 5×(B-4)

Substitute 1 in above eq. then we will get

=> (7×A)-28 = (5×(4×A⁄3))-20

7×A=(20×A⁄3)+8

By simplificatiuon we will get

=> A = 24

=> B = 4×A⁄3

=> B = 4×24⁄3

B = 32
