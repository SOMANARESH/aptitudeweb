Question :26

Sum of present age of A and B is 60 years. 5 years hence, their ages will be in the ratio 3:4. Find As present age.

A.  25 years

B.  30 years

C.  35 years

D.  40 years


Sum of present ages of A and B is 60

A+B = 60

B=60-A---> 1

After 5 years,the ratio of ages of A and B will be 3:4

(A+5)⁄(B+5) = 3⁄4

4×(A+5)=3×(B+5)

4×A+20=3×B+15

Substitute 1 in above equation

then
4×A=3×(60-A)-5

4×A=180-(3×A)-5

7×A=175

A= 175⁄7

A=25
