Question :3

The present ages of A and B are in the ratio 4:5 and after 5 years, their ages will be in the ratio 5:6. The present age of A is:

A.  15 years

B.  20 years

C.  25 years

D.  30 years


The Ratio between present ages of A and B is 4:5

=>A⁄B = 4⁄5

=>B=5×A⁄4--->1

The Ratio between ages of A and B after 5 years is

=>(A+5):(B+5)=5:6

=>6A+30=5B+25

Substitute 1 in above eq. then we will get

6A+30=5×(5×A⁄4)+25

=>A=20

=>B=5×20⁄4 = 25.
