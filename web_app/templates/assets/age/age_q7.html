Question :7

The ratio between present ages of A and B is 7:5. The ratio between As age 5 years ago and Bs age 5 years hence is 1:1. What is the ratio of age of A after 5 years and that of B 5 years ago?

A.  4:3

B.  2:1

C.  1:2

D.  3:4

Answer: b

The Ratio between present ages of A and B is

A:B=7:5

=> B = 5×A⁄7---> 1

The ratio between As age 5 years ago and Bs age 5 years hence is 1:1

(A-5):(B+5) = 1:1

=> A-5 = B+5

Substitute 1 in above eq. then we will get

=> A-5 = (5×A⁄7)+5

By simplificatiuon we will get

=> A = 35

=> B = 5×A⁄7

=> B = 5×35⁄7

B = 25.

Finally the ratio of age of A after 5 years and that of B 5 years ago is

=> (A+5)⁄(B-5)

= 35+5⁄25-5

=> 2:1.
