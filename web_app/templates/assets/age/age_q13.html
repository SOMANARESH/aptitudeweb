Question :13

3 years ago, the age of father was 7 times of his sons age. If the fathers present age is 5 times his sons age. Find the present age of the father?

A.  35 years

B.  39 years

C.  42 years

D.  45 years


Given That The present age of Father is 5 times the present age of his son

F = 5×S--->1

And also The age of father is 7 times the age of his son before 3 years

=> (F-3) = 7×(S-3)

Substitute 1 in above equation we get

(5×S)-3 = (7×S)-21

=> 2×S = 18

then S = 9

Finally Fathers age is

F = 5×S

= 5×9

= 45
