Question :9

If ratio of mohans age to that of his father is 1:5. After 10 years, their ages will be in the ratio 1:3. What is the fathers present age?

A.  25 years

B.  30 years

C.  40 years

D.  50 years

Answer: d

The ratio of mohans age to that of his father is 1:5

=> M⁄F = 1⁄5

Then F = 5×M---> 1

The ratio of their ages After 10 years is 1:3

=> (M+10)⁄(F+10) = 1⁄3

Substitute 1 in the above eq. we will get

(M+10)⁄(5M+10) = 1⁄3

By Calculations we will get

M = 10

=> F = 5×M

= 5×10 = 50.
