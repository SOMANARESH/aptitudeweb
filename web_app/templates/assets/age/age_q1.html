The present age of A, B and C are in the ratio 8:14:22 respectively. The present ages of B,C and D are in the ratio 21:33:44 respectively. Which of the following represents the ratio of the present ages of A,B,C and D respectively?

A.  12:21:33:44

B.  12:22:31:44

C.  12:21:36:44

D.  None of these


A:B:C = 8:14:22

=>(A⁄8)=(B⁄14)=(C⁄21)

By comparing fraction we will get

B=14(A⁄8)

Similarly C = 22(A⁄8)

And Also Given that

B:C:D = 21:33:44

=>(B⁄21) = (C⁄33) =(D⁄44)

By comparing fraction we will get

D = 44(B⁄21) or 44(C⁄33)

By substituting B or C value in D we will get
D = 11(A⁄3)

Then A:B:C:D =

A : 14(A⁄8) : 22(A⁄8) : 11(A⁄3)

=>1 : 14⁄8 : 22⁄8 : 11⁄3

After calculations We will get a result of

A:B:C:D=12:21:33:44.
