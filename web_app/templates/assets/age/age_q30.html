Question :30

The ages of A and B are in the ratio 8:5. If the sum of their ages is 39 years. What will be the ratio of their ages after 9 years?

A.  3:2

B.  8:7

C.  10:7

D.  11:8

Answer:d

Given that the ratio of ages of A and B is 8:5

A⁄B = 8⁄5

Then A=8×(B⁄5)--- > 1

And also given that the sum of their ages is 39

A+B = 39

A=39-B---> 2

Bysolving Eq.s 1 and 2 we get

39-B=8×(B⁄5)

5×(39-B)=8×B

195-(5×B) = 8×B

13×B = 195

B=195⁄13

B=15

A = 8×(B⁄5)

A=8×15⁄5

A=24

Finally the ratio of their ages after 9 years is

(A+9)⁄(B+9) = (24+9)⁄(15+9)

(A+9)⁄(B+9) = 33⁄24

(A+9)⁄(B+9) = 11⁄8
