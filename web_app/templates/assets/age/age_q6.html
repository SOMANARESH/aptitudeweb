The ratio between the ages of A and B is 2:5. After 8 years, their ages will be in the ratio 1:2. What is the difference between their present ages?

A.  20 years

B.  22 years

C.  24 years

D.  25 years


The Ratio between present ages of A and B is

A:B = 2:5

=>B = 5×A⁄2---> 1

The Ratio between ages of A and B after 8 years is

(A+8):(B+8) = 1:2

=>2A+16 = B+8

Substitute 1 in above eq. then we will get

=>2A+16 = 5×A⁄2+8

By simplificatiuon we will get

=>A = 16

=>B = 5×A⁄2

=>B = 5×24⁄2

B = 40

The difference between the ages of A and B is 40-16

= 24.
