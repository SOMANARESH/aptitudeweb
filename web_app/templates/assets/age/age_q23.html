The sum of ages of suresh and his father is 78 years. 6 years hence, fathers age will be twice of sureshs age. Fnd present age of father.

A.  30 years

B.  52 years

C.  54 years

D.  60 years

Answer: c

Let the ages of Suresh and his Father be S and F

Given that the sum of their ages is 78

S+F=78

F=78-S

and also given that the age of father is double that of his son after 6 years

i.e. F+6=2×(S+6)

Substitute F in above equation then

78-S+6=(2×S)+12

84-12=3×S

3×S=72

S=24 Now the age of Father is

F=78-S

F=78-24

F=54
