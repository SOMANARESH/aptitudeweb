Question :8

The average age of a man and his son is 28 years. The ratio of their ages is 5:2 what will be the ratio of their ages after 8 years?

A.  7:4

B.  2:1

C.  3:1

D.  None of these


Given that The average age of a man and his son is 28 years

=> (M+S)⁄2 = 28--->1

The ratio of their present ages is 5:2

=> M⁄S = 5⁄2

=> M = 5×S⁄2

Substitute M into 1 we will get

(5×S⁄2)+S = 56

By calculations

S = 16

M = 5×S⁄2

= 5×16⁄2

M = 40.
