The sum of ages of sunil and his father is 46 years, 3 years back, fathers age was 4 times sunils age. Find present age of sunil.

A.  7 years

B.  8 years

C.  11 years

D.  12 years

Answer: c

Let us assume the ages of Sunil and his Father be S and F

Given that the Sum of ages of Sunil and his Father is 46

S+F=46

F=46-S---> 1

And also given that the fathers age is 4 times that of sunils age before 3 years

F-3=4×(S-3)

F=(4×S)-12+3

F=(4×S)-9

Substitute 1 in Above equation then

46-S=(4×S)-9

55=5×S

S=11

F=46-S

F=46-11

F=35
