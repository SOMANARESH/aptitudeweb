Sum of present ages of A,B and C is 72 years.if 4 years ago, their ages were in the ratio 1:2:3 find As present age.

A.  7 years

B.  10 years

C.  12 years

D.  14 years


Sum of present ages of A, B and C is 72 years.
Sum of their ages (4 years ago) = 72-3×4
= 60 years
4 years ago, ratio of ages of A, B and C was 1:2:3
As age, 4 years ago = (1÷6)×60
= 10 years.
As present age = 10+4
= 14 years.
