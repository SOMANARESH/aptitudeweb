Question :22

Mohan got married 9 years ago. Today his age is 1 1⁄3 times of his age at the time of his marriage. Find his present age?

A.  12 years

B.  27 years

C.  36 years

D.  45 years

Answer:c

Let the present age of Mohan be M

then his age at the time of marriage is (M-9) since he got married before 9 years

Given that present age is 11⁄3 times the age at the time of marriage

then M=4⁄3×(M-9)

3×M=4×(M-9)

3×M=(4×M)-36

M=36
