The difference between the ages of Arun and Deepak is 14 years. Seven years ago. The ratio of their ages was 5:7 respectively. What is Deepaks present age?

A.  49 years.

B.  42 years.

C.  63 years.

D.  None of these.


Given that Difference between Arun and Deepak ages is

modulus(A-D) = 14

And The ratio of Ages of Arun And Deepak before 7 years is

(A-7):(D-7) = 5:7

=>(A-7)⁄(D-7) = 5⁄7---> 1

There are two possibilities

1.A>D

2.D>A

Case1: A>D

A-D = 14

=> A = D+14

Substitute A in 1 then we will get

(14+D-7)⁄(D-7) = 5⁄7

=>2D = -84 => D = (-42)

It is not possible to be a minus value.

Now Case 2: D>A

D-A = 14

=> D = A+14

Substitute A in 1 then we will get

(A-7)⁄(A+14-7) = 5⁄7

=>2A = 84 => A = 42

then D = A+14

= 42+14

= 56
