Question :17

The ratio of present ages two brothers is 1:2 and 5 years back the ratio was 1:3. What will be the ratio of their ages after 5 years?

A.  1:4

B.  2:3

C.  3:5

D.  5:6


The Ratio between present ages of B1 and B2 is

B1:B2 = 1:2

=> B2 = 2×B1---> 1

The ratio between their ages before 5 years is 1:2

(B1-5):(B2-5) = 1:3

=> B2-5 = 3×(B1-5)

Substitute 1 in above eq. then we will get

=> 2×B1-5 = 3×(B1-5)

By simplificatiuon we will get

=> B1 = 10

=> B2 = 2×B1

=> B2 = 2×10

=> B2 = 20

Finally the ratio of Their ages after 5 years is

=>( B1+5):(B2+5)

= (10+5):(20+5)

= 15:25

=> 3:5
