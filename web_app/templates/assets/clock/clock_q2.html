Question 2 :

At what time between 2 and 3 O’ Clock will the hands of a clock be together

A.(120⁄11)min Past 2

B.(140⁄11)min Past 2

C.(118⁄11)min Past 2

D.(122⁄11)min Past 2


Solution:

At 2 O’ Clock, Hour hand is at 2 and minute hand is at 12.

i.e, they are 10 minutes space apart

To be together,the minute hand must gain 10minutes over hour hand

We know that for 1 hour period, minute hand will gain 55 minutes over hour hand

So 55 minutes are gained by it in 60 minutes

In case of this scenario,10 minutes will be gained in 10×(60⁄55)

i.e,120⁄11
