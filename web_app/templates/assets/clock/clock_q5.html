Question 5 :

My watch gains 5 min every hour. How many degrees the second hand moves in every min?

A.330°

B.390°

C.360°

D.430°

Answer: B

Solution:

We know that for every minutes second hand will cover 360°

Given that watch gains 5 mins for every hour.

So that means, for one hour it covers 65 minutes. i.e, 13 spaces.

We know that for each space second hand will cover 30°

So for 13 spaces, it will cover 13 × 30° = 390°
