Question 8 :

What is the angle between minute hand and hour hand when the time is at 6:30

A.15

B.20

C.25

D.30


Solution:

We know that, hour hand will cover 30° for every hour.So Angle by hour hand in 6 hours = 30°×6 = 180°

We also know that, for every minute hour hand will cover 0.5°

So for 30 minutes, hour hand will cover 15°

For 6 hours 30 minutes, hand hour will cover 180 + 15 = 195°

For 60 minutes, minute hand will cover 360°

So for 30 minutes, minute hand will cover 180°

So the angle between them = |180 - 195| = 15°
