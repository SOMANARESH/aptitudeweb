Question 7 :

At what time between 4 and 5 o’Clock will the hands of a clock be right angle

A.4 Hr (60⁄11)min

B.4 Hr (420⁄11)min

C.4 Hr 5 min

D.Both A and B

Answer: D

Solution:

We know that, hour hand will cover 30° for every hour.So Angle by hour hand in 4 hours = 30°×4 = 120°

We also know that, for every minute hour hand will cover 0.5°

In order to have right angle between hands of clock, the angle between hands of clock = 90°

We know that the Angle between X hours and Y minutes θ=|(X*30)-((Y*11)/2)| ‐‐‐> (1)

In this case, we have two cases.

Case 1:

    When minutes hand is behind hour hand

    Here θ=90° X = 4

    Then from above equation (1),

    90° = 4×30° - ((Y*11)/2)

    ((Y*11)/2) = 120° - 90°

    Y×11 = 30×2 = 60

    Y = (60⁄11)min

    So hands of a clock will be right angle at 4 Hr (60⁄11)min

Case 2:

    When hours hand is behind minutes hand

    Then from above equation (1)

    90° = ((Y*11)/2) - 4×30°

    ((Y*11)/2) = 90° + 120°

    Y ×11 = 210×2 = 420

    Y = (420⁄11)min

    So hands of a clock will be right angle at 4 Hr (420⁄11)min
