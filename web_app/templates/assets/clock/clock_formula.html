Clock

  • The angle in degrees of the hand measured clockwise from the 12, θ=0.5° × (60 × H + M) where H = hour, M = minutes past the hour

  • The Angle in degree of the minute measured clockwise from the 12, θ = 6° × M where M = minutes

  • Angle between X hours and Y minutes θ=|(X*30)-((Y*11)/2)|
