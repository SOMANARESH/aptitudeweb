Question 4 :

A clock is started at noon. By 20 minutes pass 4, the hour hand turned through

A.90°

B.130°

C.155°

D.120°


Solution:

Given that Clock started at noon means at 12 O’clock PM.

We know that, hour hand will cover 30° for every hour.So Angle by hour hand from 12 to 4 = 4 times of Angle cover by Hour hand per hour

So total Angle covered for 4 hours = 4 × 30° = 120°

We also know that, for every minute hour hand will cover 0.5°

So for 20 minutes, hour hand will cover 20 ×0.5° = 10°

So total angle covered from 12 to 4 hour 20 minutes = 120°+10° = 130°
