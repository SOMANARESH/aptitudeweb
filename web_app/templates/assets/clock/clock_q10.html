Question 10 :

How much does a watch lose per day, if its hands coincide ever 64 minutes?

A.358⁄11

B.338⁄11

C.348⁄11

D.328⁄11


Solution:

We know that,in 1 hour(60 minutes), minute hand gains 55minutes than the hour hand

So 55 min spaces are covered in 60 min to get coincide each other

1 min space can be covered in 60⁄55

60 min spaces are covered in (60⁄55)×60

60 min spaces are covered = 3600⁄55 = 720⁄11

Loss in 64 min = 720⁄11 −64

Loss in 64 min = (720-704)⁄11 = 16⁄11

For 1 min, the loss in minutes = (1⁄64)×(16⁄11)

Loss in 24 hours = (1⁄64)×(16⁄11) ×24 ×60 min

Loss in 24 hours = (1⁄4)×(1⁄11) ×24 ×60 min

Loss in 24 hours = (1⁄11) ×6 ×60 min

Loss in 24 hours = 360⁄11

Loss in 24 hours = 328⁄11
