Question 9 :

At what time between 9 and 10 O’clock will the hands of a watch be right angle to each other?

A..9 and 358⁄11 past 9

B.9 and 348⁄11 past 9

C.9 and 328⁄11 past 9

D.9 and 338⁄11 past 9

Answer: C

Solution:

Case: 1

    At 9 O’ Clock, Hour hand is at 9 and minute hand is at 12.

    i.e, they are 3 hours space apart

    We know that, there will be 30° angle between successive hours. Total difference = 3 × 30° = 90°

    To be together,the minute hand must gain 30 minutes over hour hand

    We know that for 1 hour period, minute hand will gain 55 minutes over hour hand

    So 55 minutes are gained by hour hand for 60 minutes

    In case of this scenario,30 minutes will be gained in 30×(60⁄55)

    i.e,360⁄11

    32 8⁄11

    So Both hands of clock between 9 O’ Clock and 10 O’ Clock will be right angle to each other at 9 hours 32 8⁄11 Mins

Case: 2

    We know that, hour hand will cover 30° for every hour.So Angle by hour hand in 9 hours = 30°×9 = 270°

    We also know that, for every minute hour hand will cover 0.5°

    Let us assume that both hands will make an angle of 90° after X minutes from 9 O’Clock

    So for X minutes, hours hand will cover = 0.5×X°

    Total angle covered by hour hand = 270°+(0.5×X°)

    We know that, minute hand will cover 6° for every minute

    So for X minutes, minute hand will cover = 6×X°

    As per the condition, the angle between these hands = 90°

    So 270°+(0.5×X°) − 6×X° = 90°

    270° − 5.5×X° = 90°

    5.5×X° = 180°

    X = 180° ⁄ 5.5°

    X = 1800 ⁄ 55

    X = 360 ⁄ 11

    X = 32 8⁄11
